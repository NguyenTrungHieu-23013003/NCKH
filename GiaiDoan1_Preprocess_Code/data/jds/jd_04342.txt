Department The Client Intelligence Unit (CIU), part of BCIU, is responsible globally for the Correspondent Banking Enhanced Due Diligence (CB EDD) program, FinTech Review program and Global Corporate Review program. AFC’s Mission is: To be a trusted and respected independent control function that aims to protect the Bank from financial crime risk To establish a proactive framework to prevent, detect, investigate and report bank-wide financial crime risk events Overview The CIU undertakes research of open sources and internal information, conducts onsite visits, and reviews clients’ financial crime compliance programs, in order to assess the regulatory risks clients pose to Deutsche Bank. There is currently a need for an analyst in the CIU to undertake research, participate during onsite visits, and assist with producing post-visit assessment reports, in connection with the CB EDD and FinTech programs. Key Responsibilities: You will be required to: Support the Regional Head of BCIU in Americas to run the relevant CIU programs Be responsible for client selection methodology, making sure that supporting data is obtained and submitted into relevant databases Liaise with different areas of AFC to obtain updated data on a frequent basis Undertake desktop and open source research on clients, including transactional analysis Attend onsite visits with CIU, Business Line AFC and business colleagues Assist with writing post-visit reports Assist in tracking CIU caseloads Develop and maintain internal and external stakeholder relationships Recognizing the need to drive the long-term success of the program, and the current size of the team, you will need to possess a ‘can do attitude’, and have exemplary communication skills You will have: Experience with financial crime compliance programs and enhanced due diligence reviews, preferably within a banking environment A good understanding of correspondent banking and/or FinTechs The ability to communicate clearly both verbally and written You will be: Of graduate caliber, with relevant financial crime compliance experience A team player and self-starter Able to act on own initiative and to learn quickly Able to handle and manage a number of different matters simultaneously A strong communicator Ideally experienced at dealing with clients For candidates applying for positions in the US: Deutsche Bank is an Equal Opportunity Employer - Veterans/Disabled and other protected categories.
