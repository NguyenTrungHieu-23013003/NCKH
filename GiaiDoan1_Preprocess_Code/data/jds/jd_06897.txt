Job Description Plus is looking for a Global Mobility Specialist to join our Client Services team. Someone with 1 or more year of experience working in customer service. An ideal candidate will possess skills in their attention to detail, communication, organization, and working in a fast-paced environment. This role will be working under the direction of our Client Services Support Manager. If you are an experienced, passionate, and driven customer service professional, then we want to speak to you! Global Mobility Specialist Overview: You will have the responsibility of assisting Global Mobility Counselors, Plus partners, and the overall Service Delivery team while effectively managing relocation files. What your day as a Global Mobility Specialist looks like: Assisting assigned Global Mobility Counselors with file management supporting the relocating employees and corporate clients, as an extension of the Counselor Generating and reviewing reports and communication pieces for corporate clients and relocating employees Coordinating and managing supplier services as directed Researching requests and resolve issues for relocating employees and corporate clients Preparing and reviewing relocation and assignment cost estimates Tracking progression of service delivery information and communicate to Counselor
