Overview The Business Analyst is responsible for ensuring the company’s operational efficiencies, and identifying areas for improvement with the operations’ organizations. The Business Analyst will also design and document daily, weekly, monthly reports and helps to identify trends using data visualization and statistical modelling. Responsibilities Defines metrics, automates data collection, synthesizes relevant data, and builds analytical models and forecasts. Evaluates the data collected through task analysis, business process, surveys and workshops. Effectively communicates with external clients and internal teams to deliver reports in a timely fashion. Provides summary documentation on the acquired results of the analysis. Works with internal teams to automate or streamline data collection processes. Manages any change requests related to data or data collection. Assists the internal managed services team for new program on-boarding. Performs data extraction from MYSQL, Hive, Oracle and relates the data via the appropriate Data Visualization methods. Leverages tools, such as SQL, Tableau, Oracle, and Excel to drive efficient analytics. Qualifications Advance skills in MS Word, Outlook, and Excel. Solid mathematical aptitude, and the ability to gather and interpret relevant data and information. Effective project management skills. Ability to analyze and document complex business processes. Experience with PL/SQL, Tableau and any other related programs useful for executing the above responsibilities. Solid understanding of data visualization principles. Solid understanding of finance-related processes. Service industry background is a plus. Agile and exhibits a strong sense of urgency. Exceptional professionalism and customer-service skills. Excellent task management, verbal & written communication skills, combined with relentless follow-up.
