The Design Applications Technician position is a key role in Perkins+Will’s Digital Practice,focusing on our design process and practice through the comprehensive integration of digital tools & workflows.The Design Applications Technician will be expected to balance efforts between assisting project teams and providing technology support and training. The position requires expertise in Perkins+Will’s design applications, a solid foundation in all phases of architectural practice, and the ability to manage multiple projects. The ideal candidate will be dynamic, self-motivated, and collaborative. Capable of engaging effectively with project teams, office leadership, and Perkins+Will’s firm-wide Digital Practice group. Responsibilities: Collaborate with Design Teams to develop project-specific implementation strategies. Act as ongoing project consultant to ensure success. Ensure Quality Assurance with project teams to maintain modeling quality & fidelity, adherence to firm-wide modeling and information workflows, and ensure continuity of information across applications. Represent and coordinate with the firm-wide Digital Practice group to promote local adoption of new technologies, including Virtual & Augmented Reality, Computational Design, Digital Fabrication, Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence & Internet of Things, workflows and practices. Coordination of applications support with our IT infrastructure group and firm-wide helpdesk. Provide Digital Design support to end users. Communicate exemplar processes, procedures, practices, and workflows. Conduct continuing education workshops and assist with curriculum development. Participate in office & firm-wide planning,relative to design technology innovation & implementation. Proactively maintain and communicate current knowledge relative to technology through journals, vendor resources, and design community.
