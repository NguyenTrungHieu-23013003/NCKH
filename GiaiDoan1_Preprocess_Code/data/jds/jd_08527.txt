The mission of the Cloud Developer Advocacy team is to win the hearts and minds of Developers and IT Operators to secure the future of the platform. The Cloud Developer Advocacy team is first and foremost focused on enabling a healthy ecosystem for technical communities targeting Azure. Each team maps to a set of Azure services and open source technologies relevant to that technical audience. As the Cloud Developer Advocate Program Manager, you’ll be responsible for growing Microsoft’s influence and contributions to the San Francisco developer community. You’ll work with our Cloud Developer Advocates in San Francisco to identify the top influencers and communities applicable to our business, build deeper and mutually respectful relationships with those influencers, grow our contributions to those communities, and stay abreast of major trends in developer communities. You’ll understand the local developer event landscape, work with Cloud Developer Advocates to identify local events of importance that we should sponsor and/or speak at, and work to ensure a diverse and inclusive experience that will make Microsoft and developers proud. You’ll need to be analytical and measure the efficacy of our efforts, constantly tweaking and improving our processes. You’re a social media rock star and understand how people influence one another today, eager to experiment with changing consumer consumption patterns, and obsess over constantly measuring and improving our community outreach on social media and via in-person events.
