Supply Chain Integration Team @ Wayfair Supplier Integration Associate Overview of Supply Chain Integration The Supply Chain Integration (SCI) team at Wayfair is the team responsible for building and maintaining all technical integrations between Wayfair and our external business partners using our growing EDI and API platforms. It is focused on strengthening and scaling our technical systems used by our growing supplier base and always pushing to be on the forefront of tech operations in e-commerce. Supply Chain Integration is often the bridge between the technical and non-technical worlds, which requires the team to collaborate cross functionally with many departments and external stakeholders, balancing between meeting technical and business priorities of Wayfair, suppliers, and third party vendors. Ideal candidates for this growing team will value collaboration, creative ideation, and using data to solve complex challenges in supply chain management operations. We are looking for team members who, when presented with a problem, demonstrate systems thinking and practical on-the-ground tactical thinking. Candidates will work with internal teams and suppliers to uncover data trends, identify root causes, and develop and implement innovative solutions. Those under consideration will ideally be passionate about leveraging technology to productively disrupt the supply chain management industry and improve operational efficiency and performance. Team- oriented individuals with strong communication skills will be viewed as ideal candidates during in the hiring process.
