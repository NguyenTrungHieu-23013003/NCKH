Our Baylor Scott & White Health's (BSWH) Business Analyst manages activities to support systems, applications, and reporting that are essential to the core business processes for the Health Plan. The ideal Business Analyst candidate has excellent analytical and problem solving skills and the ability to make effective business decisions.
