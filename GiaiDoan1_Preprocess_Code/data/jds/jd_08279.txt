The Manager of Centralized Records reports to the Director of Quality Assurance/Chief Inspector and is responsible for the day to day operation of the Centralized Records Department. Essential Functions (Include percentage of time after each function): 1) Responsible for the overall document security in the Centralized Records Department. 2) Responsible for the daily administration of the Centralized Records Department. 3) Performs other duties as assigned by the Director of Quality Assurance/Chief Inspector. 4) Has the authority and responsible for facilitating hazard identification, safety risk analysis and monitoring the effectiveness of safety risk controls within their assigned departments. 1) Holds a Mechanic's Certificate with an Airframe and Powerplant rating or general knowledge of aircraft technical records and Inspection programs. 2) Have knowledge of the Code of Federal Regulations Specifically Title 14 section 121. 3) Have at least one-year experience in a supervisory role in the aviation industry.
