The Partner Operations Team is dedicated to operational partner management of our key payment partners, processors, and service providers. This team’s responsibilities include relationship management, investigating, triaging and solving payment processing issues, testing new payment methods and partner integrations, and influencing internal stakeholders to help drive the business forward. Success in this role will require technical knowledge, strong analytical/critical thinking skills, business acumen and interpersonal ability to manage various partner relationships. RESPONSIBILITIES Investigate reported escalations across multiple channels, while providing timely and accurate responses to our partners Develop and implement scalable solutions to support our global partners Develop and maintain standard internal project and process documentation Drive efficiency, productivity and customer-experience improvements MINIMUM QUALIFICATIONS BA/BS degree 2+ years of experience in an online operations, project management or online escalations environment Foundational understanding of core payment processing and risk systems Ability to drive high priority cases through process of resolution PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS Ability to use and learn tools like SQL and Excel to drive analytics and reporting Ability to assess, analyze and resolve complicated issues, and distill that complexity into simple and concise concepts Experience problem solving in a collaborative, fast-paced environment
