Mission Genesys Works is a nonprofit organization that trains and employs high school seniors to perform professional services for major corporations. Our mission is to provide pathways to career success for high school students in underserved communities through skills training, meaningful work experience, and impactful relationships. Purpose As a Program Manager, specializing in the management of Genesys Works Chicago's internship programming, primary responsibilities will include managing a team of Program Coordinators, as well as assisting the Director of Corporate Partnerships in the successful management of all initiatives and matters involving students and their internships. Key Responsibilities People Management Serve as manager to team of 3-6 direct reports Assist in recruiting, interviewing, and training employees as needed Maintain a safe, secure, and legal work environment; developing staff by communicating job expectations and policies, planning, monitoring, and appraising job results, and coaching, counseling, and disciplining employees Work with leadership and management team to design and implement staff development initiatives Project Management Act as project manager to team of 6-12 Program Coordinators Lead planning and implementation of student evaluation, selection, and placement strategies during summer training Lead on-boarding of corporate partners, hiring, and deployment of interns Conduct workplace check-in meetings with Program Coordinators and act as first-level support for student/workplace issues Ensure quality programming is being delivered by managing workplace initiatives and training, supporting, and reporting on staff performance Fill role of acting supervisor for students whose Program Coordinators are unavailable Provide timely reports to leadership regarding status of workplace projects, initiatives, student performance, and staff performance Represent Genesys Works Chicago on national initiatives as needed Ensure data quality of related data in GWC systems (i.e. Salesforce, Box, TSheets, Google Apps, etc.) Qualifications Bachelor's degree required Experience managing projects, as well as managing people Ability to provide high quality customer service to internal and external partners Strong organizational skills and a talent for creating efficiencies and refining processes Ability to take initiative, and to manage multiple tasks and projects at a time Possesses strong systems knowledge and demonstrates great attention to detail with sensitive information A strong sense of accountability and the ability to produce results independently and as part of a team Excellent verbal and written communication skills Ability to relate to and work with diverse populations Flexible and adaptable style; a leader who can positively impact both strategic and tactical business development initiatives Other Info Physical Requirements Including, but not limited to standing and sitting for long periods of time; speaking loudly and clearly; seeing and hearing things both near and far away; and reaching, stooping, kneeling, and fine-finger and hand manipulation in use of a computer, chalkboard, dry erase board, or projector.
