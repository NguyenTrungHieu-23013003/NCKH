Under direct supervision, schedules patients for surgery. Duties include but are not limited to: preparing pre-operative forms, scheduling pre-operative exam(s), scheduling surgery (inpatient & outpatient) admissions, preparing various statistical reports, & coordinates the scheduling of courtesy surgeons. Essential Functions: Upholds KP's Policies & Procedures, Principles of Responsibilities & applicable state, federal & local laws Processes and/or schedules all surgery scheduling requests Coordinates & schedules all pre-operative appointments Collects surgical service statistics Monitors surgical service block utilization Interacts w/ & assists patients & family members, in person & telephonically in answering questions & in scheduling surgeries & ancillary activities, including but not limited to preoperative exams, tests & completion of required forms Assume other activities & responsibilities from time to time as directed
