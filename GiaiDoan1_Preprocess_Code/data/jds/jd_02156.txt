Job Description Review, negotiate, draft and manage NDAs, Software and Technology Evaluation Agreements, SOWs, consulting agreements and other documents for Bosch business units in coordination with assigned attorneys. Manage templates for, and a database of, IoT agreements and contracts, such as Terms of Use, Terms of Service, Cloud Services and SaaS. Interface with Bosch’s customers and vendors to provide input and recommendation on contracts. Draft and modify contract templates to suit business unit and customer needs and ensure proposed changes receive appropriate review and approval, including compliance with Bosch policies and guidelines. Assist local Bosch business units with communications between such business units and attorneys within Bosch’s legal department, both in the US and internationally. Comfortably work cross-functionally across all levels in the organization including, with upper management. Assist with the creation of standard operating procedures to build on existing contract templates and experiences. Administer procedures to ensure consistent use of best practices. Actively participate in comprehensive development of plans including an outline of duties and project assignments. Perform routine legal research and compiling data, charts, legal research and reports as required. Assist with the coordination of local events. Support proprietary software license management initiatives in coordination with Central Purchasing and License, Software and Development expert group. May include travel to Bosch Domestic and International locations.
