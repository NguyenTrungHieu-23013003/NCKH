Duties: Under the supervision of the Library Media Specialist and Technology Integration Specialist, the Library Support Aide will: Staff busy time blocks at the circulation desk or be used for class coverage when there is a conflict in the schedule, process new materials, shelve books and other library materials, use library automation system to do copy- cataloging, assist students and staff in locating resources and using the library, do special tasks as assigned by Library Media specialist and/or Technology Integration Specialist to support teachers and students on projects. Qualifications: Associates degree, or 48 college credits. BA degree preferred, high level of organizational and collaboration skills, familiarity with automated circulation; experience working with JK-5 school children. Experience with Apple and PC platforms. Salary: In Accordance with the Less than Six Hour Aide Salary Schedule.
