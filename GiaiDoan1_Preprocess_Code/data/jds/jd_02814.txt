The Manager of Marketing Analytics will be responsible for helping to drive traffic and retail performance by reviewing data, developing new analytics, looking for insights and helping to deliver recommendations on how to continually improve our marketing campaigns. The Manager of Marketing Analytics will be part of the senior-most marketing discussions within the organization, will have exposure to executive management and will influence our marketing efforts. Essential Duties and Responsibilities Review and analyze all marketing efforts including performance by individual ad, media, store/market and campaign to identify successful elements and work to continuously improve ad performance Develop actionable insights and help formulate strategic direction of all marketing efforts Identify customer trends and recommend data-driven responses to help guide strategy and decision making within the Marketing organization Assist in the creation of compelling, persuasive presentation materials to communicate key learnings and recommendations to senior level executives clearly and concisely Support Media Managers with reporting, analytical insights and make strategic recommendations related to the media performance and marketing support for each store/market, ad, and campaign Assist in the management of dashboards and analytics that communicate status and evaluate effectiveness of all ads, promotions, offers and campaigns Manage, track and analyze all marketing related test campaigns and initiatives Assist in the design and execution of key test which will drive the development of insights Assist with the execution of ad hoc strategic marketing projects as assigned Build, develop and grow business relationships across the organization vital to the success of our projects Additional duties as assigned by manager Scope Organizational Scope – All US locations Decision Making – Creates policy and resolves problems Travel – as neededThe Manager of Marketing Analytics will be responsible for helping to drive traffic and retail performance by reviewing data, developing new analytics, looking for insights and helping to deliver recommendations on how to continually improve our marketing campaigns.
