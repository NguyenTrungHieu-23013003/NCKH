Business Analyst, Strategy – Digital Consumer Group Background The media industry is going through a period of immense consumer driven change as OTT and digital access to TV increases. The Fox Digital Consumer Group (DCG) is a new strategic business unit in Fox’s TV group (FNG). The group’s focus is developing TV & video-centric products bringing Fox’s content to more consumers in new and exciting ways. First, it will be bringing together & furthering the innovation of Fox’s existing TV Everywhere products including FX Now, Fox Now, the Nat Geo TV app and Fox Sports Go. Next, it will be working to develop & bring to market direct to consumer propositions. Within, the digital consumer group the Strategy, Commercial & Marketing team will be responsible for defining the overall strategy, developing the propositions and business cases, working with the product teams to bring products to market and then delivering sales and performance targets. Role Description The Strategist will be responsible for organizing content rights management, video content editorialization and evaluating & recommending strategies for content utilization, acquisition and partnerships.
