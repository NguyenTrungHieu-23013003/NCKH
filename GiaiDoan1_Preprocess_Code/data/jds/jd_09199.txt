Security Analyst 1 - 350467 An Analyst 1 is a part of a key team of Technology professionals that helps Nordstrom grow by improving technology and process and ensuring effective use of applications and systems. Through research and interaction, this position assesses priorities of internal and external customers. The Analyst 1 will collect relevant information and data about new or existing solutions then analyze, review, document, and communicate business needs, requirements, and impacts on work processes. A Security Analyst 1 will apply best security practices in delivering effective and safe solutions quickly. Understanding complex networking environments is vital to performing as a Security Analyst 1.
