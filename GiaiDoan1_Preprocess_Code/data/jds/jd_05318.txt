Under the direction of the Drug Use Management Leader, develops and implements drug cost and quality initiatives for the Division and their assigned Region. Provides functional direction and leadership to Service Area Clinical Operations Managers (COMs), leads Drug Education Coordinator (DEC) work teams to accomplish drug cost and quality initiatives. Coordinates activities with PMG Chiefs of Service, AAMDs/PICs and PMG Regional Leadership. Directs and manages personnel in Drug Information/Professional Services to meet needs of Service Area customers for the production of data, evidence and promotional material, and ambulatory care support. Active leadership in designing drug cost and quality initiatives Program-wide through the Interregional Clinical Pharmacy Services Subcommittee. Essential Responsibilities: Leads and manages designated Regional Drug Information/Professional Services staff (both union and non-union exempt) for production of data, evidence and promotional material to support Area and Regional cost and quality initiatives. Leads DEC groups in Region in developing, implementing and evaluating drug cost and quality initiatives. Integrates Regional and Area personnel into the drug use management process (e.g., guidelines development, formulary process, physician education). Coordinates allocation of resources from other Regional departments (e.g., Centralized Services) to implement drug cost and quality inititiaves. Leads development and implementation of Interregional cost and quality initiatives through the Interregional Clinical Pharmacy Services Subcommittee. Kaiser Permanente conducts compensation reviews of positions on a routine basis. At any time, Kaiser Permanente reserves the right to reevaluate and change job descriptions, or to change such positions from salaried to hourly pay status. Such changes are generally implemented only after notice is given to affected employees.
