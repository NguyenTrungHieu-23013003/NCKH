The Business Solutions Architect (BSA) has the primary responsibility to provide and optimize the delivery of information capabilities to the business. The goal is to realize the integrated business and technology strategy, and ensure that individual business units are receiving the necessary information to perform their functions. BSA will lead a team focused on development activities, application interface designs, integration approaches, and data analytics. This role has accountability for several Business Platforms solutions including support, upgrades, data governance, data integrity, and the associated documentation required to maintain these solutions in their validated state. The position relies on extensive experience and judgment to handle conflicting requirements and accomplish goals. A wide degree of creativity and latitude is expected.
