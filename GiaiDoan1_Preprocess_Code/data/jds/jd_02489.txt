The Business Systems Analyst is accountable for delivering high quality, comprehensive systems designs, business requirements and business cases that will help to improve business efficiency and productivity once implemented. The BSA will also be responsible for helping drive projects through testing, training and full implementation, including ongoing enhancements and support for core functionality. Duties and Responsibilities Perform business systems analysis, solutions implementation and ongoing support Act as contact and liaison to business systems users, stakeholders and IT Collaborates with functional owners and Business Technology management to understand business needs and implement applications using best practices Facilitate requirements gathering and analysis of customer software solution needs then design and/or recommend solutions Apply governance policies and procedures Coordinate deployment activities with infrastructure, vendor and development teams Perform Quality Assurance Testing on IT solutions Support business users during Business User Acceptance Testing phase Coordinate the development of training guides for end-users and training sessions if needed Provide any necessary day to day support, system troubleshooting, validation of data Develop and maintain reports, interfaces and workflows for ERP and related systems as needed Proactively seek solutions that offer improvements in efficiency, stability and scalability
