Join the Non-GMO Project as the Program Integration Manager and help shape the future of North America’s most recognized and rigorous non-GMO product verification program. In this position, you will manage various projects to ensure the effective and integrated delivery of the Product Verification Program through stakeholder engagement, program improvement projects, policy & guideline development, and Technical Administrator oversight. This is an exciting opportunity for a seasoned project or program manager who thrives in collaborating with internal and external stakeholders to solve complex challenges through analysis, strategies, and policies. This role is perfect for you if you are a creative, life-long learner looking to build on your experience leading work in a mission-aligned organization. You enjoy working with a range of stakeholders to understand complex problems and opportunities and have a unique ability to facilitate and execute on effective solutions across a wide range of disciplines. You have experience managing external vendor relationships and contracts; gathering, analyzing and reporting on key business metrics to decision makers; and developing program policies and guidelines. The Program Integration Manager must be conversant in both the technical and non-technical aspect of the Product Verification Program and be able to diplomatically facilitate systems-level changes.
