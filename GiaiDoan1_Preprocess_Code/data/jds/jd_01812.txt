The Data Analytic Consultant provides support in making strategic data-related decisions by analyzing, manipulating, tracking, internally managing and reporting data. These positions function both as a consultant and as a high- level data analyst. The position works with clients to develop the right set of questions/hypotheses, using the appropriate tools/skills to derive the best information to address customer needs. Essential Responsibilities: Assists in issue identification and design of analytic approaches. Employs specified analytical methods and tools to access data to provide reportable information from KP information systems. Provides support and point of contact for department clients requesting routine information, data summaries and or reports. Applies specified analytical methods including assessing the significance of observed changes, identifying associations among measured quantities, forecasting trends, etc. Contributes to written reports and presentation materials describing project or task objectives, methods, data, and results. Implements specified QA procedures to ensure accuracy of project data, results and written reports & presentation materials.
