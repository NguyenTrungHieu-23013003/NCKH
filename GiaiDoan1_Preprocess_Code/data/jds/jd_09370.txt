Job Description Responsibility:  Monitor and analyze security event data  Coordinate with Business Units to develop Information Security processes  Identify Business Units regulatory and compliance needs.  Track and report on the effectiveness of information security technology, processes and polices  Respond to and track Security Incidents  Perform Risk Assessments  Develop Information Security Awareness programs Competencies, Knowledge, Skills and Abilities Ability to:  Understand Network Security Architecture  Develop and maintain Information Security Policies and Processes  Recognize and describe Information Security Technology  Solve practical problems and in situations where only limited standardization exists  Effectively communicate with both Business Unit leaders and technical professionals  Understand regulatory requirements and their application to a variety of business units  Stay informed of Industry trends and emerging threat landscape. Knowledge of:  Information Security principals and best practices  Working knowledge of Information Security Technology: Firewalls, IDS/IPS, SEIM, etc  Familiarity with Information Security Regulations: HIPAA HITECH, CJIS, PCI etc  Risk assessments and audits  Industry trends Qualifications  5 years’ experience in an Analytical IT or Information Security Role  Completion of a Bachelor’s Degree program at an accredited college or university, which has included major course work in computer science, information science or information security.  Relevant Industry Certifications required Additional Information Successful candidate must be a City resident within six months of hire Please submit a resume and cover letter with your application.
