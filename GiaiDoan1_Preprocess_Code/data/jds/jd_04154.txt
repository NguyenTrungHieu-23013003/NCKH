An effective IC will ensure that our school?s instructional program and school environment prepares every student for success in life. The IC is an active member of the school leadership team and reports to the Principal. PERFORMANCE EXPECTATIONS The Instructional Coach is responsible for managing, coaching, developing, and supporting instructional staff in accordance with the mission, vision, philosophy and policies of EAS Schools.
