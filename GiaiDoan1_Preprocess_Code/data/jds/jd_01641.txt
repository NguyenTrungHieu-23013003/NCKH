This position is a somewhat physically demanding job that requires attention to detail while working at a fast pace. Reporting to the Supervisor of HIM, this position is responsible for correctly interpreting federal, state, and hospital policy for release of information, data entry and initiating follow up phone calls. Must be able to assist patients, physicians, attorneys and all visitors/callers to the department in a pleasant helpful fashion. Teamwork is important and the ability to work under pressure in a very busy reception area is critical. Prepares all documents for scanning. Scans all pre-identified loose documents into the EMR. Performs quality and validation checks of the scanned documents. Retrieves discharged paper charts and all loose documents from various locations throughout the hospital. Keep productivity statistics and turnaround statistics as required to meet standards. Answers the telephone, responds to and assists callers and customers. Serves as the department receptionist as needed and is able to interpret Mercy Hospital’s policy on confidentiality of patient information. Assists in sorting and/or copying medical documents. Answers request for medical information in accordance with state laws and hospital policy.
