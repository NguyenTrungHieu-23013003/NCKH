Summary The Marketing, Finance & Strategic Planning team is part of the Filmed Entertainment, IT Division. With a growing suite of Essbase and SAP based FP&A; applications, the Film Finance portfolio is experiencing continued investment and growth, and is home to some of the best in class studio accounting information systems. The Senior Business Analyst will be responsible for supporting the solution delivery lifecycle for the suite of FP&A; applications under the direction of the Film Finance Portfolio Manager. This includes participating in change management activities to support the successful business adoption of new IT solutions. This role requires a strong understanding of film finance/accounting based principles and proven experience supporting financial based EPM applications. The Senior Business Analyst will leverage established NBCU processes, methodologies and associated practices, while carrying out his/her responsibilities.
