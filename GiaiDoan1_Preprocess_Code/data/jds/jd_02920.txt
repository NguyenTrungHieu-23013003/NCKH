Job Summary and Mission This job contributes to Starbucks success by providing strategic insights and analytics to drive revenue and margin optimization across the US Retail business. In this broad and dynamic role, you will be responsible for designing and measuring experiments to test and learn how our most strategic new products, equipment, technology, operational processes and marketing strategies impact our business, serving as not only a data analytic expert, but also as a valued internal consultant to a wide range of internal customers across functions. If you have a passion for the intersection of data, strategy, story-telling and consulting and find yourself naturally curious and excited about analytics, join us and be part of something bigger! Summary of Key Responsibilities Responsibilities and essential job functions include but are not limited to the following: Builds financial and statistical models, designs and measures tests/experiments with minimal guidance. Participates in discussions with business partners to define business questions. Guides business decisions through deep understanding of business drivers and degree of business impact. Creates structured presentations that concisely presents complex data and analysis. Connects data to insights to inform decision making.
