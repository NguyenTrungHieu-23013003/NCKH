The Expro Group has an operational structure of multiple regions which support global operations. The operation regions are supported by a number of global functions of which is the Survey Analyst. The Survey Analyst analyses and interprets customer caliper data, including both electronic and mechanical caliper data. Key Activities and Accountabilities: Develop an in-depth knowledge of recorded data from all types of Kinley Calipers (MC, KBC, MD, SO & TFL). Transfer recorded data from original recording chart to approved format. Translate data from other analysts for corrections and consistency. Review logs from other analysts for corrections and consistency. Report Calipers to Caliper Shop if they are not in calibration, alignment or working properly. Handle inquiries from customers both foreign and domestic for problem solving, report explanation and complaints, as needed. Deliver and discuss reports (follow-up sales work). 24 hour on-call rotation for rush surveys. Assist with other projects and any other department related matters. Customize reports/logs or sections of reports/logs to fit customer needs. Compile well logs to send to customers. Job Knowledge and Qualifications: Bilingual in Spanish is highly preferred Strong mathematical, critical thinking and problem solving skills Above average computer skills (Microsoft Word and Excel) Able to work 24/7 on-call rotation, as needed Familiar with the oil and gas industry is a plus Ability to work independently and confident in making decisions
