The Business Systems Analyst is responsible for maintaining and supporting Chemico’s ERP and inventory management systems. They are also responsible for developing appropriate and efficient solutions for the operations, accounting, and supply chain departments and are expected to coordinate between all departments. Responsibilities/Duties: Sage 100 troubleshooting and training for all company departments. Sage 100 administration. Handle technical support cases with Sage. CMIS (Clearspider) troubleshooting. CMIS (Clearspider) new program setup and training. Write and edit Crystal Reports. Advise on and develop technology and process solutions that solves relevant problems, streamlines workflow for all departments, and meets customer requirements. Assist with new program setup in Sage 100 and CMIS (Clearspider). Maintain an understanding of diverse and customer specific process flows. Develop an understanding of chemical management and chemical distribution. Backup the Business Intelligence team. Act as liaison between various business departments to translate business needs and processes into functional BI designs. Perform other duties and assignments as required.
