Description: SUMMARY Working with general supervision, the Data Analysis and CRM Insights Marketing Manager leverages a combination of strategic, quantitative, technical and presentation skills to deliver impactful analyses and easy-to-understand insights while partnering with business leaders, outside agency partners and marketing channel owners to help bring these insights to life in the form of executed customer facing campaigns. Associates in the role recognize and proactively explores instances where CRM can enhance business decision making, improve the customer experience, and lead to incremental revenue initiatives. As an expert in customer analytics and insights, this role collaborates with business stakeholders, external partners, and IT to influence improvements in the customer data and analysis environments; and may serve as the business stakeholder for data-related technical projects. Associate may interact with his or her supervisor several times a week, perhaps daily, to receive guidance and feedback. Some non-routine activities may require a supervisor’s advance approval. This role is generally characterized by moderately high levels of scope and complexity, and a high level of independent decision making.
