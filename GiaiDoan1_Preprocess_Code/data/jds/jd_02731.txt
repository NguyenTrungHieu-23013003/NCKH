Hiring Salary Range $68,344 - $88,847/annually DOE Grade 120 Work Schedule Monday - Friday, 8am - 5pm; some evenings and weekends may be required Work Calendar 12 Months Job Summary This is a key role that will focus on driving the rollout, performing steady state administration, planning, and maintenance of ITSM-based tools, methodologies, and practices across the Maricopa Community Colleges District. This role will establish and maintain strong working relationships with stakeholders and partners (e.g., IT support teams, help desk teams across the District, project teams) to successfully drive adoption of new ITSM capabilities and rollout and sustain enterprise ITSM tools, services and methodologies in the District. Areas of importance and in-scope for this role include ITSM practice expertise, ITSM tools management and administration, stakeholder engagement, training and District-wide knowledge sharing, implementations, service catalog and service desk metrics, and analysis of business needs and services and the supporting ITSM capabilities in place to meet the requirements of internal customers. The IT Support Coordinator will play a key role as assist IT leaders and teams in entrenching a “business and service culture” across the Maricopa Community Colleges District. analyzes and validates complex business, technology, systems and service management requirements and existing business processes and information systems, including functional validation and traceability and consensus building as required on IT and help desk teams 10% - As required, leads and/or assists in the negotiation, gaining buy-in for, the design, and maintenance of SLAs, SLOs, OLAs and related service desk metrics with partners inside the Information Technology Service organization and across the Maricopa Community Colleges District;
