If you answered "yes" to these questions, we want to talk to you about joining the Office of Legal Compliance (OLC) as a Business Program Manager/Analytics Specialist supporting the Compliance Analytics Program. OLC sits in the Litigation, Competition, and Compliance Group (LCCG) within Corporate, External, and Legal Affairs. LCCG’s mission is to champion Microsoft’s strategic interests with courts and regulators worldwide; to serve as trusted advisors; and to empower employees to uphold the highest standards of integrity. OLC’s specific strategies include designing, implementing, and running a system to provide early warning and monitoring for certain types of compliance risks using data analytics to (1) identify and prevent misconduct and (2) inform compliance program priorities. OLC works in close collaboration with Finance, Internal Audit, and Enterprise Commerce IT on the compliance analytics system. This role is based in Redmond, Washington. Responsibilities As a Business Analytics Specialist you will… Work with a team of business professionals and data scientists to help understand, analyze, define, and scope the business requirements, metrics, and KPIs for compliance risk scenarios. Contribute to the change management required to support the adoption of new compliance analytics and processes, including training content, and provide day-to-day program support for specific analytics within the Compliance Analytics Program ( _e.g.,_ High Risk Deals, High Risk Partners, Health of Competition, and/or Health of Compliance Program). Partner with teams to identify and explore opportunities for the application of data analytics to manage our compliance risks. Participate in development of go-to-market (GTM) activities for a compliance analytics solution. Develop and maintain strong relationship within and across various Microsoft teams.
