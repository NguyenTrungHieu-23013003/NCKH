The Analytics Coordinator will work within the Interactive Marketing team to aggregate, interpret and communicate meaningful findings to respective stakeholders throughout the entire marketing department. Data sources include but are not limited to website data via Google Analytics, app data via Firebase as well as paid digital media data (Google PPC and other paid digital platforms). The candidate should possess the following: Curiosity and passion for analytics as well as data’s ability to drive results for the business Experience with Google Analytics and suite (Data Studio, Google Tag Manager, Google Ads) Proficiency with Excel, including exposure to CPA/ROI calculations, data cleansing and queries Ability to interpret data, identifying “aha” results that can be acted on to improve performance Some exposure to testing methodology and best practices, including A/B tests of content, ads Responsibilities Work with Interactive Marketing team to accurately capture and present data via Data Studio Identify opportunities to automate reporting via custom dashboards, auto-sending reports, etc. Presentation of findings and recommendations of analysis, insights and immediate takeaways BA/BS Degree or Associate Degree in related field Data analysis background and/or advanced excel skills (pivot tables, etc) Familiarity or Certification with Google suite of marketing products preferred
