ASRC Federal Data Solutions (AFDS) has a contract to provide comprehensive IT services to NASA Ames Research Center located in Mountain View, CA. From cloud computing & network/information security to systems administration and technology development, we are encouraging talented IT professionals to explore the vast opportunities available on this NASA contract in the heart of Silicon Valley. We are currently seeking a Program Integration Lead to be a part of our ACITS4 operation. This role is responsible for overseeing the successful development, integration and implementation of program capabilities and the delivery/deployment of customer solutions. Additionally, the PIL is responsible for serving as the "voice of the customer" for any technical needs, challenges or issues regarding ACITS capabilities or solutions. This position is contingent upon the successful award of the contract. Supports the Program Manager and Deputy Program Manager in the execution of all ACITS-4 technical or technology requirements Periodically assesses the ACITS 4 Program technical capabilities/products/solutions against the level 3 and 4 requirements of the ACITS 4 SOW to measure Program technical effectiveness and success Ensures that ACITS 4 program solutions are designed and integrated based on the IT and programmatic standards and requirements defined by Ames and NASA (especially regarding the NASA Enterprise Architecture, IT Security requirements and Data Center standards/requirements) In coordination with Task Area Managers, ensures that ACITS-4 solutions meet or exceed customer requirements at the budget level defined in each Task Order and/or SOW before they are delivered to the customer Where feasible, ensures that ACITS-4 product/solution interfaces are compatible and that products/solutions can be integrated into other customer solutions, effectively lowering the cost to future customers through reuse Coordinates/communicates with ACITS-4 Program Management and technical SME's/Task Area Managers to ensure that technical and programmatic risks are managed in a manner that improves solution reliability/success and improves customer satisfaction In the absence of the Deputy Program Manager and at the direction of the Program Manager, may serve as the Deputy Program Manager with all necessary responsibility and authority of the position Supports outreach strategies necessary to assist the Ames market and implement service offerings within the ARC customer base as well as NASA-wide and other Federal Entities as appropriate Presents and communicates ACITS-4 products, services and solutions periodically at customer meetings and Ames Center IT Forums/events.
