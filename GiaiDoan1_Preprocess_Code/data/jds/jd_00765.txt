Visit our website at http://www.azdhs.gov Healthcare Data Analyst Public Health Licensing Open until filled JOB SUMMARY The Division of Public Health Licensing is looking for a motivated and dedicated individual to join our team! The Healthcare Data Analyst serves as the Senior analyst in the Office of Data and Quality Improvement in the Division of Licensing and oversees data management and administrative maintenance of all systems. As the senior analyst and under the direction of Bureau Chief, is responsible for advanced analysis in performance tracking and quality standard reporting for the improvement of licensing procedures including capturing key metrics, implementing new technologies and structural redesign. This position will regularly collaborate with Information Technology (IT) staff and assist in developing highly complex technical data systems and ensure they are operational by participating in software development life cycle projects including quality assurance testing. All Arizona State employees operate within the Arizona Management System (AMS), an intentional, results-driven approach for doing the work of state government whereby every employee reflects on performance, reduces waste, and commits to continuous improvement with sustainable progress. Through AMS, every state employee seeks to understand customer needs, identify problems, improve processes, and measure results. State employees are highly engaged, collaborative and embrace a culture of public service.
