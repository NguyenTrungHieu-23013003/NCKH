The Material Lead is responsible for determining and communicating the priorities from the manufacturing floor to the materials group. They are also responsible for inventory accuracy through the adherence to inventory control processes. Key Job Responsibilities Provide logistical support to plant manufacturing with emphasis on uninterrupted material availability. Track and report status of material being processed through manufacturing including overages and shortages. Provide priority and direction for the materials department with a positive and flexible attitude by directing the work needing to be accomplished daily using effective communication skills. As a team player must be able to identify and solve problems with only partial information have the ability to pull a cross functional team to resolve the problem. Monitor materials receiving reports to identify incoming materials required on the manufacturing floor. Measure performance and continuous improvement efforts in the areas of material handling and inventory control. Independently provide leadership in integrating existing processes with new innovations to economically solve inventory control and material handling issues in a timely manner Support the administration of employee activities for the materials, receiving and shipping (EP only) group e.g. training, development and mentoring. May manage the inventory cycle count program to continually improve our processes affecting inventory accuracy and control. Assists management in project related tasks and performs other duties as assigned.
