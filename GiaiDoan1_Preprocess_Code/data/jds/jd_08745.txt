Roza Route is a business management platform for the building services industry, helping teams of any size monitor and streamline their work through real time data on the job, while providing guidance and training. With RozaRoute’s one touch task completions, collecting real time data on site about your team’s performance is easy for companies and technicians. Dive into the details and uncover important insights from the entire business like never before. Roza Route can help transform company culture and empower teams with the data- driven recognition they deserve, simplifying and Automating Operations with integrated job scheduling, time keeping and geofencing throughout each workflow. Technician Profiles put a face to hard work and provide a place to showcase the unique talents and accomplishments of employees. We have a small team developing the platform while our parent company, Rozalado Services, employs over 250 people across commercial services like cleaning, floor services, property maintenance, post construction and more that will make for the perfect test environment. Our beta program begins August 1st and our official Launch will be at the ISSA Show on October 30th. This position is for our third Software Engineer. As a member of the Roza Route team, responsibilities include developing and launching a production application in the commercial cleaning and facility services industry. We are building a mobile application on iOS and Android with React Native, as well as a backend API and administrator portal built with React, Node and MongoDB. This application will be commercialized following successful implementation in the Rozalado business.
