Overview HarperCollins Publishers is seeking a Data Architect to join its Systems division. This role will be responsible for implementing a data architecture that supports the global operations at HarperCollins. The Data Architect will organize disparate data, control access to core data, establish data standards and definitions, and serve the engineering teams with clean and consistent data. The ideal candidate will have relevant experience transforming and modernizing large, complex, and global data architectures. This role will report into the Director, Data Engineering.
