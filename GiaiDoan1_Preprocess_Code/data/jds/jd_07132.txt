Job Summary and Mission This position contributes to Starbucks success by supporting our globally complex, vertically integrated operations. By managing the day-to-day operations of HFM, Oracle general ledger and related applications, this role is responsible for driving the month-end financial close process and providing user support, such as troubleshooting, input on business requirements, system maintenance, training and process enhancements. Models and acts in accordance with Starbucks guiding principles and values. Summary of Key Responsibilities Responsibilities and essential job functions include but are not limited to the following: Drives the monthly financial close process by proactively monitoring task completion, system outages and user updates and by assisting users on an ad hoc basis Manages day-to-day activities for HFM and related applications, including end user support, troubleshooting and managing user access requests Defines and maintains metadata dimension structures within DRM and HFM Defines and manages HFM business rules and security access Manages loading of financial data between systems and ensuring data integrity Oversees application governance for HFM systems as well as Oracle general ledger Maintains Oracle general ledger including cross-validation rules, security rules, open/close periods, creating and publishing FSG’s, etc. Manages FDM mapping, creates locations and manages securities Ensures appropriate internal controls are defined, communicated and followed Understands job requirements and performs internal controls, including providing evidence of internal control performance to management as well as internal and external auditors Leads rapid prototyping of system enhancements Participates in, and may lead, cross functional system implementation projects Develops and delivers end user systems training to ensure correct use of new tools, applications and procedures financial reports Develops recommendations and coordinates issue resolution with Information Technology (IT) Documents and communicates problem resolution with user community
