Job Description Expeditors’ data is a key element of Expeditors’ customer service and critical to the success of the company. Databases enable Expeditors’ data to be secure and readily available in a performant manner. The Database Administration Supervisor is a leader of a highly skilled technical team that is responsible for developing and supporting Expeditors’ Databases through a focus on people, projects, metrics, and culture. Major Duties and Responsibilities: Hire, develop, appraise, challenge, and mentor staff Proactively promote the company’s cultural attributes and a collaborative atmosphere Participate in defining, communicating, and implementing standards and processes Oversee team’s projects, taking ownership to ensure work is accurately estimated and projects are completed in a timely and cost effective manner Utilize metrics to set standards and goals in a collaborative manner Develop techniques to encourage staff to utilize metrics as a means to improving productivity Work with technical and non-technical stakeholders to understand their needs and priorities while keeping Expeditors’ best interests in mind Ensure that Expeditors’ database systems remain operational and well maintained Monitor the level of customer service within the team In a professional manner, provide honest input and feedback on work to peers and senior management Qualifications Essential Experience Bachelor’s PostgreSQL, or Vertica) Effective interpersonal skills, including proven abilities to listen and communicate effectively Ability to communicate technical information both verbally and in writing to technical, non-technical, and senior management staff Proven leadership skills and background An understanding of project management and a proven record of execution Experience in developing and
