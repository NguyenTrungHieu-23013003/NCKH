The IT Administrator will be responsible for the daily support and operation of technology systems in a growing environment. The scope of this role is broad and includes support, maintenance, and administration of many corporate systems including network, desktop support and provisioning (PC and Mac) and corporate telephony. In addition to daily operations, the selected candidate will be a crucial participant in the long-term strategic projects and implementations needed to scale the enterprise rapidly. Specifically, this will include the architecture and implementation of a content management system needed to efficiently support multiple, multi-site, clinical studies. In addition, the position will be responsible for the design and implementation and maintenance of diverse activity reports required for a governmental contract. Key Responsibilities: · Maintain office network, desktop, rack configuration, all hardware and software. · Management of voip telephony. · Create and maintain documentation on all systems. · Design and implement content management system for collaborative clinical study (possibly enterprise level in Box, Dropbox, or other vendor) · Design and implement electronic time/activity platform for governmental contract compliance. Required Qualifications: · Microsoft network/server pro, Office 365 pro. · Ability to communicate technical information, both verbal and written, to a wide range of end-users.
