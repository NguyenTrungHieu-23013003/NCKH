RingCentral’s Data Center Operations Team is responsible for monitoring, maintaining and upgrading the physical infrastructure for our data centers which deliver our cloud-based unified communications services to customers all over the world. RingCentral Cloud Operations is engineered for 99.999% uptime with domestic and international geo-redundant data centers. As a RingCentral data center engineer, your duties will range from physical deployment of servers, storage and network equipment to interacting with our key internal and external partners. While our headquarters are in Belmont, our local data centers are located in San Jose and up to 50% of your time will be spent there, as well as supporting our East Coast and international data centers as needed.
