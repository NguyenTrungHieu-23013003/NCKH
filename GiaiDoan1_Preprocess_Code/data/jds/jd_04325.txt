The manager of Competitive Intelligence will be the driving force to deliver insightful, actionable, and timely competitive intelligence to drive profitable growth and enhance corporate business strategy. This position represents a key role within Mitel and will identify the differentiation of Mitel’s Services offerings as compared to our competitors, provide competitive intelligence to key stakeholders, as well as influence the direction of our services offerings. Candidates will be able to easily transition between understanding competitors financial position and their product and channel strategies to build long term vision of their likely moves enabling Mitel to develop compelling and differentiated offers, and ultimately winning. Responsibilities: Provide insights on competitor strategies, strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, threats and long range strategic plans Develop predictive models on the evolution of the market and industry, providing recommendations on Mitel’s approach based on the markets and competitions likely strategic moves Leverage industry knowledge, competitive benchmarking, analyst data and internal company intelligence to build and maintain forecast model Work with key stakeholders to collect, analyze, and update reports, dashboards, scorecards, and analysis Prepare industry, market and competitive intelligence assessments, with business implications and actionable recommendations Develop and align the organization around an integrated and continually updated Competitive Intelligence System Become a trusted advisor on market and share implications, using data, insights and vision to help business leaders understand and act upon developments in the industry.
