The Role: The Research Analyst Intern will contribute to best in class research that differentiates JLL while building and maintaining a competitive advantage in the marketplace through market expertise, analysis, and insight. The analyst will have primary responsibility for managing data input and data quality while building out capabilities in JLL’s proprietary research database. This is a unique opportunity to contribute to the development of a new system that will benefit every service line in the JLL Minneapolis office. This is a paid internship beginning in June 2018. The internship length is estimated two to four months. The position has been budgeted for 20-40 hours per week. All applicable technology will be provided, including a laptop. A workstation will be assigned to the intern at JLL’s Minneapolis office.
