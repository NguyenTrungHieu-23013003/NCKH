We are currently seeking a Network Analyst to join our IT Security team. This full time role will work days. Provides expertise and leadership in the areas of networking and telecom by developing, implementing, ongoing management of cost effective infrastructures based on industry standards and best practices. The Network Analyst's role is to design, build, and implement network systems across the enterprise including planning, developing, installing, configuring, maintaining, supporting, and optimizing all local and wide area network connections, corporate servers, associated software, and communication links. This person will also troubleshoot network performance issues, as well as analyze network traffic and provide capacity planning solutions. RESPONSIBILITIES Analyze network and system faults, troubleshoot and perform diagnostics on network operating systems and hardware to isolate and resolve communication issues. Respond to or coordinate the response to incident, problem, and change management requests made to the network teams in order to meet published service levels and/or quality-related goals. Provide effective oral and written communication to management as requested for the status of day-to-day operations and for specific projects Implement, support, monitor, and provide advice on risk assessment, network security solutions including firewall, intrusion prevention, host-based security, authentication and encryption systems to ensure internal and external security controls are appropriate and operating as intended Maintain and support enforcement of information security policies, procedures, standards and guidelines based on knowledge of best practices and compliance requirements Define security requirements, perform risk assessments, establish and maintain security controls, monitor and evaluate security measures, install and configure network security solutions in support of business objectives Conduct communications with vendors and other outside resources to resolve complex network issues when needed.
