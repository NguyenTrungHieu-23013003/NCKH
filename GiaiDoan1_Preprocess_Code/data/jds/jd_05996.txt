A Day in the Life of a Sr. Data Warehouse Developer: This is a senior role for experienced data warehouse and ETL developers. The role will be expert at eliciting requirements from business subject matter experts, leading discussions and facilitating sessions to get deep understanding of how the warehouse and business intelligence can support their departments. This position is responsible for developing broad healthcare subject matter expertise as it relates to building the warehouse, supporting BI reporting, and analytics. The role will be expert at developing and understanding the correct business rules to apply, and is essential to assuring that only quality data enters the data warehouse and the big data environment. The senior developer will be expert at ensuring data quality and will ensure that transformation processes properly transform and enrich the data. The role will be expert at understanding data warehouse design, both Inmon and Kimball methodologies as well as data denormalization techniques. The senior developer will mentor and assist junior staff in developing SQL, ETL, data structure designs, and data warehouse designs. This position requires a level of interest and passion for Big Data processing, warehousing, reporting, and analytics that is unparalleled!
