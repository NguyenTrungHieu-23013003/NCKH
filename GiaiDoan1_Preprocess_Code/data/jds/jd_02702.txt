Computype, a global manufacturer of labeling solutions, is seeking a Business Analyst with an Epicor ERP focus. This position is responsible for gathering and documenting business requirements and working with technical teams to develop and deliver solutions that enable and support the business. As a member of Computype’s Information Technology team, this candidate will apply their knowledge of business systems to identify and solve unique and complex problems related to global business software. Major Areas of Accountability Facilitate meetings with business partners to understand their needs and build positive relationships. Translate business needs into detailed system requirement documents. Analyze business requirements and work with other technical resources to propose solutions. Collaborate with the business to review solutions and influence positive change. Develop detailed test scripts and test business software changes with a high degree of accuracy. Provide training and Work Aid support to departments as needed. Take the approach of ‘train the trainer’. Support continuous improvement by developing and sharing expert knowledge of our business systems. Become a trusted advisor to business partners. Desired Skills and Experience Qualifications Bachelor’s degree or related work experience equivalent to a degree. Experience with Epicor ERP software versions 9 or 10. Have a natural analytical way of thinking and be able to explain difficult concepts to non-technical users. Strong problem-solving skills related to business software. Excellent organizational skills, works efficiently and is detail oriented. Excellent written and verbal communications skills. Have a customer centric attitude.
