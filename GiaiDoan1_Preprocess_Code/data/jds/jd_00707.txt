This position is housed within the Neuropsychology group at the Framingham Heart Study and BU School of Public Health. Primary responsibilities of this job are to assist with data management and data analysis of genetic and genomic data both at the Framingham Heart Study and BU School of Public Health. This includes assisting with analyses to meet the aims of several funded projects, assist with FHS data cleaning and maintenance of data sets and preparing datasets to meet FHS data sharing requirements. This position is entry level for someone pursuing a career in biostatistics and big data analytics. Other administrative tasks may be assigned as needed. Required Skills 1-3 years of experience and Bachelor's degree required. Travel required and willingness to work non-traditional hours when needed is also desired.
