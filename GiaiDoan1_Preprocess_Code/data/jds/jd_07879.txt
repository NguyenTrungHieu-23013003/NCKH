The Customer Success Consultant is focused on developing and improving customer's marketing strategies, working closely with client leadership and partnering with Web.com Sales, Product Performance, and Client Services teams. As a Customer Success Consultant I- you will be responsible for managing National Enterprise Multi- Location or Franchise accounts where the annualized book of business of approximately $200K-$500K.
