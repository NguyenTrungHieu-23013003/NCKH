Monster is searching for an Enterprise Data Architect to join our team! The Enterprise Data Architect is a hands-on role that helps define the Monster Worldwide data strategy and its implementation. The data architect will provide thought leadership across all data systems and data stores including transactional, operational, warehousing and big data stores. This individual will collaborate across all levels of the organization, business and technology to build a holistic view of the organization’s data architecture. This includes maturing enterprise standards, processes and workflows to manage and ensure data quality, data ingestion, data processing, data visualization and data consumption. This individual will also participate in business and IT assessments to drive alignment to overall enterprise data strategy. This individual will provide technical thought-leadership across the organization, from strategic decision making down to the project planning level. He/she will be expected to demonstrate significant leadership in influencing and driving architecture and technology initiatives across the organization as it relates to data architectures and principles. Over time they will help define and evangelize the vision and strategy for data architecture, data quality and data governance.
