The NASA Ames Research Center has the requirement for a Senior Systems Architect who performs the role of simulation architect. The SIMLABS have four simulator lab systems that work both independently and in various networked configurations as a system of systems. They are “Hard” real-time devices. Each lab has one to 50 simulation stations attached. They are used for high fidelity flight simulation and air traffic control simulations. They are presently located in 5 buildings at Ames. In addition these devices occasionally network to other real-time simulations throughout the country. This position will have the responsibility of working with NASA personnel to maintain and improve the HW and SW architecture of the individual simulations and also the network of simulations Requirements: Required: BS in computer science or engineering plus 15 years applicable experience including networking of a system of systems. 5 years experience in flight simulation is required. Leadership in managing complicated technical programs is a must. Desired: Advanced degree in Engineering or computer science. Experience in development of real-time simulation architecture. Experience with HLA, DIS, and DDS network simulation protocols is further desired.
