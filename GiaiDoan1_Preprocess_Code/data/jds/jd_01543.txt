The Network for Computational Modeling in the Social and Ecological Sciences (https://www.comses.net), part of the NSF West Big Data Hub (http://westbigdatahub.org), is dedicated to improving the way we build, share, and reuse computational models of complex social-ecological systems. We have an immediate opening for a frontend software engineer interested in building cyberinfrastructure to facilitate reuse and reproducibility in scientific computation. Under general direction, the successful candidate will develop intuitive web-based user interfaces and data visualizations that foster community engagement and enable researchers to create, share, and extend citable archives of the complete computational workflows (models, analyses, dependencies, and datasets) crucial to reproducible computational research but often neglected in publications. Full stack developers interested in specializing into frontend work are welcome to apply.
