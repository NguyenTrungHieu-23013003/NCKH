Job Summary: The Business Analyst is responsible for the reporting and analytics framework of Yamibuy. This critical role will interact with different business units directly to understand reporting needs and works to deliver reporting, dashboards and analysis. Job Requirements: Understand high level business goals and use these goals to guide the work Collaborate with internal stakeholders to obtain information for ad-hoc/routine reporting and analysis needs. Perform qualitative and quantitative analysis that provides meaningful insights and synthesized recommendations to support informed decision-making Cooperate with data engineers to build up Yamibuy data platform, by understanding the business requirements and the data users’ needs. Develop dashboards that provide a clear view of the business by visually translating complex data into actionable insights. Present data related information succinctly and effectively to stakeholders and leadership Monitor published information regularly and follow-up timely when corrections are necessary to ensure reporting accuracy and integrity Understand basic finance and accounting Qualifications and Requirements: Minimum of 2 years business analyst or data analyst related experience Bachelor’s degree in Accounting, Business, Information Systems, Engineering or related field preferred, or a combination of education and equivalent work experience. Highly proficient in Excel Extract, cleanse and validate data using mySQL Experience developing reports and dashboards utilizing visualization tools Ability to perform analysis with data supported conclusions Strong verbal, written and interpersonal communication skills Problem solving and critical thinking skills Ability to organize and prioritize work, meet tight dead-lines Proponent of developing a data-driven culture and supporting other team member Understands data architecture within relational database environment is a plus Proficient in writing time efficient mySQL script is a plus Knowledge of using ETL tools, SSIS, and Alteryx is a plus Experience developing dashboards and reports with Tableau is a plus
