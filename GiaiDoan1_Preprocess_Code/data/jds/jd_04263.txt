The Chief Equity and Inclusion Officer (CEIO) is responsible for developing, implementing and advocating for a comprehensive health equity, inclusion and diversity strategy for Children's Minnesota. This role is in direct service of Children's vision to be every family's essential partner in raising healthier children. The CEIO will align critical elements of a clinical equity strategy and oversee execution of prioritized, sequenced projects/initiatives in collaboration with other senior and executive leaders across the organization. These critical elements include: addressing organizational/structural inequities, clinical/quality interventions, developing recruitment and retention strategies, as it relates to diversity and inclusion, that help create an open and inclusive workforce environment, fostering community engagement and building organizational awareness. This leader will provide direction and create accountability to the equity plan with executive leadership and organizational leaders. S/he will provide strategic and operational level guidance and consultation to relevant teams and leaders, including the medical leadership teams. In partnership with/reporting to the Chief Legal Officer and Chief Medical Officer, the CEIO will advise Children's executive team and Board of Directors on appropriate approaches and commensurate resources to advance equity and inclusion.
