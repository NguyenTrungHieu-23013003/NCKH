Duties Summary Defense Contract Management Agency uses the Expedited Hiring Authority to recruit and appoint qualified individuals directly into shortage category or critical need acquisition positions. Responsibilities Reviews contracts and technical data packages to identify the specification requirements. Establishes and maintains communications with contractor's top management, including applicable functional managers such as production, engineering, and QA personnel. Designs, plans and implements an effective risk management program to ensure that all threats to the successful delivery of the contract are addressed and mitigated. Conducts post-award first article meetings to assess the adequacy of suppliers first article plans and processes. Performs pre-award surveys in conjunction with other functional specialists. Plans and carries out assignments independently and determines the scope and level of quality assurance activities based on the requirements. Travel Required Occasional travel - Business travel is required 5% of the time. Supervisory status No Promotion Potential 11
