The HIPAA Information Security Analyst is responsible for supporting the initiatives of the Montefiore Care Management Organization. This individual works with compliance in creating and implementing relevant policies and procedures to ensure compliant information security processes. The policies provide direction within the organization and are critical in supplying the appropriate information for independent security audits from Health Plans, and State and Federal regulatory agencies. The Analyst provides outstanding client service and contributes to the organization’s mission of utilizing information technology to improve overall user experience while maintaining a HIPAA secure data environment. The position will also assist in performing periodic internal audits to ensure compliance as well as preparing material for any external IT audit from delegated Health Plans or State and Federal agencies as needed. Requirements: Bachelor's Degree Information Security Analyst Level certification preferred (SSCP, CISA) At least 5 years experience Candidate must be well verse in IT Security concepts, Windows Active Directory. Also requires thorough knowledge of the following operating systems, Windows 10 & 7, MAC OS.
