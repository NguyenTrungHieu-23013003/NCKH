The Salesforce Developer designs, builds and deploys solutions and enhancements into existing Salesforce.com ecosystem including integration with partner applications such as DocuSign and PROS CPQ. Additionally, this position builds out new and extends existing functionality within custom quoting tools using Excel VBA. RESPONSIBILITIES Implements Customer Relations Management (CRM) solutions. Integrates and deploys third party tools such as DocuSign, SpringCM, TIN Check into Salesforce Force.com platform. Provides expertise in implementing Salesforce best practices while developing Apex, Visualforce pages, Batches, Triggers and Lighting frameworks. Understands and applies product requirements, designing, coding and testing towards building the next generation of PROS CPQ products within Cable One Business Services environment. Builds out new functionality and extending existing features on existing applications developed using MS Access/Excel VBA. Provides support and general maintenance of third party applications including communications with vendors. Analyzes project requests to estimate time and cost required to accomplish project. Works as part of a project team to coordinate software environments and determine project scope and limitations. Other duties and/or responsibilities not specifically set forth above may, however, be assigned as needed. QUALIFICATIONS _EDUCATION and/or EXPERIENCE_ Bachelor’s degree (B.A.) from four-year college or university; and a minimum of three years related experience and/or training; or equivalent combination of education and experience. _CERTIFICATES, LICENSES, REGISTRATIONS_ Active salesforce certifications are required. _OTHER QUALIFICATIONS_ Minimum of 3 years Salesforce.com platform/Sales Cloud/Service Cloud implementations is required. Familiarity with agile software delivery methodologies. Up to 10% travel may be required.
