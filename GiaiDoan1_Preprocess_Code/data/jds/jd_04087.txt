Postdoctoral position within the Department of Mechanical Engineering at The University of Texas at Dallas in grid integration of renewable energy. The position will focus on power system operations with high penetrations of renewables and distributed energy resources, extreme ramp events forecasting, solar and wind power forecasting, dynamic reserves, and flexible ramping products. Minimum Education and Experience Ph.D in a related field. Preferred Education and Experience Experience with one or several of the following would be beneficial: renewable energy forecasting, deep learning, unit commitment and economic dispatch, optimization, energy storage, microgrid, electricity market design, distribution system modeling, stochastic modeling and optimization. Good programming skills and familiar with one or more of the following tools: R, Python, MATLAB/Simulink, OpenDSS, GridLab-D, CPLEX, GAMS, PYOMO. Essential Duties and Responsibilities Duties to include, but not limited to designing experiments, conducting research, writing articles, and training graduate students. Research in grid integration of renewable energy. Work with industry and academic collaborators on related research projects, as directed by advisor. Report research results as journal and conference publications. Identify new sources of funding and participate in the preparation of proposals as required.
