Perkins Coie LLP seeks a talented Financial Analyst to join its Timekeeper Analytics Team in the Seattle office. The Analyst will assist with the development, implementation and distribution of financial data, reports and tools used by firm management and compensation committees to analyze and evaluate timekeeper performance. Specific responsibilities will include: modeling timekeeper performance for profitability and generating standardized profitability reporting; compiling, validating and analyzing data to ensure accuracy of information used to make compensation decisions; assisting with the documentation, review and revision of algorithms that define performance- related statistical data; developing and maintaining tools used to monitor attorney compensation arrangements; performing other complex and strategic analysis; supporting the activities of the firm’s partner and other timekeeper compensation committees in collaboration with the full support team. This position requires superior analytical skills and strong financial capabilities. Demonstrated ability to work across functional teams and will all levels of firm personnel. Mastery of data analysis using advanced Excel analytical functions. Ability to work on multiple projects simultaneously and adapt to changing priorities. Experience working in a team-oriented, collaborative environment. Highly self-motivated and self-directed, with strong attention to detail. Ability to communicate effectively to business and technical stake holders throughout the firm. Proficiency with the Microsoft Office suite, including Excel, Outlook, Word and PowerPoint. Solid understanding of relational databases and working knowledge of SQL. Experience with Cognos, Tableau or other business intelligence tools a plus. The ideal candidate will possess BA/BS degree in Accounting, Finance or Management Information Systems, and at least 3 years of relevant professional experience.
