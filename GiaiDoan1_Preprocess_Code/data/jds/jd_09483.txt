The ERP Technical Analyst reports to the Manager of IS Business Applications. The person in this role provides technical support for the ERP system, including configuration, testing, integration, reporting and security. Additionally, the position includes interaction with end users in HR, Payroll, Finance and Supply Chain departments to gather requirements, build solutions, resolve issues and coordinate testing and validation. The scope of work in this capacity includes both projects with starting and ending dates as well as ongoing support with consistent standards and routine best practices. QUALIFICATIONS: 1\. Bachelor's degree with a minimum of 4 years related work experience OR Master's degree in a related field with 2 years related work experience. 2\. Degree in information technology, industrial engineering or engineering, business or health care preferred. 3\. Demonstrated skill and experience in supporting the Workday ERP system. 4\. Experience with Visio, flow diagrams, SharePoint or other collaboration tools. 5\. Excellent written and oral communication skills. 6\. Excellent interpersonal and customer service skills 7\. Ability to learn, understand and apply new technologies. 8\. Ability to effectively prioritize and execute tasks in a high-pressure environment. 9\. Moderate to expert levels of knowledge around IT and healthcare sector concepts and trends. 10\. Experience in system designs and workflow, identifying solutions, multi- tasking and managing multiple types of support activities, successfully interacting with clients, problem solving and initiating corrective project actions. License/Certification/Registration Requirements 1\. Certification or related experience in at least two areas of knowledge within the Workday ERP system.
