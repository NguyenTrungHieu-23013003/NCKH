POSITION OVERVIEW: The Chicago Bulls Business Strategy and Analytics Department is a unique in- house strategy group responsible for supporting the creative development and execution of long-term growth initiatives of the team in terms of both revenue and fans. Our team is dedicated to better understanding fans of the Chicago Bulls. With outputs of this enhanced understanding, we focus on improving customer facing revenue streams (e.g. tickets, sponsorship, merchandise, concessions), driving improved fan engagement, and optimizing overall marketing efforts across channels. We serve as consultants and advisors on the strategic direction for the company and on individual initiatives. The department’s overall vision is to be a customer and consumer insight hub for the entire organization that delivers timely data and insights to the key decision makers across the business. In this role, you will support strategic and financial evaluation and implementation of a broad range of business initiatives across various departments. These include building upon and developing new strategies to support organic and inorganic growth in existing and new business lines, and working with business leaders in departments such as ticketing, sponsorship, or merchandising to execute these strategies.
