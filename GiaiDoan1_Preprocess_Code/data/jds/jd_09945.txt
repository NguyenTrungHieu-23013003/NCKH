Overview Provides discipline-specific or system-specific engineering support on all systems, system elements, interfacing systems, components, devices and/or processes. Serves on a team tasked with providing technical solutions for the production environment. Helps support internal and external customers on complex technical issues. Develops documents and supports the processes and methodologies that meet the department’s service standards. Manages level 2 support for all applications and components of client-facing devices. Assists in resolving incidents and problems to prevent or address systems issues and performance crunches. Works with internal and external clients and consultants, including business managers, project managers, software engineers, security analysts, data center engineers and technical architects, to diagnose and recommend course of action.
