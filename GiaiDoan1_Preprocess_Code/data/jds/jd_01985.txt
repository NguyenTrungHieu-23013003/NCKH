The Database Administrator-Foundation is an exciting full-time position that is responsible for comprehensive database administration for the Foundation. The successful candidate ensures all donor data is collected, stores and properly used by all members of the organization. Database cleanup, complex queries, exports and reports and data entry including gift processing and gift acknowledgment. This position conducts reconciliation between the Foundation and Finance department, and researches current and prospective donors using web-based databases and wealth screening services, preparing written donor profiles. Minimum 3 years hands-on experience in database management or information technology. AAS Degree required. Equivalent combination of education and experience will be considered. Must be proficient in Excel, advanced Excel skills preferred. Experience with Blackbaud Raiser’s Edge/NXT preferred. Experience in other CRM systems will be strongly considered. Experience with Crystal reports a plus. Must be detail oriented and possess excellent organizational skills with ability to prioritize, multi-task, and juggle multiple assignments and deadlines. Ability to maintain confidentiality.
