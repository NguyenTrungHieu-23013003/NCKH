Job Description IBM Watson Health is searching for a Database Administrator with SQL Server, MongoDB and DB2 skills to join the Data Management team. The Data Management team is responsible for the database systems that drive IBM’s Population Health Management and Patient Engagement product offerings. Database Administrator will be responsible for performing routine database operational tasks to ensure security, high performance, and availability of IBM’s production and non-production database computing environments. The Database Administrator will occasionally perform database development using advanced Transact SQL. Database Administrator must be familiar with designing and constructing SSIS packages, SSRS reports in order to support the varying needs of our customers.
