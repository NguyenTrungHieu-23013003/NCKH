Job Description Work as a Product Manager in the merchant data space. Drive, execute and deliver on the defined roadmap with data engineers, operations and infrastructure; be the 'Product Owner' in the scrum model. Come up with data insights to influence product development, vision (ability to tailor data driven results to various audience levels), and develop proof-of-concept prototype, if required. Focus on merchant data quality and coverage from a regional data perspective; be the regional merchant data SME. Manage business operations from a data product/platform perspective and work with clients and third party as needed. Manage defined operations services framework and service levels. Identify new opportunities and uses of standardized merchant data to improve overall data usage experience. Work with other product managers to execute and deliver as a team.
