The role of Cabinet Operations Data Support is critical to the success of the company’s cabinet design team. This position is responsible for the transfer of all cabinet materials and related items designed in the 20/20 computer program to our proprietary ERP system. A working knowledge of and experience with the importing of cabinet bill of materials and related items including installation layouts is highly desired as this role is also responsible for maintaining bill of materials for related customer and industry specifications. Experience with kitchen and bath cabinetry is preferred, as this role is required to read and interpret technical information related to cabinet design specifications along with the responsibility of generating and publishing pricing for cabinet bill of materials. We are seeking a team player who is self-motivated to meet project deadlines while working in a group environment. If you enjoy a fast-paced office environment and have a “can do” attitude when it comes to meeting crucial project timelines, then this role is especially designed for you.
