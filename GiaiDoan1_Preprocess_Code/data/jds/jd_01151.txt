He/she documents the architecture and design, technical requirements, HW and SW requirements, and works with the engineering and infrastructure teams to ensure successful implementation and operation of big data and analytics systems. This person will also participate in and lead proof of concept activities in a broad range of big data and analytics activities across the enterprise.
