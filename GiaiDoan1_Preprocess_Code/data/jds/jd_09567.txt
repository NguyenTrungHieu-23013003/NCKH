Overview The Network Engineer is responsible for design, support, and maintenance of the data, voice, video, and wireless networks. Candidates must have previous experience managing networks and possess excellent troubleshooting skills in order to be considered for this role. Responsibilities Fundamental Duties Manage and maintain multi-vendor, multi-protocol networks with expertise in Cisco switching, routing, and firewalls Plan, schedule, and implement network maintenance outside of business hours to include firmware upgrades, hardware replacement, and configuration changes Collaborate with network architects on existing and future designs to optimize performance, security and scalability Interface with 3rd party carriers to troubleshooting and repair network outages Maintain and update network documentation and designs Monitor network usage, access, and performance metrics to ensure it meets current and future needs of company Ensure adherence to PCI and other regulatory guidelines with regard to system security Qualifications Preferred Minimum Experience/Education Bachelor’s degree in computer science or other related field, or equivalent experience Minimum of 7 years in network management Proficient with enterprise class networking equipment within a data center environment Cisco certification preferred PCI experience a plus
