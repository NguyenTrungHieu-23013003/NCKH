The Systems Administrator (Systems Admin) provides administrative and technical support for the Arc of Onondaga’s (Agency) IT systems infrastructure, application systems and client end-users. Primary duties include deploying and supporting IT strategies and initiatives. Individual must be capable of working hands-on in support of a complex IT environment. The Arc of Onondaga’s IT environment supports the agency’s multi-functional programs for individuals with developmental disabilities. As a result we have evolved our IT capabilities to meet the associated business challenges providing health services across the various agency programs. The candidate for this position will need to be able to contribute and learn many different IT disciplines. End user computing PC / Windows administration Email Administration Domain administration Security administration Network management Server management Mobile devices and applications Voice and Data, including collaboration The successful candidate will bring 2-4 years’ experience in a similar technical role. The initial and primary focus area for the Systems Admin will be end user deployment and support. Skills that support that role are required for consideration of this position. A minimum of a 2-year degree in an IT discipline is required.
