Job Code 405016 IT Help/Support (N) Duties & Responsibilities Media Services provides best-in-class support to the HBS global community by connecting campus learning and convening spaces to the world, and by offering comprehensive and innovative audiovisual solutions to our customers. The Live Events Producer is part of the Media Services Operations team and is responsible for coordinating AV technology and resources for live events across the HBS Campus. This includes 250 audiovisual enabled classroom, conference rooms, auditoriums, and other convening spaces spread across 35 campus buildings. A focus of the role will be on the production and coordination of events in Klarman Hall, a new 80,000 sq. ft. convening center opening in summer 2018. This 1,000 seat facility features immersive and industry-leading technologies, including one of the largest indoor video walls on the east coast, voice lift, and an electronically controlled acoustical adjustment system. The Live Events Producer will be responsible for closely collaborating with stakeholders during all phases of event planning, from concept to day of support, to ensure Media Services has a comprehensive understanding of the technology and support expectations for success. They will be the subject matter expert on Klarman Hall and represent the full catalog of services offered to customers.
