Position Summary: The Clinical Data Analyst is responsible for accurately capturing, recording, validating, and reporting data for quality assurance and performance improvement (QAPI). They must ensure that QAPI reports and associated electronic health record documentation are completed consistently, and that summarized data is presented back to the appropriate stakeholders. This analysis may include acute care, outpatient, ambulatory clinic, health plan data, and other areas of the continuum of care. Position Details: Part-Time | 20.00 hours/week Required Qualifications: Bachelor’s Degree in Health Information Management, Computer Science, Business Administration, Finance, or a related Healthcare discipline. Three (3) or more years of data management, data analysis, or quality improvement experience in an acute care or clinical setting. Proficiency with Microsoft Excel, Microsoft Access, SQL, or other statistical software. Preferred Qualifications: Experience working with Electronic Health Record or similar data sets.
