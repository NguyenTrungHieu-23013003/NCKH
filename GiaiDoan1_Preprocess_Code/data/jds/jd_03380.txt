Job Description This posting is for 2018 Ford GDI &A; Summer Interns that are seeking full- time offers in the GDI&A; FCG Program. The GDI&A; FCG Program provides a structured job rotation process for participants. The goal of the program is to provide foundational experiences that will accelerate your development. FCG’s will have the opportunity to practice analytics in multiple departments and domains, share fresh perspectives on systems and processes, and develop a macro-level understanding of the GDI&A; eco-system. FCG’s will also have additional development opportunities through exposure to multiple supervisors, networking opportunities, and mentoring circles. We are trusted advisers enabling Ford to see our business, know our customers and act in a meaningful way. We drive evidence-based decision making by providing timely, actionable and forward-looking insights to our One Ford business partners.
