General Summary of the Purpose of the Position This position is responsible for research and development of machine learning and signal processing algorithms for hearing enhancement and health and wellness monitoring in hearing aids and related accessories. DSP Research Engineer II signifies that at least 50% of the competencies at the Sr. Subject Matter Expert level have been demonstrated. Approach and Deployment (What to Do & How to Do It) Research machine learning and signal processing algorithms for hearing devices for hearing enhancement and health and wellness monitoring Review the existing literature, competitive products and patents Propose a set of algorithm candidates Prototype the algorithm candidates using simulation and/or various research platforms Evaluate the algorithm candidates based on a combination of laboratory evaluation, field evaluation and benchmarking Select the final algorithm Contribute to the research report Participate in and contribute to general machine learning and signal processing research and development activities including brainstorming, patent and publications Participate in and contribute to the relevant professional conferences Participate in and contribute to the Signal Processing Journal Seminar and the Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence Journal Seminar Participate in and contribute to the competitive analysis efforts Participate in and contribute to the benchmarking efforts Participate in and contribute to the intellectual property related activities, such as patents Actively collaborate with product development teams to bring the research results to products Work with product development teams to create the firmware requirements definition Work with the DSP firmware engineers and software engineers to identify and make the design tradeoffs Provide technical support to the clinical validation effort Providing technical training to product development group members Results – Performance Measures (How to Measure Success) This goal will be evaluated based on research report review for each research project This goal will be evaluated in the annual review of publications, patent applications and active participation in benchmarking efforts and journal seminar This goal will be evaluated in the annual performance review Other Duties (Non Measurable) Support Starkey Employee
