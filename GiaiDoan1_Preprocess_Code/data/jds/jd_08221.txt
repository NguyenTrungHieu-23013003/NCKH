Duties Summary About the Position: The mission of the U.S. Army Garrison - Natick (USAG-N) is to provide Natick, with the installation capabilities and services to support expeditionary operations in a time of persistent conflict, and to provide a quality of life for Soldier& Families commensurate with their service. Responsibilities As a Plans and Operations Specialist, for the US Army Garrison Natick, Directorate of Plans, Training, Mobilization & Security, Plans and Operations Branch you will: Serve as the Branch Chief with supervisory responsibilities i.e. planning work to be accomplished by subordinates, set and adjust short-term priorities and prepare schedules for the completion of work. In addition, assign work to subordinates based on priorities; evaluate work performance; interview candidates, etc. Serve as the primary point of contact (POC) and Subject Matter Expert on contingency planning for the installation, providing guidance on Emergency Operations procedures of short and long term Installation Operations and Training plans. Oversee organization information management operations during crisis/contingency operations. Inspect facilities and equipment such as emergency management centers and communications equipment in order to determine their operational and functional capabilities in emergency situations. Coordinate crisis/contingency plans with local emergency response organizations both internal and external to the organization, Higher Headquarters, and local government agencies. Manage life cycle requirements, maintain operational status, and safeguard Emergency related automated systems according to existing laws and regulations. Develop and conduct effective training programs for all Employees and Supervisors on the Installation Operations Center requirements, procedures, and communications.
