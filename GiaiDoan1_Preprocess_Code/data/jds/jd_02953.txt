The Payments Compliance team is a global team responsible for protecting the Facebook Payments platform and ensuring compliance with a wide range of global BSA processes, such as Sanctions, Anti-Money Laundering, Know Your Customer (KYC) programs. The team is looking for a Data Analytics Manager who can lead a team to leverage data and analytics to identify opportunities to increase the efficiency of the payments compliance team. The successful candidate will manage a team focusing on driving data-informed strategies and creating the necessary infrastructure to scale our operations as well as the reporting to measure the team's top goals and KPIs. The ideal candidate is an expert in modeling techniques and methodologies and has experience training a vast array of models. They must also be able to see beyond the data to identify solutions that will raise the bar for all things payments compliance-related. Reporting to the Head of Payments Compliance, you will help Facebook Payments ensure that its compliance policies, internal controls, and systems help Facebook in its mission to "make the world more open and connected." This is a full-time position based in our Menlo Park HQ.
