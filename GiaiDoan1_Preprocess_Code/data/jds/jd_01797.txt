Qualifications: Ability to work independently and with other personnel. Excellent communication skills, a professional appearance, some computer knowledge, and the ability to merchandise and understand superior customer care. Education: High school diploma or equivalent. Healthcare Related certification or degree is a plus. Primary Purpose of Position: This position is responsible for all daily activities involved in the retail environment, i.e. Sales, merchandising, rentals, requisitioning product, cleaning, stocking, etc. Essential Duties & Responsibilities: Essential duties and responsibilities include the following. Other duties may be assigned. 1) Perform phone and walk-in sales. 2) Assist with evaluation of products to maximize customer benefit. 3) Enter patient information in computer system. 4) Maintain open lines of communication with family/facility with regards to orders. 5) Establish and coordinate contact with the patient caregivers/physicians for supplies and patient condition updates. 6) Responsible for verification of insurance coverage, patient information and vital statistics needed to service and generate customer orders. 7) Participate with in-service education programs/seminars that need to be presented internally and externally. 8) Representation at sales shows, display areas, public health services, classes and educational medias. 9) Assume responsibility for continuing education through seminars, in- services, classes and/or educational media. 10) Maintain a professional atmosphere in department. 11) All other related duties as assigned by manager.
