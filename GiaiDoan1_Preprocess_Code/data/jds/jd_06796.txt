The Learning and Development Consultant position plays an integral role in creating a continuous learning culture and enhancing the professional growth of UCSF staff and leaders. Uses proven experience and abilities to create and deliver learning solutions that fully engage learners enhance their performance and support UCSF’s mission and priorities. LEARNING & ORGANIZATION DEVELOPMENT The purpose of the Learning and Organization Development department is to enhance the success of UCSF by supporting the growth and development of its most valuable resource, its people, and to create a continuous learning environment. The department offers comprehensive professional development experiences for supervisory and non-supervisory staff to facilitate talent management. It also creates custom e-learning solutions, blended learning and other technology-enabled solutions tailored for performance improvement. And through organization development consulting, the department enables UCSF leaders to increase their departments' effectiveness through needs assessments, consulting, and facilitation in many areas, such as: Engagement Strategic planning Team/department performance Change management Executive coaching and Leader transitions into new roles. ABOUT UCSF The University of California, San Francisco (UCSF) is a leading university dedicated to promoting health worldwide through advanced biomedical research, graduate-level education in the life sciences and health professions, and excellence in patient care. It is the only campus in the 10-campus UC system dedicated exclusively to the health sciences.
