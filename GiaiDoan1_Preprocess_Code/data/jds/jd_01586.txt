Job Description What We Can Offer You: This reporter will cover the intersection of power, culture, and politics through the lens of gender. From the fight over reproductive rights to fair pay to MeToo and beyond, this reporter will break news on the biggest issues impacting women today. The ideal candidate will have a high news metabolism and be attuned to the national conversation around politics, the workplace, popular culture, and more. The reporter must also have demonstrated skills to write quick stories and deeply-reported articles that weave interviews, profile material, anecdotes, and exclusive details into compelling narratives. The reporter then must be able to effectively communicate those stories across TV, radio, and social media . What You Get To Do: Research/write multiple items per week. Examine copy for accuracy, checking all factual information, quotes, and statistics. Interview newsmakers and tastemakers. Contribute to breaking news coverage of major events. Help produce additional video, photo, or social content as needed.
