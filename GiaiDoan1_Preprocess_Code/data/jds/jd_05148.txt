A Whirlwind Data Entry Specialist supports our Inside Sales Team Members for one of the Metal Building Industrys most experienced companies. Due to its extremely large and unique product offering, the Data Entry Specialists purpose is to key all orders, receive payment, coordinate deliveries as well as assist the Territory Managers with gaining new customers and keeping them buying from Whirlwind. The Data Entry Specialists effectiveness is measured and graded by their low mistake ratio when keying orders as well as their internal and external customer service qualities. The Data Entry Specialist is responsible for maintaining mid-level relationships with customers and prospects and their inside sales manager or TM and District Sales Managers. Our goal is to provide the highest level of quality service to our customers and this position is an essential part of Whirlwinds initiative. Therefore, the ideal Data Entry Specialist will possess professional communication skills, quick typing skills, an analytical mind, exceptional attention to detail and a passion for precision. Responsibilities: Support of the Inside Territory Sales Managers. Key all Sales Orders into Oracle. Receive payments. Communicate with customers via phone calls and emails regarding their orders, deliveries and pick ups. CRM input in accordance to the CRM Process Guidelines as necessary. Adhere to the companys values and delivering the Whirlwind brand promise and Core Values. Qualifications: Oracle experience. Outstanding attention to detail and accuracy. Professional communication skills including both written and verbal form. Excellent time management skills, ability to focus on high payoff sales activities . Strong work ethic. High School Diploma. Two or more years in a similar fast paced position.
