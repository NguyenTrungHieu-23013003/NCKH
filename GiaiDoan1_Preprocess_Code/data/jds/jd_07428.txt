Position Summary An Enterprise Architect provides technical expertise in developing architectural frameworks for enterprise applications. Essential Duties Ensures the optimum design, quality, performance, integrity, and reliability of relational database applications through the application of data modeling principles, practices and software tools. Researches and suggests new technologies to improve application performance. Provides architectural support for application development, enhancement and migrations. Reviews and recommends improvements to existing architectures. Provides guidance to other Architects in their assigned job responsibilities as needed. Qualifications & Experience 10+ years' IT experience. 2+ years supervising formal or informal matrixed program team members preferred. 3+ years leading solution design workstreams for large/complex application integration projects. 3+ years supporting enterprise cloud applications or infrastructure. Demonstrated knowledge of open enterprise architecture frameworks. Demonstrated project management knowledge. Demonstrated knowledge of Agile methodology. Education Bachelor’s Degree preferred.
