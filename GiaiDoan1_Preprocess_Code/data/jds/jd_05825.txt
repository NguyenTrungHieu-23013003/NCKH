What You’ll be Responsible For in this Role In this role you will be responsible for providing service for the Customer to oversee all aspects of communication with the company. Speak with Customers by phone and receives orders, quote requests, follow-up, and all other inquiries. Be responsible for data entry of all orders for the Customer through direct contact, mail, fax, or e-mail through the ONA Sales Representatives. While you are processing orders to bring product onto our warehouse floors, the Customer Service Representative evaluates historical data on such items and provide assistance with inventory control processes. Provide inside support to outside Sales Reps. Provide expertise in solving Customer problems with any, and all internal departments, including other divisions. Perform contract review work processes on Customer Purchase Orders. Respond to Customer inquiries on the telephone through on-line networks, by mail, and fax. Write Customer quote requests, sample requests, formal quotes to Customers, and Sales Representative leads for prospective accounts. Handle Customer problems that appear to arise from the use of the product. Handle requests for replacing and picking up defective parts. Submit follow-ups on orders through Expeditor via hand written or computer request.
