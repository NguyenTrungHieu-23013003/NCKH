About This Position The CASB Solutions Architect plays a critical role within the McAfee Security Innovation Alliance (SIA) partnering program, working alongside the business development team to identify, develop and manage key technology partnerships that will be fulfill the “Better Together” vision of McAfee using CASB Connect technologies. He or she would blend technology expertise, product management, program management and professional interpersonal relationship skills to spearhead the delivery of API-based integrated solutions. He or she would also act as a liaison between SIA partners and McAfee CASB product management and engineering teams. About the Organization The McAfee Security Innovation Alliance (SIA) is a technology partnering program that: Accelerates the development of interoperable security products Simplifies the integration of these products with complex customer environments Delivers solutions to maximize the value of existing customer investments, reduce time to problem resolution, and lower operational costs Central to McAfee Security’s relationship with each SIA partner is at least one customer use case. Use cases are driven by the needs of mutual customers and are architected around integration options available through the CASB Connect API guide. Use cases improve threat visibility, and create powerful new security behaviors and workflows Responsibilities The CASB Solutions Architect will be responsible for guiding partners through recruitment, development, delivery and GTM strategies for their integrations with McAfee CASB Connect APIs. He or she is expected to understand the current product offering set and deployment models as well as the road map features of future product releases of McAfee Security products.
