Product Placement (Merchandising) - Full Time Job Summary: The Product Placement (Merchandising) Team Member is an integral part of the Living Spaces Brand experience. This position performs a number of job functions that are critical to offering our customers the best experience when they shop in our store. This position reports to the Brand Manager. Job Responsibilities Support retail store operations in reaching goals by providing superior customer service and assisting with operational activities Support our brand with the movement and display of furniture and accessories throughout the store Work with visual team to ensure products are displayed according to Plan-o-gram Ensure products are unloaded, received and set for special promotions and events within the store Work within a Merchandising team to set floor displays Initiate contact with guests to make them feel welcome, by assessing their needs and providing courteous, helpful and knowledgeable service Process and track customer pick up, and incoming and outgoing product transfers Provides warehouse support, stocking and inventory functions Print new POP and update product Follow loss prevention and safety procedures Prior retail, warehouse or customer service experience preferred Demonstrate a strong desire to succeed Excellent attention to detail Must complete in-house lift certification & warehouse equipment certification Job Requirements High school diploma or equivalent required, Associate degree or higher a plus 2 years’ experience in merchandising/warehousing and/or a retail environment preferred Follow direction from management team on floor displays Ability to lift up to 100 lbs. Loading and unloading of merchandise Ability to climb 6 foot ladder Balances team and individual responsibilities Effective planning, organizational, and time management skills Basic knowledge of computers, data entry experience & mathematical skills Excellent attention to detail Must be self-motivated and able to complete many tasks with minimal supervision Respond to guest requests for service and assistance; meets commitments Able to pass a background check and pre-employment drug test Stellar communication skills and ability to engage guests in a relaxed yet focused manner. Ability to work flexible retail hours which include weekend, evenings and most holidays.
