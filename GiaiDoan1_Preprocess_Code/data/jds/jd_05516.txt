Information Security Data Engineer - 256615 Federal Reserve Bank of Chicago Primary Location IL-Chicago Full-time / Part-time Full-time Employee Status Regular Overtime Status Exempt Job Type Experienced Travel No Shift Day Job Job Sensitivity Tier II - No Credit Check Job Summary This role provides operational and technical support for the logging platform infrastructure and is responsible for ensuring its availability, performance, recoverability, and security. The complexity of work is considered intermediate and individuals work under moderate supervision. This role has no direct reports but does assist in training junior staff.
