Our Houston Area Client is a rapidly growing specialty electronics design and integration company focusing on high-end residential projects. In particular they specialize in the design, installation and maintenance of distributed video & audio systems, private theaters, media rooms, lighting, shading and environmental controls as well as security. Responsibilities The Technology Specialist (TS) is responsible for transforming the sales activities of the Company President into project scope, design and proposal. This position will serve as a liaison between sales and production upon creation of a proposed project. Additionally this position will provide seamless communication between the President, Clients, Industry Partners and the Production Team. Qualifications A Minimum of 3 years’ experience in the design and installation of audio/video and residential electronic systems projects. Ability to conceptualize technical component interactions and installation environments to ensure a functional design prior to physical installation. Advanced computer skills, including proficiency in the use of diagrammatic software tools (such as AutoCAD, Revit and Visio, etc.) with additional knowledge in D-Tools and Excel. Ability to read and understand technical specifications and architectural blueprints. Knowledge of the construction and design/build industry, including contract structures (time and materials, fixed fee, cost plus, etc.), trade coordination, and project management.
