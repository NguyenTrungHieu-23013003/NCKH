The Manager Enterprise Data Architecture (EDA) will lead the development of TAMKO’s data strategy, creating a sustainable information environment while ensuring reduction of risk exposure and improving quality, retention, archival and deletion strategies across the enterprise. The EDA is responsible for establishing and promoting the company’s Enterprise Information Architecture (EIA), including governance and definition of data entities and their relationships, depiction of the enterprise information supply/value chain (where and how information is created, updated, stored, distributed, used, deleted or archived), conceptual and logical data models, records retention & archiving, and other related responsibilities. This individual provides the architectural vision, thought leadership, planning and oversight needed to define the company’s data strategy and EIA, and to implement them in meaningful, tangible ways, including but not limited to the implementation of an appropriately defined Enterprise Data Model, Retention & Archival strategy, and governance ensuring integrity across the enterprise.
