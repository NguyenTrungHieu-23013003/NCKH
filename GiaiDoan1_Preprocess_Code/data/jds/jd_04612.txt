The Business Intelligence Analyst (Enrollment Management) will provide actionable business intelligence and predictive modeling, focusing primarily in student recruitment and enrollment management. The candidate will use statistical software and business intelligence tools to design, develop, and maintain models and reports from various data sources and articulate findings to non-technical university administration. Required Skills BA/BS, experience in enrollment management forecasting and reporting, and the ability to communicate with both technical and business staff. The candidate must have a strong desire to learn new skills and systems, a willingness to adapt to change, and strong problem solving skills. Knowledge in institutional research, admissions, financial aid, and other higher education areas strongly preferred. Familiarity with statistical software (SPSS, SAS, R), statistical analysis and predictive modeling, BI tools (Microstrategy, PowerBI, Tableau), and database and data warehousing concepts and querying (SQL) preferred.
