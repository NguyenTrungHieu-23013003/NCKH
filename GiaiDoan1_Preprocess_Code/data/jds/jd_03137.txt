Overview The Customer Success mentality is all centered on helping new and existing clients derive the most value from your product/service as possible. This in turn reduces termination risks, and provides opportunities for account growth and strengthening. At LawLogix, as a new department initiative, we are quickly making progress in this goal and our clients love the extra proactive attention they are obtaining from us. We are seeking to expand this initiative further and are looking for a qualified candidate to come help elevate our customer’s success. As a Customer Success Manager, it will be your responsibility to own the client journey post-implementation which will include: triaging client issues, maintaining strong partner relationships, ensuring product adoption and stability, reducing churn risks and working to create customer growth opportunities. The CSM is someone who is passionate about working with clients, an effective multi-tasker, a champion problem solver, a trusted client advisor, and someone who can exercise sound business judgement. You will work cross functionally with various departments to remove roadblocks and solve strategic and tactical issues impacting customer’s health.
