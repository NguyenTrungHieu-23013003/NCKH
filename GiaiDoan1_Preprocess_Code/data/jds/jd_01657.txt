Receives general supervision from a Program Manager and provides clear and precise directions to persons who contact the office either by telephone or in person. The Intake Clerk is the initial contact person for the Office of Social Services. Schedules appointments for central and satellite offices. Receives visitors and/or guests to the Jensen Office. Registers clients as they arrive for services. Assembles any written information given by the clients for use by the Eligibility Specialists. Provides information and referrals to clients. Enters information regarding client information into the computer. Handles all telephone calls. Files information. Light typing. Contacts utility companies as to the status of the bills presented by clients seeking assistance. Compiles statistics utilizing basic computer skills. Performs other job duties as assigned. High school diploma or G.E.D. Excellent customer service experience with good and pleasant telephone voice. Good communication skills - oral and written. Basic computer skills with some familiarity with filing systems. Courteous, neat in appearance, possess good organizational skills. Ability to handle multiple job duties. Valid driver's license and liability insurance. General Information The work is performed in an office setting requiring no special safety precautions.
