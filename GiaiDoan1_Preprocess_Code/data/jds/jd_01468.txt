This work will be challenging, fast-paced, and competitive. Your interest and drive to apply cutting-edge machine learning techniques to large financial data sets will enable this team to expand quantitative research at SIG. What we're looking for Advanced degree in Computer Science, Statistics, Machine Learning, Applied Mathematics or related field.
