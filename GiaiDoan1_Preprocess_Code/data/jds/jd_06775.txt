Job Description: The Implementation Consultant manages the implementation and configuration of Learning and Talent Management Systems. The Implementation Consultant ensures that all activities are carried out in an efficient, cost-effective manner. The Implementation Consultant acts as a bridge between all departments within HCM, including Sales, Customer Support, and Partners. This person serves as an internal subject matter expert and supports all company activities including but not limited to system implementations, integrations, and process consultations. The Implementation Consultant reports to the Practice Manager. Key Responsibilities: Business Process Primarily manages large, strategic accounts Perform business requirements analysis and design for specific modules Identify opportunities for improvement in processes Perform gap analysis between product functionality and client’s requirements Identify and recommend product customizations, enhancements, or work-arounds to meet client requirements Participate in creating project specific documentation for focus area(s). Work with Methodology team when new document needs arise Author project documentation Document functional requirements Resolves issues by obtaining and coordinating reaction among service areas and escalating as necessary Identify and QA project efforts to ensure project decision/deliverables are sound; Will QA, at a granular level, all medium and large size projects QA project documentation authored by Senior Consultants and Consultants Lead project teams through design workshops and pilot environments Tap into at internal (Team Member) and external resources to address decisions Make and communicate design/configuration/process decisions on projects and research specific topics/issues when asked Customer Seeks opportunities to increase customer satisfaction and build client relationships Lead client meetings or facilitated strategy sessions Train users on new processes and product Develop in-depth knowledge and understanding of all aspects of a client's general business and will be SME in Talent Management Clouds or other area of industry.
