Cigna's Digital Services organization proactively collects digital analytics and customer feedback in an effort to continually upgrade our customer's digital experience in web and mobile applications. The Digital Analytics team within Digital Services provides actionable insights to internal and external business partners and customers that help increase task completion and transactional Net Promoter Score on our digital properties, reduce health costs, improve outcomes, and measure and forecast business performance. This is a hands-on individual contributor role responsible for the end-to-end design, development & deployment of global analytics solutions for assigned projects. The Digital Analytics Consultant collaborates with team members, technical delivery partners, systems analysts, and matrix partners to evaluate analytical needs, facilitate requirements, design and develop analytical capabilities, and reporting.
