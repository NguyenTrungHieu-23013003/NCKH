General Description Delaware County Community College strongly encourages applications from members of traditionally under-represented groups. DCCC promotes an organizational culture and structure that honors diversity through integration of the principles of access, inclusion and most importantly equality. Delaware County Community College is seeking adjunct instructors to teach day or evening classes in the Advanced Technology Area at both the Main Campus in Media and Pennocks Bridge Campus. Duties The successful candidates are required to teach classes consisting of both lecture (theory) & lab (practical applications). Courses of high importance are the following: Machine Tool Technology (MTT): - Introduction to Manufacturing Manufacturing Processes Print Layout & Measurement for Machining (with focus on GD&T;) Technology Dept. Core (TCC): - Construction Blueprint Reading Electronics Technology (TEL): - DC Analysis The various fields of interest, course descriptions and competencies are available online at http://www.dccc.edu/academics/academic- divisions/workforce-development-and-community-education Minimum Qualifications Candidate need to have at least 3 years of practical experience in the related field. They must have a Bachelor's degree in at least one of the following areas: Applied Technology, Electronics Technology, Electrical Engineering, Manufacturing, Mechanical Engineering, or Industrial Engineering DCCC is an equal opportunity employer. Supplemental Information Pennsylvania Act 153 Clearances Applicants are required to either present to human resources current and valid PA Act 153 clearances or be able to obtain them expediently.
