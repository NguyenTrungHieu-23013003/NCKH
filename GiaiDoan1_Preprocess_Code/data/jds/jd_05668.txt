BIG RED DOG places great importance on training and imparting knowledge to the next generation of engineers. BRD selects current college students to work in offices across the state and in varying market sectors and directly with Project Engineers and Graduate Engineers. These interns are exposed to every stage of a project and given hands on opportunities to be a part of the success of BIG RED DOG. Responsibilities \---------------- Our interns take on similar tasks and responsibilities as full-time entry-level staff. We provide planning and engineering services associated with all modes of transportation. We are a team of entrepreneurial individuals empowered to collaborate, innovate, and develop creative solutions that improve communities. Transportation interns will work closely with more senior staff to learn the basics of advanced transportation planning and traffic engineering practices. Interns aid with data collection, roadway and intersection analysis, multi-modal studies, and creation of reports and graphics. Education/Experience \-------------------- All applicants must be current students in good standing with their university. Interns with BIG RED DOG work between 15 and 29 hours per week.
