A Day in the Life of a Business Analyst: The Business Analyst position is primarily responsible for requirements gathering, requirements analysis, impact assessment, and creation of Business Requirements documents and Functional Specifications for system development projects of various size and complexity. The Business Requirement documents are at a detailed level, including related functional requirements, use cases, testing considerations and functional solution design. This position collaborates with Business Leads and subject matter experts, Delivery Leads, Architects, vendors, and technical resources on a regular basis throughout the project delivery lifecycle. The Business Analyst 2 is responsible for adhering to corporate software development standards and methodologies. Secondary responsibilities include creation and maintenance of various types of documents, testing support, issue investigation, analysis, and resolution, and assisting in responding to new corporate initiatives and work requests as needed.
