The AIDS/LifeCycle (ALC) Technical Operations Manager is responsible for the data and technical needs of the ALC participants and staff. This support includes, but is not limited to, the CRM system, the databases, the website and the mobile applications. Responsibilities include managing and analyzing data, reporting, web development, project management and troubleshooting problems. This position contributes to ALC by allowing staff to better recruit, retain and engage participants for ALC, ultimately bringing in revenue for both the Center and the Foundation to fund programs and services. ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS: 1) Configure and maintain the Luminate Online/Luminate CRM databases to track registrations, donations, and encounters/inquiries with stakeholders across a variety of platforms; 2) Responsible for uploading and maintenance of all ALC constituent records, including personal information and interaction/involvement history; 3) Provide staff training and second tier support for Luminate Online/Team Raiser technical support issues for participants; 4) Track distribution of ALC incentives and participant initiatives; 5) Develop metrics and provide regular activity reports on participant performance to the leadership team; 6) Develop methodologies for forecasting the number of participants who will be participating in the event; 7) Maintain and updated aidslifecycle.org; 8) Implement changes and troubleshoot issues with website; 9) Maintain and troubleshoot issue with mobile applications; 10) Develop strategic plan for future website redesign; 11) Develop project requirements, organize project definitions, planning strategies, and timelines for new technical initiatives; 12) Manage and control various projects, including driving stakeholders, tracking progress, and ensuring timely completion of existing technical projects; 13) Manage outside contractors and vendors in regard to technical initiatives; 14) Establish electronic communication systems for accurate delivery of all electronic communications from ALC; 15) Work with leadership and staff on targeting email messaging to improve participant performance and overall satisfaction with the event; 16) Provide technical orientation to new staff members and ongoing technical training and support to the staff and key audiences of ALC; 17) Act as the key technical contact between ALC and the technical vendors;
