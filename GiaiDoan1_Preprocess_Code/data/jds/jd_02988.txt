A Day in the Life of a Manager of Utilization Management - Operations and Excellence: This position is responsible for managing the day-to-day operational aspects of the Operations and Excellence program within the Corporate Utilization Management Department. The Operations and Excellence department includes the UM Analyst, UM Audit Program, and Clinical Excellence functions. Responsibilities Provide direct oversight to the UM Analysts in completing all UM reporting and analysis, system testing and release implementation, project management, process improvement, policy and procedure development, committee preparation and facilitation Ensure the UM Analysts have effective and collaborative relationships with UM leadership as well as the Data Analytics, IT, Training, Compliance and Operations departments and are well versed on all state, federal, contractual, and accreditation requirements Provide direct oversight of the Audit Program which will include completion of quality and process audits for all UM roles responsible for Utilization Management functions Liaison with all UM leadership on their team audit performance and assist the UM audit team with providing education and support to UM family members and leadership to correct non-compliant audit scores Liaison with the Compliance departments to ensure the UM audit program is compliant with their practices as well as state, federal, contractual, and accreditation requirements Assists the UM Director with ensuring that all Utilization Management policies and procedures are updated at least quarterly, new policies are developed as needed and that all Utilization Management staff are appropriately informed/trained in updates and are compliant with Medicaid regulatory and accrediting requirements Assists the UM Director to ensure Utilization Management training initiatives are directed at meeting program goals and competency assessment is performed and compliant with Medicaid requirements Prepare monthly and quarterly utilization management reports for quarterly Utilization Management Committee (UMC) and the Board of Directors Conduct review of services that prior authorization at least annually and offer recommendations to UMC Directly manage assigned staff including but not limited to hiring, scheduling, monitoring workload, providing project oversight, conducting performance evaluations, facilitating transitions and implementing disciplinary action, when necessary Collaborate with MHP Care Coordination to fully integrate UM and CC functions and support seamless service coordination for members Provide in-depth learning experiences, ongoing mentorship and targeted training across a range of business areas within the company Contribute towards developing departmental goals, initiatives and strategies Assist in achieving operational objectives by contributing information and recommendations towards projects, preparing and completing action plans; implementing production, productivity, quality, and customer-service standards; resolving problems; completing audits; identifying trends; determining system improvements and implementing change Provide ongoing individual support to develop talent, motivate success and prepare staff for future roles within the company Promote the use of problem solving and data analysis techniques for process improvements throughout all operational processes Ensure compliance with regulatory and quality accreditation requirements for maintenance of company policies and procedures Prepare and/or oversee preparation of department presentations and other training materials Consistently demonstrate compliance with HIPAA regulations, professional conduct and ethical practice Promote an image of
