The Network Systems Analyst I assists with the administration, installation, configuration, and troubleshooting of the NYCM Insurance enterprise technology environment. This role ensures uptime requirements are met for all critical infrastructure and applications and identifies, researches, and proposes solutions which facilitate the needs of our employees and customers. Duties & Responsibilities: Plans the vision for the basic platforms they work on. Researches and reports on basic platform and moderate platform features. Perform basic system installs and upgrades. Lead moderate system installs and upgrades. Work with vendors regularly and open tickets on supported platform. Use, research and create documentation for basic platforms and moderate platforms. Team lead on basic platform design and team member on moderate platform design. Perform, research and report on basic platforms. Understand and help define security posture for basic platforms and moderate platforms with guidance. Set up and configure basic and moderate platforms. Respond timely to service calls and installation requests. Completion of all paperwork in follow up to service calls. Attend meetings and seminars as needed. Requirements: Associates degree in a Computer Technical field, or commensurate experience. Strong customer service background. 3-4 years of experience preferred. Must have a moderate understanding of the Server and/or Voice/Data Network infrastructure as well as a moderate knowledge of selected IT/Business processes and applications. Ability to research, test and implement non-standard requests from end users. Moderate problem-solving skills. Ability to multitask. Payband: 6 Salary Range: $41,098 - $78,416
