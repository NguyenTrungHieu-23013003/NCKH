Description PROGRAM ADMINISTRATOR - DATA ENTRY Performs variety of activities in support of Program Management. Gathers, collects, records, tracks and verifies data and information from multiple sources. Compiles, reviews and analyzes data. Uses software for functional area to compile and generate reports, statistics, timelines, tables, graphs, correspondence and presentations. May design processes to enhance work flow. Provides data and information to others on functional unit processes and procedures. Assist Program Managers with purchase order administration (receive, enter, log, and distribute) Assist Program Managers with various administrative tasks including securing shipping manifests, entering data onto customer portals, and updating and distributing daily metrics Coordinate program calendar events and customer visits with team and Program Management and facilitate events and visits Prepare memos, presentations, and reports under the direction of the Program Manager Coordinate program meetings, to include notifying attendees, scheduling the room, and preparing/distributing minutes May perform purchase order review for valid terms and conditions, price and lead time validation, ship to address, special instructions, verifies correct SART routing and aircraft codes, etc. May provide customer support including updating customer reports, informing of issues, coordinating PS changes, etc. May perform functions for creating and routing travelers to identify material needs Communicates frequently with Program Manager and team for guidance and work objectives
