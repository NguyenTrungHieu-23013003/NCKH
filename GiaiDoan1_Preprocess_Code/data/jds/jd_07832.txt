Cloud Architect - Customer-Facing Infrastructure Bose is reinventing the way we interact with our customers by employing data and cutting edge technologies to better understand them and deliver relevant personalized in-the-moment experiences. We are defining and building out a cloud-native globally-scalable platform architecture and infrastructure to enable customer-centric solutions across many digital touchpoints. We are looking for a top notch Cloud Architect to come and join our digital architecture team. In this role you will be responsible for the architecture of our customer engagement infrastructure and related DevOps methods. You will research and evaluate new cloud technologies and solutions, make informed technology investment recommendations, and propose and develop infrastructure solutions based on leading-edge cloud technologies and service offerings. The ideal candidate is experienced and very comfortable using open source and commercial technologies and frameworks to build and maintain a large-scale web infrastructure serving customers across the globe. The candidate must be extremely results driven, customer focused, up-to-date and hands-on with current technologies and trends, and skilled at working in an agile highly collaborative environment.
