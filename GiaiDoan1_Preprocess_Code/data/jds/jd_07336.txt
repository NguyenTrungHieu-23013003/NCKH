The Systems Administrator III will plan, implement, maintain, backup, and ensure the availability of the company's various computer systems. In addition, this position contributes to or manages infrastructure projects and provides backup to other infrastructure personnel as needed. The Systems Administrator III actively participates in steering operational practices and knowledge sharing with the rest of the Infrastructure team. The Systems Administrator III does maintenance and system administration to include maintaining server operating systems and hosted services; applying necessary patches and upgrades on a regular basis; preventing unplanned downtime of hosted services; ensuring user access to services; ensuring complete and reliable backups of services are completed according to policy; monitor / audit systems logs; participation in 24/7 on-call rotation. The Systems Administrator III also handles infrastructure projects and provides 3rd tier Helpdesk Support.
