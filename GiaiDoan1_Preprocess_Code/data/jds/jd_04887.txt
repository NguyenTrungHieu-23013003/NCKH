Job Description Job Description/Overview: The Data Analyst role resides within the Ford GDIA organization supporting data operations and the OneFord Plan for profitable growth. In this role, you will support data requirements of the Data Scientists and Data Analysts who are using data and analytics to achieve business outcomes providing insights to accelerate customer, vehicle and application initiatives. This position will help bring data to life to solve business problems and provide data support for enterprise to include data integration tasks, such as, ingestion, standardization, enrichment, mastering and assembly of data products for downstream applications.
