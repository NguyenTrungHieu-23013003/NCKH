Business Intelligence Reporting Analyst, VP Purpose of Role: The mission of the Global Reporting Team within Finance & Risk Infrastructure & Business Solutions (FRIBS) is to support the strategic objectives of Finance, Treasury, Credit Risk, and Global Markets by enabling innovative data analytics and reporting solutions. The Global Reporting team, in support of a data-driven culture across the organization, is tasked with developing methods and controls to ensure the consistent application and use of data empowering our business partners by publishing controlled and consumable data via Spotfire, Cognos, and TIBCO Data Virtualization (TDV). Providing expertise in the areas of Business Intelligence and Data Warehouse tools and utilization, best practices, and business process management, the Global Reporting team will lead the development, design and execution of reporting and analytical solutions defining goals and objectives, meeting key project deadlines, and ensuring results are aligned with the overall business strategy. Working closely with business partners, the Global Reporting team will bridge the gap between technology and analytics by establishing and driving a long-term BI strategy and roadmap, managing an increasing demand for advanced analytics, and delivering interactive, robust data solutions through a self-service model. The ideal candidate will contribute to the execution of the Finance & Risk Infrastructure & Business Solutions (FRIBS) roadmap working directly with our business partners to understand their current data challenges, eliciting their requirements to define, scope, and deliver solutions that meet their business intelligence needs. Responsibilities: Design and implement reporting framework needed to support report development and delivery that is responsive to multiple cross organizational stakeholders/downstream constituent groups. Participate in cross organizational working groups in order to understand and prioritize reporting deliverables by adhering to a robust demand management process. Implement, and provide training on, query tools to enable the development of the reporting team’s analysis capabilities, automation of data quality metrics and enablement of ad- hoc analysis.
