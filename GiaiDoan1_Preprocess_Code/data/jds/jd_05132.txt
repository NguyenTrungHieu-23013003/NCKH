Manage National Cancer Database (NCDB) data quality by evaluating quality of existing and incoming data, recommending changes in data collection procedures and registrar training, working with outside organizations to assure quality and continuity of data requirements, and assisting with Cancer Programs training activities. This Exempt position is located in Chicago, Illinois, and will report to the Senior Manager, NCDB in the Division of Research and Optimal Patient Care. Primary Responsibilities: Administers NCDB Quality Assurance Program: plans strategies to facilitate data quality management and continuous quality improvement. Plans and carries out data quality projects using computer software programs to analyze data. Uses statistical approaches to achieve quality improvement. Represents NCDB and CoC on registry data related inter-organizational committees and work groups (NAACCR, AJCC, ACS and others). Maintains the NCDB Standards for Oncology Registry Entry (STORE) coding manual, CoC edits and any aspect of NCDB data submission technology, including customer support. Manages agile data collection to support needs of NCDB and related stakeholders across Cancer Programs (CoC and specialty accreditation, AJCC, research, quality measures). Acts as an internal resource to NCDB and Cancer Programs staff regarding data quality, standards, and accreditation requirements. Collaborates as subject matter expert in projects regarding research, quality measures, and education for registrar needs and training sessions. Manage User Support Specialist staff. Other duties as assigned.
