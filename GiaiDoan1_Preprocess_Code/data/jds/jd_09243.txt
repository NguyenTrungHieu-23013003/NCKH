Auto req ID: 152610BR Job Description The Global Security Analytics and Insights Analyst is a role in the Risk & Performance Management Reporting team, within the Information Security organization, to assist in the sustainment and improvement of the security metrics program. The candidate will support PepsiCo’s security agenda by providing risk based insights for varied global audiences ranging from operational leaders to PepsiCo’s Board of Directors. The primary objective of this role is to support critical functions across the Information Security organization by developing key deliverables including, but not limited to security white papers, executive reports, process documentation, and dashboard insights based on security data analysis and analytics. The candidate will: Contribute to the execution and improvement of the security metrics program Be responsible for collaborating with internal stakeholders and data providers to develop data-driven insights Be responsible for ensuring complete, accurate, and timely reporting of analytics and insights to stakeholders Perform analysis, identify, and effectively communicate information security risks to leadership through key deliverables Develop information applying strong writing skills with consideration to clarity, grammar, spelling, consistency, presentation, and logical organization Be responsible for documenting best practices and processes for the security metrics program Be responsible for documenting requirements for new reporting, and procedural documents as needed Qualifications/Requirements Education: Bachelor's degree in Statistics, Mathematics, Computer Science, or related discipline Experience: Experience in executing the full research lifecycle from data collection and normalization, to insight analysis, writing/documenting insights, editing, and document delivery Experience with identifying, analyzing, and reporting on value-added key risk indicators and key performance indicators related to information security, cybersecurity or broader IT 5+ years of experience and proven leadership in identifying, reporting, and communicating analytic results, and methods of research 3+ years of experience in the use of reporting and business intelligence solutions to drive insights, such as Tableau Working knowledge of information security industry frameworks (e.g. ISO, NIST, COBIT) 3+ years of experience writing technical procedural documents and/or security white papers using visualizations to explain security risk Experience preparing executive-level communication An ideal candidate is: A self-driven and motivated individual with proven ability to communicate risks and insights identified through a security metrics program involving a multitude of areas and stakeholders A strong writer, able to clearly articulate complex security concepts to leadership, and to stakeholders outside of Information Security Able to present effectively using multiple reporting and presentation tools such as Microsoft PowerPoint, and Tableau dashboards Able to derive meaningful and risk based insights from data, and to identify trends in the threat landscape Able to edit information to conform to style guidelines, express ideas clearly and succinctly, and communicate information visually Knowledgeable in the Information Security domain Able to understand information security risks and determine ways to identify measures that will accurately reflect the current degree of risk Any technical writing certifications are preferred
