The Space Data Manager (SDM) manages the client’s Space Data Management (SDM) Program, and associated portfolio reporting within the framework of the client’s real estate programs and strategy. He/she will interact with the client’s internal CRE team and apply expertise in CAFM administration, data governance, data analytics and reporting to identify portfolio metric improvement opportunities. This role can be located in a virtual office. Occupancy Planning at JLL features over 400 professionals engaged in creating space solutions, optimizing space utilization, and achieving cost savings for 90+ clients and 575 million SF of real estate worldwide. Essential Duties and Responsibilities Accountable for the results and performance of the space data management team and service delivery on the account, including KPI results Accountable for space data integrity and accuracy within a CAFM database, and support of dashboards and analytics, by driving use of drawing and data standards, and review and remediation of data errors Acts as single point of contact on space data management and CAFM issues for the client’s CRE management team and JLL account management Manages relationship with JLL Technology Solutions team to enhance performance, manage application issues and ensure maximum “up time” of the CAFM System Serves as Subject Matter Expert for all proposed process related changes concerning Space Data Management and the CAFM system Participates in the client’s data governance program to define, categorize, measure, and audit client space data Demonstrates understanding of the client’s regional portfolio composition, including location and interaction of organizations, site type/use (call center, agent office, administration), and corresponding space metrics (vacancy, density, etc.) Manages reporting on the demand planning program to compare forecasted demand for space against supply of space over one to three years Reports to client CRE team on regional occupancy, vacancy and utilization metrics Ensures regional consistency in delivery of JLL services by providing training, following standard operations manual, and incorporating evolving best practices into the account team’s work product Accountable for the health of the client engagement Required Knowledge, Skills and Abilities 2 to 4 years work experience in CAFM/IWMS administration, occupancy or space planning in a corporate environment; or move/project management Demonstrated knowledge of office space categorization principles (BOMA, OSCRE, etc.), CAFM procedures and AutoCAD Moderate experience in CAFM/IWMS database management and design (i.e. FMS, Manhattan, Tririga, Archibus, or similar) Demonstrated abilities in analytics, data visualization, and trend reporting using PowerPivot, Tableau, etc. Advanced proficiency in MS Office (Excel, PowerPoint, and Word) with the capability to master client specific programs Self-motivated, able to work independently and within a team to build relationships and interact effectively with business partners Excellent written and verbal communication skills;
