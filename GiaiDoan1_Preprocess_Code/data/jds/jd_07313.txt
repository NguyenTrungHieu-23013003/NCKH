Job Description Linux Systems Administrators work with other members of the Information Technology department to support all aspects of the Systems Infrastructure. The Linux Systems Administrator will also support internal Development staff with installation, configuration, and support of applications. Candidates must be highly motivated and will be required to respond to and provide resolutions to problems on a timely basis. This position involves the planning, configuring, managing, monitoring, and troubleshooting of a variety of network systems.
