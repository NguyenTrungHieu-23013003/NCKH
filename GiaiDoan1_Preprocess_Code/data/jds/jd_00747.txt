Job Title: Data & Pricing Analyst Department: IT Status: Full Time / Exempt Reports To: CIO Essential Functions: Pricing support for new business opportunities and preparing responses for quotes and requests for proposal. Maintain company-wide and customer-specific pricing programs according to business objectives. Involves working directly with sales team and support teams on pricing strategy and maintenance requests. Update and maintain promotional pricing contracts in to support marketing programs. Perform data analysis and reporting to support sales & marketing initiatives, business strategy planning and other miscellaneous requests for internal & external customers. Manage product restricted lists to support customer requirements. Assist with maintenance of ERP content including web item content, customer-product restricted lists, managed print services pricing and supply-chain routing. Create and maintain documentation for all periodic functions, with a special emphasis on monthly/quarterly procedures to support customer pricing and rebate programs. Team Work: Openly exchange ideas and opinions with co-workers and ask for help when needed. Prioritization of work load. Good attitude and communication is essential to effective team work. Minimum Qualifications: Strong working knowledge of Microsoft Excel and Microsoft Access. Experience with other Microsoft programs is also preferred. Bachelor’s Degree or equivalent business experience Ability to work independently and in a collaborative, team environment. Work well under pressure and achieve deadlines. Have keen problem-solving abilities and curiosity with data. Ability to record and work with detailed information accurately. Ability to communicate both written and orally. Must be reliable and prompt. Equal Employment Opportunity 5/2018
