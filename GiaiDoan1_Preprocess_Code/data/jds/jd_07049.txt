JOB TITLE: Procurement Systems Administrator REPORTS TO: Senior Manager, Capital Planning and Contract Management DUTY SCHEDULE: 12 Months ROLE OVERVIEW: The primary purpose of this position is to assist with procurement of goods and services to meet the needs of KIPP Texas, Inc. dba KIPP Texas Public Schools. Under general supervision, the Procurement Systems Administrator is responsible for all aspects of the configuration and maintenance of our procurement systems used to procure materials, services, and supplies. The Procurement Systems Administrator will help to solve system-processing issues, stay current on software and hardware advancements and updates, and assisting end-users, as needed, to utilize the system compliantly. This position will be primarily responsible for the administration of the statewide electronic procurement system (Jaggaer/SciQuest platform). MAJOR RESPONSIBILITIES AND DUTIES: The Procurement Systems Administrator will: Assist with procurement of products and services Assist end users with proactively identifying needs and obtaining resources Prepare and maintain procurement documentation Create and disseminate procurement reports Help to improve the effectiveness of the KIPP purchasing process via KIPPeMart, KIPP’s electronic procurement software system Administer the KIPPeMart platform Be a subject matter resource for statewide users Be a system liaison for suppliers and internal departments Provide feedback on procurement processes as it relates to systematic compliance with Board Policy, State and Federal procurement laws Manage contract documentation via the electronic procurement system Perform other job-related duties as assigned FUNCTIONAL DUTIES: The Procurement Systems Administrator will: Act as the project manager Determine the need for additional functionality and subsequent roll out to improve the electronic procurement system Complete system upgrades/updates as needed Troubleshoot and resolve system errors and integration issues Manage product & service catalogues and punch-outs Act as the documentation controller via KIPPeMart Contracts Scope of Works Vendor Contact List Bid Documents User profiles Manage the following requests New supplier inquiries Change order inquiries System updates Procurement process improvements [BW1] Perform other job-related duties as assigned QUALIFICATIONS: Education and Knowledge: Bachelor’s preferred, plus minimum of 2 years experience working in a relevant capacity Strong working knowledge of the Jaggaer/SciQuest procurement platform Purchasing certifications a plus;
