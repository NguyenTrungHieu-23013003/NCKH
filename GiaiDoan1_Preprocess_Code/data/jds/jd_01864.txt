Penn State Abington’s Science and Engineering Division seeks math and statistics instructors (non-tenure track) for a part-time appointment to begin August, 2018. The successful candidate will teach undergraduate math courses at the introductory through intermediate undergraduate level. The ideal candidate should be open to offering flexibility in course scheduling and methods of course delivery. Applicants who will have completed a PhD in Math, Statistics, Math Education, or a related field at the start of the appointment are preferred. Preference will be given to candidates with demonstrated excellence in teaching math and statistics at the college level. The review process will begin immediately and continue until the position is filled. Penn State Abington is a four-year undergraduate college of Penn State University located two blocks from Route 611 in Abington, Pennsylvania, and 10 miles north of Center City Philadelphia.
