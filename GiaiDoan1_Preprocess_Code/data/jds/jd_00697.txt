Responsibilities: Under the direction of the Assistant Director of CIS, the Health Data Analyst analyzes and extrapolates key data elements into the Core Measure database. Data accuracy and timeliness are maintained to support ongoing needs of internal quality review and process improvement, as well for reporting to outside agencies. Qualifications: 2+ years of experience reviewing medical records required. 2+ years of experience abstracting and analyzing core measures preferred. Associate of Science in Health Information Management or related field required; Bachelor of Science in Health Information Management preferred. Registered Health Information Technologist (RHIT) required or Registered Health Information Administrator (RHIA) preferred. Ability to interpret informational content of medical records. Advanced Microsoft Excel skills required. Proficient in medical terminology and clinical medicine.
