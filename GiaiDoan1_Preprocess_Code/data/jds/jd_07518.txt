Chief Architect Lab (CAL) consists of senior architects across varying technology domains related to cloud computing, and works under the Chief Architect to help define the overall architecture of Alibaba Cloud’s technology stack, including underlying data center and networking infrastructure design, our cloud operating system (Apsara), and cloud product strategies. CAL is also leading cutting-edge innovations and pathfinding for Alibaba Cloud growing in the long run. We are focusing on application-aware energy efficiency improvement and intelligent operations for cloud servers and data centers. If you are interested in cross-disciplinary research and pathfinding, and are ready to solve practical problems in real world applications, please consider joining our group. Our ideal candidate is an expert in two or three areas listed below, and should also have reasonable familiarity with the rest areas: • Server system architecture • CPU, GPU, FPGA and ASIC architecture and micro-architecture • Data center power delivery and cooling • Operating system kernel and driver • Distributed system design • Resource scheduling • Machine learning and deep learning algorithm and implementation • Power management and performance optimization Knowledge in other areas is a plus: • Low level programming language such as assembly and intrinsics • 2-3 high level programming languages such as C/C++, Java, Go, Python • Proven contribution to open source community • Paper publication on famous academic conferences or journals • Demo on industrial conferences, seminars, summits or exhibitions • Work experience in standard organizations and working groups Responsibilities include and are not limited to: • Data center and server telemetry and metrics collection and processing • Data center, server and chip PnP (Performance and Power) modeling and simulation • Machine learning or deep learning algorithm development for fault and exception analysis and isolation, performance optimization and power management. • Performance, power and thermal aware scheduling algorithm prototyping • Intelligent algorithm accelerator development and algorithm mapping • Operating system kernel optimization • Driver development • Data portal and report system development • Deep collaboration with product team on interception and enabling
