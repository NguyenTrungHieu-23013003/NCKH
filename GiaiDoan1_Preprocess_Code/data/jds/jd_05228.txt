Overview In 1897 C.C. Filson began outfitting prospectors headed for the Gold Rush with tools and apparel strong enough for the toughest spots on earth. More than 100 years later, Filson remains committed to providing pioneers of all fields around the world with unfailing goods made from the highest quality materials available. Filson recognizes that it takes a world-class team to make a world-class product. As we build on the Filson legacy of American craftsmanship, we are looking for individuals who share our dedication to innovation, excellence, and superior customer service. Manager in Training - Plant Manager The Manager in Training will be responsible for the production of (garment or luggage) to ensure the operations are meeting company standards. This role reports to Sr. Plant Manager and will assist in managing Production Supervisors as well as overseeing adherence to company policies for our sewing operators. Will work closely with the Director of Production to plan and improve production operations. This is an analytical role and requires an individual to think strategically. Responsibilities Asssist in planning production operation line capacities to ensure completion of orders are timely Employ Financial Acumen by demonstrating an understanding of how work unit’s performance contributes to the overall organization’s financial results. I.e. always looking to find efficiencies and reduce cost Manage quality by using data to identify trends and track progress Always look to improve processes and communicate expectations for quality outcomes Understand and execute company policies and procedures Create and manage line schedules according to demand to ensure business targets are met in an effective and efficient manner Assist in payroll processing Develop and coach key employees to enhance skills and prepare individuals for advancement in company Report all maintenance issues in a timely manner to ensure compliance of all safety standards.
