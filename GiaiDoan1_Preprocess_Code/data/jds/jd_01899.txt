ABOUT OUR ORGANIZATION The Investment Office of Partners Healthcare oversees a large investment portfolio for Partners HealthCare (PHS), an integrated health care system in Boston, founded in 1994 by Brigham and Women's Hospital and Massachusetts General Hospital. PHS, a non-profit organization, is the nation's leading biomedical research organization and is a principal teaching affiliate of Harvard Medical School. In addition to its two academic medical centers, the PHS organization includes community and specialty hospitals, a managed care organization, a physician network, community health centers, insurance affiliates, home health and long-term care services, and other health care entities. PHS is committed to patient care, research, teaching, and service to the community. The Investment Office manages a large and diverse global portfolio of assets including equities, fixed income, hedge funds, private equity, and real assets. SUMMARY: The Investment Office seeks highly motivated professionals to provide analytical, research, and investment process support to join our Investment Team. An analyst is a key part of the investment team that manages PHS’s externally managed investment portfolio. A successful candidate will have a demonstrated record of academic achievement, an interest in investment markets, success working in a team environment, and a strong willingness to learn to be a long-term institutional investor. An Investment Analyst is an essential part of the team responsible for the timely execution of assigned investment research, analytics, risk management, due diligence and reporting activities. An analyst works in a collaborative and flexible environment as part of a generalist team that engages in portfolio management, asset allocation, and general market and investment strategy research across all investment asset classes. This mandate challenges an Investment Analyst to be informed and thoughtful about varied investments in changing markets.
