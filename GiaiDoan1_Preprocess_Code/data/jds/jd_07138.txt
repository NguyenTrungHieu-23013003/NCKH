The senior level Business Systems Administrator is responsible for the maintenance of Savers business application systems for all company location s. The individual in this position will work in conjunction with the Savers IT Application Development and Infrastructure teams, as well as business units, to ensure Savers’ global IT capabilities and ongoing IT operation meet Savers’ business needs. Detailed Description Essential Job Functions: · Administer and maintain policies and procedures for ensuring the availability, security and integrity of business systems · Administer change control processes and maintain software release levels and system design documentation for all environments for each application · Install, upgrade and configure systems as required, often during non- business hours (some nights/weekends) · Create, modify and maintain reports as necessary · Create, modify and maintain scripts and programs · Create, modify and maintain data conversion strategies and interfaces with other software · Review, analyze and evaluate business systems and user needs · Provide support and training to end users on a variety of issues; identify, research and resolve technical problems. · Work with internal business users to gather requirements · Suggest solutions to address business requirements · Implement appropriate system and application changes · Document, configure and test applications and system interface specifications · Create and maintain system-related and auditable documentation, such as business requirements, user guides and process flows Job Requirements Required Knowledge, Skills and Abilities: · Working knowledge of installing, configuring, administering and troubleshooting Microsoft Dynamics AX (Azure), Management Reporter, FRx Forecaster, Kronos WFC, Ceridian, Microsoft CRM and IIS · Strong experience configuring, administering and troubleshooting MS SQL Server as well as developing solutions for MS SQL Server · Strong experience with relational databases and client-server systems · Strong analytic ability and “Power User” skill level with MS Access and Excel · Experience with report development against SQL databases · Demonstrated organizational and customer service skills under high stress; ability to interact with all levels within the organization · Communicate orally and in writing · Business experience, complemented by technology knowledge, and the ability to grasp business concepts quickly and translate them into technical solutions in language that is easily understood by both business and technical groups. · Work independently or as a member of the team · Identify problems and recommend solutions · Set priorities, meet deadlines and multi-task with minimal supervision · Detailed-oriented Additional Details Required Education, Training and Experience: · Bachelor’s degree in computer science, finance or a related field, or a combination of education and experience that yields the required knowledge, skills and abilities · 7+ years relevant and progressive experience in this field Physical Requirements: · Lift up to 30 pounds Savers is an Equal Opportunity Employer Savers is an eVerify Company
