Alliance Health is transforming the way Medical Care is provided. As we progress through this transformation of being available for our patients and providing care to the whole person, we are adding a Medical Data Analyst. This person will work closely with our Quality Improvement Staff, Physicians, and Care Team to ensure that quality measures are identified. Once identified, the Medical Data Analyst will create measures, test data, analyze data, and manage the performance data. Data analyzed will consist of everything from procedures, tests, diagnosis, patient compliance, and prescriptions issued. Your job is to find trends and provide us with "what we don't know" about patients and services that are taking place. Qualifications Candidates must have: Bachelors Degree and 5 years of Experience in Biostatistics, Health Care Administration, Statistics, or Health Sciences. Masters degree is preferred. 2-3 years experience in a Medical Facility analyzing data is required. Advanced Excel Experience or SAAS is required. Experience with creating Dashboards.
