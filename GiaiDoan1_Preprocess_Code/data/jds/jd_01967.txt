Reporting to the Chair of Mathematics & Statistics, the Administrative Analyst Specialist works independently to provide moderately complex administrative, budgetary, and operational support department office, faculty, staff, and students. Working independently under general supervision, displays a high level of judgment in the execution of all duties. The AA/S has overall responsibility for the management of the day-to-day operations of the Mathematics & Statistics Department. The incumbent, in collaboration with the Department Chair, will determine the annual budget, administer the budget throughout the fiscal year, and conduct the fiscal analysis and operational analysis. The AA/S serves as a liaison between the Department, the College of Science Dean's Office, Faculty Affairs, Human Resources, Office of the Provost and other departments/colleges, along with outside agencies.
