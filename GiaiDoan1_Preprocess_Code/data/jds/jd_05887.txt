Position Summary Ensure the accurate and safe handling of materials and completion of other duties required to effectively operate in a high-volume and fast paced distribution environment. Essential Position Functions and Responsibilities Responsible for performing general warehousing duties involving physical handling of product, materials, supplies and equipment Maintain orderly, clean work environment in compliance with Company safety and sanitation requirements Operate industrial power equipment in a safe manner Storing and stacking materials according to company standards Ensure compliance with regulatory and company policies and procedures Regular bending, kneeling, lifting of 60+ pounds, and climbing steps and ladders Transfers materials and items from receiving dock to bin location or other designated area by hand, hand truck, forklift or other industrial powered truck Assembles and prepares customer orders for shipping and loads the order onto trailers Assists other warehouse workers in completing tasks as requested by the Warehouse Manager and/or immediate supervisor Perform basic data entry transactions utilizing designated corporate software and hardware Accurately execute inventory functions/processes as assigned Perform other duties as assigned by the supervisor/manager Qualifications Critical/Core Competencies Excellent written and oral communication skills Ability to operate a forklift and/or order picker in a safe and efficient manner. Demonstrated aptitude for problem solving; ability to determine solutions for customers High level of organization and follow through Results oriented and able to work both independently and within a team environment Comfortable interacting with high-level leaders in across functional disciplines Regular attendance is required Ability and willingness to work overtime/flexible hours as assigned by supervisor Ability to keep all business and operations information confidential Familiarity with modern warehousing practices, methods, and theories Knowledge, Skills and Abilities Education: High School Diploma or equivalent required Experience: 1 - 3 years of general work experience required 1+ years of warehousing experience preferred Required Skills & Certifications: Ability to operate a manual / powered pallet jack or lift product Forklift certification is a plus Excellent organizational and time management skills Ability to read, write, and speak English required Ability to add, subtract, multiply, and divide in all units of measure, using whole numbers, common fractions, and decimals Preferred Skills: Analytical Problem Solving Project Management Customer Service Interpersonal Skills Teamwork Strategic Thinking Initiative Equipment and Software Requirements Proficiency using Microsoft Office Suite (Word, Excel, PowerPoint) preferred Working Conditions & Physical Demands Work inside and outside of non-heated or cooled warehouse Continual walking, standing, climbing, stooping, bending, kneeling, reaching, hearing, speaking Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.
