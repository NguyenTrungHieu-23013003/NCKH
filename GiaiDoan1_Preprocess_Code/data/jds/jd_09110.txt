In the Information Security Analyst role, you’ll collaborate on initiatives related to information security and compliance. You’ll have the opportunity to work with a progressive, forward thinking team that’s comprised of a core group of security and compliance professionals, as well as an extended network of teammates throughout the organization. You’ll work with others to identify and remediate risks “on the front lines” of a rapidly evolving SaaS offering, while at the same time helping to continuously improve and mature a security program whose mandate is to mitigate risk and demonstrate compliance at speed and at scale. Through interactions with your peers as well as professional development opportunities, you’ll be exposed to the “new normal” in application architecture and operations, including DevOps methodologies, cloud services adoption and automation of security controls. You’ll be part of a security team that strives to maintain ongoing partnerships focused on safely achieving business objectives rather than a security team that’s seen as a blocker by the rest of the organization. Specific Duties \--------------- Your primary mission will be to work with product management, engineering, infrastructure and operations teams in order to identify potential risks to the confidentiality and integrity of our customer data, as well as the security and availability of our systems. You’ll collaborate on potential risk treatment plans as well as other projects that improve the security posture of the company. You’ll also serve as a resource for others with regard to security and compliance requirements, fostering a culture of security throughout the organization. You’ll work with compliance-focused members of the team as well as engineering teammates to evaluate and continuously improve the monitoring and response mechanisms that underpin our security strategy. Responsible for the following: \------------------------------------ Coordinate and manage all aspects of vulnerability identification and remediation. To include: Network and application penetration testing Vulnerability scanning Findings surfacing as part of SDLC Emerging threats with possible exposure within the CommerceHub environment Third party software security updates Coordinate with product teams and Infrastructure to ensure that periodic and ad hoc security patching are implemented in a timely and comprehensive manner. Monitor and maintain company-hosted sources for security event information and serve as primary point of contact for outsourced capabilities Identify monitoring changes necessitated by expansion, alteration or decommissioning of systems Identify findings through manual and automated review of various logs Coordinate security incidents as per the CommerceHub Incident Response Plan Operate the Information Security Training Program. To include: Annual Security Awareness Training Security Incident Response Training Training and orientation for Security Advocates Secure Development Training for Software Engineers Internal awareness campaigns through multiple channels (blogs, online and in-person training, posters, etc.) Social Engineering Exercises Assist information security teammates who are responsible for the Vendor Risk Management Program Ensure that access to CommerceHub systems by internal personnel and third party contractors and vendors is managed effectively through monitoring of the access management process. Identify opportunities to automate and streamline security monitoring and other controls, working with technical resources to implement solutions Requirements \------------ Successful candidates for the position must meet the following requirements: Driven, polished, technology-proficient professional Hands-on experience collaborating with extended teams in a fast-paced environment Results-oriented and able to motivate others, with a demonstrated ability to create clarity and action from ambiguity and to challenge assumptions Familiarity with basic methodologies associated with vulnerability scans and penetration tests. Familiarity with enterprise cloud services security best practices, enterprise logging / monitoring platforms and mixed Windows / Linux environments Familiarity with information risk and control frameworks Education / Experience: Bachelor’s degree in an Information Assurance-related discipline, or business degree with similar focus; or 3 – 5 years’ experience working in an Information Technology or Information Assurance related field Strong multi-tasking and effective time-management skills Strong written and verbal communication skills Additionally, knowledge of and/or experience with the following is a plus: CISSP or equivalent certification Knowledge of customer technology challenges within the retail and/or e-commerce industries Experience designing and driving implementation of security controls in a SaaS / web application delivery model Project management experience
