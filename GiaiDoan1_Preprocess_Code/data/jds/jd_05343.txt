Updating HMS with information Reviewing admission papers with the patient to verify accuracy: Copy all insurance cards for business office files Request signature on proper documents, such as promissory notes, release of information, etc. Orient patients and family member or caregivers as follows: Explain all registration forms, rules and procedures to patient and or family in a manner pre-registering patient, based on information provided in the pre-admissions assessment or by family, patient, or referral source Obtaining copies of all insurance cards and calculating amount of
