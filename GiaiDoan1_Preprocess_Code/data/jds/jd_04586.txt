This role is part of the Strategic Business Operations group that has overall responsibility for providing budget and resource management, contracts and sourcing support, performance management services and internal governance to the Clinical Development (CD) organization. This position will specifically be responsible for providing support in overseeing the performance / strategy management system for CD and for providing data that will allow the organization to make more effective decisions. In doing so, they will: Assemble data and associated analysis required to enable data driven decisions by the organization, identify risks and issues, and optimize trial planning and conduct. Create performance reports that provide an understanding of the organization’s performance with respect to cost / quality / speed. Work closely with eClinical and other functions to develop and produce dashboards. Facilitate the use of historical performance data into the CD planning process. Support the use of business intelligence and benchmarking data to compare CD performance to the industry and identify opportunities for improvement. Worker Type: Employee Worker Sub Type: Intern (Fixed Term) (Trainee)
