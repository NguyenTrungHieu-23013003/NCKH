Cloud Infrastructure Operations and Engineering, Vice President Job Summary Cloud Infrastructure Operations & Engineering, Vice President is responsible for design, build, implementation, monitoring, and management of the MUFG enterprise cloud infrastructure and shared services that provide the foundation for the applications running on them. This key role will be operating across both traditional areas of infrastructure including compute, storage, networking and IaaS ensuring continuous and seamless delivery and automation of infrastructure services. He/she will be responsible for operational management with a balanced approach to quality, risk, and cost, while driving efficiency and effectiveness of the cloud platform.
