Position Summary The Marketing Analyst will support the efforts of the Marketing Department and will interface with 3rd party service providers as well as multiple departments within the company. This position reports to the Director of Gas Marketing and is located in our downtown Houston, TX office. Level and salary commensurate with background and experience. Essential Job Functions Prepare and send well connection notices to crude oil and natural gas gatherers in advance of the initial production from new wells Be the initial point of contact for the 3rd party gas gatherers assigned to you. Establish and maintain appropriate relationships with key contacts with each counterparty Prepare monthly gas price accrual for assigned gatherers Contract obligation tracking Review and understand contract language for surveillance and enforcement of provisions for assigned gatherers Run time of gas gatherers Timely well connections of oil and gas gatherers Pressure requirements Penalty enforcement Minimum volume commitment tracking Other contract provisions Interface with internal departments including Legal, Midstream, Development, Regulatory, Production as well as all Marketing teammates Prepare and maintain statistical and analytic reports for Management Support the Director and Team in ongoing contract negotiations. _This job description is not intended to be an all-inclusive list of duties and responsibilities of the position.
