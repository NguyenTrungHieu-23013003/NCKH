Position Description: Under the direction of the Quality Assurance Manager, the Assistant Quality Manager is responsible for the direction, implementation, and maintenance of the facility’s food safety and compliance programs. Primary Responsibilities: Manage all aspects of the plant HACCP and HARPC plans and programs Evaluate daily records for compliance Verify finished product conformance by analyzing raw material, work-in- process, and finished product testing and monitoring data Create, maintain, and revise food safety and quality policies and procedures Actively participate in all customer and third-party audits Identify and implement quality systems improvements based on findings from internal audits and assessments and external audits Assist Operations in addressing product and process related quality issues Perform hold and release activities, including retention of product, incident investigations, inventory verification and reconciliation, and disposition of product Assess trends from electronic compliance monitoring system and identify opportunities for improvement Provide functional support for facility projects and initiatives Serve as back-up to the Quality Assurance Manager in his/her absence Perform other duties as assigned Supervisory/Management Responsibilities: Supervise and coach hourly direct reports in the performance of assigned duties across all three shifts Monitor and process employee attendance records/timecards and PTO in a timely manner Ensure direct report’s timely completion of assigned food safety and human safety training courses Resolve personnel issues in accordance with company guidelines and policies Requirements: Bachelor’s degree in Food Science or related field or equivalent experience 1 – 3 years of food manufacturing experience 1 - 3 years of food quality management experience Proficient with Microsoft Office including database usage Able to work variable shifts and weekends as needed Working knowledge of GMP, HACCP, SSOP and USDA regulations Physical Requirements: Able to push/pull up to 50 pounds occasionally daily Able to be on feet, tolerate excessive walking (approximately 60% of time daily) Able to climb stairs and ladders and tolerate heights (approximately 5% of time daily) All qualified applicants will receive consideration for employment without regard to race, color, religion, sex, sexual orientation, gender identity, national origin, disability or veteran status.
