SRI International's Artificial Intelligence Center (AIC) is seeking scientists in the area of machine learning, data analytics, and natural language processing. The AIC is one of the world's major centers of research in artificial intelligence. Founded in 1966, the AIC has been a pioneer and a major contributor to the development of intelligent systems. Its objectives are to understand the computational principles underlying intelligence in man and machines and to develop methods for building computer-based systems to solve problems, to communicate with people, and to perceive and interact with the physical world. Because the AIC is an integral part of a nonprofit research institute, it can carry out comprehensive long-term research programs. The AIC's staff includes more than 50 computing professionals, more than half of whom have a Ph.D. or its equivalent. At the same time, the AIC provides the stimulation and creative exchange of ideas characteristic of an academic setting by maintaining associations with universities and other research groups, and by providing opportunities for students and visiting fellows from the United States and abroad to participate in ongoing projects. The Advanced Analytics group within the AIC, located in San Diego, conducts cutting-edge research in the application of statistics and machine learning to knowledge discovery from data.
