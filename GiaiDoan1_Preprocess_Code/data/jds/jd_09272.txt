This position requires an analytical, detailed-oriented professional to communicate technical concepts to business leaders, coordinate with multiple stakeholders across an organization, maintain and execute a reporting portfolio, and apply input and feedback quickly and accurately. High attention to detail with ability to synthesize information from multiple sources and distill it in crisp, clear language appropriate for a variety of audiences including technology and business executives Contribute towards process improvement of team processes, templates and tools.
