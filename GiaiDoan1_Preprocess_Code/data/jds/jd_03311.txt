Purpose and Job Summary ORIX Corporation USA is seeking a Director of Data Solutions to join the Technology Solutions team responsible leading data solution efforts for ORIX and its affiliate companies. This individual must be a superior business and data analytics professional comfortable at all levels of big data management including sourcing, vetting, analyzing, and reporting. They must also be able to ideate, design, and execute key strategic projects and lead and mange cross-functional partners and stakeholders through the execution and delivery of that project. The candidate will lead of team of data solution team members to deliver results. The ideal candidate has expertise for seeing solutions in sprawling data sets and the business mindset to convert insights into strategic opportunities for our company. The candidate will work closely with leaders across each business unit to support and implement high-quality, data-driven decisions. They will ensure data accuracy and consistent reporting by designing and creating optimal data acquisition processes and procedures. They will use advanced data modeling, predictive modeling and analytical techniques to interpret key findings from company data and leverage these insights into initiatives that will support business outcomes.
