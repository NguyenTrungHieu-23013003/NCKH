Job Description Love working at a startup where your personal effort can help drive the company to success? Looking to make the jump from a QA role to a more engineering-focused role? In this position, you’ll be right at the heart of a fast-growing company changing the way the world uses water. Data quality is at the heart of WaterSmart's products. Every day, we process and aggregate millions of new datapoints. In this role, you'll use SQL queries, Python scripts, and internal tools to monitor the flow of data into the system, proactively investigate potential data quality issues, and work directly with our Customer Success team to troubleshoot inbound data requests. Qualifications Strong SQL skills and attention to detail are a must. You'll also be working on data-related issues in Python - some previous scripting or Python experience is a big plus, but we're willing to train the right candidate! Additional Information WaterSmart seeks a committed and diverse workforce. Women, minorities, individuals with disabilities, and veterans are encouraged to apply. This is a position local to San Francisco - no remote applicants, please! All your information will be kept confidential according to EEO guidelines.
