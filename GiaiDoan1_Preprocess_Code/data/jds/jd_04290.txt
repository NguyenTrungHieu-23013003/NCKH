The Employee Engagement Specialist will develop and deliver development initiatives that directly enhance and impact employee and team capabilities for a fast paced, technology driven company. An innovative thinker, they will support projects and programs that enable our Employee Experience. This role supports all phases of the Employee Experience through strong partnerships within the Human Resources Department, cross-functional teams and employees at all levels that strive to impact process improvement and employee engagement. The ideal candidate, working closely with the business, will facilitate, design and deliver learning solutions focused on leadership development and employee engagement. This person will implement and measure initiatives that drive employee and team capabilities to enable the business to meet their organizational objectives.
