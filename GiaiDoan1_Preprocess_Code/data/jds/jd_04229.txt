Overview Jazz Pharmaceuticals is an international biopharmaceutical company focused on improving patients’ lives by identifying, developing and commercializing meaningful products that address unmet medical needs. We are continuing to expand our commercial product portfolio and our research and development pipeline in therapeutic areas that can leverage our unique expertise. Our therapeutic areas of focus include sleep and hematology/oncology – areas in which we have a deep understanding of the patient journey and a suite of products and product candidates to address critical needs. Position Profile Jazz Pharmaceuticals, a global biopharmaceutical company, was founded in 2003. Headquartered in Dublin, Ireland, our main offices are in Palo Alto, CA, Philadelphia, PA, Dublin, Ireland and Oxford, UK. We focus on improving patients’ lives by identifying, developing and commercializing meaningful products that address unmet medical needs. We are driven by one simple motivator: a genuine desire to improve patients’ lives. This is a universal truth across the entire organization, no matter the location, therapeutic focus, discipline or level of the individual. Since its creation, Jazz Pharmaceuticals has experienced strong corporate growth, and continues to build upon a dedicated development team, a focused commercial team and a seasoned management team, all working together for the betterment of patients. Our strategy is to create shareholder value by: Growing sales of the existing products in our portfolio, including by identifying new growth opportunities. Acquiring additional differentiated products that are on the market or product candidates that are in late stage development, and pursuing focused development of a pipeline of post discovery differentiated product candidates. Expecting to continue to grow both organically and through acquisitions, and other business development opportunities in the foreseeable future.
