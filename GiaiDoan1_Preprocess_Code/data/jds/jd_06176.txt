Job Description This is a senior level technical leader position for Heterogeneous Integration (HI) technology. The candidate must have 10+ years of professional R &D experience in materials, processes, and / or process integration and characterization for applications in advanced high density packaging. Responsibilities include devising new approaches to achieve higher density chip to chip interconnects, identify leading edge industry trends, assist or lead in the development of an industry leading HI R&D roadmap, lead and mentor other team members, and advise management on focus areas and / or gaps in the research agenda. Required Technical and Professional Expertise Required Skills: Expertise in one or more of the following areas required: C4 bumping / solder materials Cu pillars TSVs Other critical technology elements associated with 2.5D / 3D packaging and assembly Preferred Tech and Prof Experience Preferred Skills: Skills and current knowledge in the advanced packaging industry and supply chain, materials and equipment supplier interactions and management.
