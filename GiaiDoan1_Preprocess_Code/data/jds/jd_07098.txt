Overview The GRC Systems Administrator II will be responsible for the day-to-day activities associated with the overall administrative management of the RSA Archer eGRC platform. This will include support and maintenance of any eGRC system environment across multiple instances (i.e. development, test and production). Additional front-end development support and 3rd party system integration will also be required as additional modules are configured and deployed, or entirely new solutions are created.
