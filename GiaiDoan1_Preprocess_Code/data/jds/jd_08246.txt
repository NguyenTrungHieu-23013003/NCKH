Position Summary The Business Services Specialist (BSS) provides support to team members across multiple financial business processes, while continuously assessing areas of improvement for inefficient processes. The BSS possesses base level knowledge around all financial processes and systems and serves as the first point of contact for areas such as Accounting, Accounts Payable, Workday Operations, Payroll, Compensation and Reporting. Job Duties and Responsibilities Designs and implements training and knowledge documents utilized by Corporate and field team members Identifies and designs process improvements and communicates solutions while working cross functionally when necessary Provides continuous training and feedback to team members to reduce business process exceptions Identifies patterns in requests received through the Business Services Portal to determine areas of process improvement Acts as the subject matter expert and liaison to team members for requisition, purchase order, receiving, workday and accounting questions Performs general procurement functions such as new vendor set-up, vendor record maintenance, closing purchase orders & creating catalog items Responds to team member transactional level inquiries as a result of financial statement review with a focus on operational transactions such as expense reports and invoices Provides support for various financial reports in Workday Responds to invoice and invoice settlement inquiries Position Requirements Bachelors Degree in a related field 1 to 2 years of experience in a professional business environment Knowledge in Microsoft Office, specifically Excel Strong technical aptitude accessing multiple applications to provide support Experience working in a high volume and fast paced environment requiring the ability to multi-task Preferred Requirements Experience designing training materials Experience with ServiceNow or Workday ERP Experience in accounting, accounts payable, accounts receivable, payroll or procurement Experience in Project Management
