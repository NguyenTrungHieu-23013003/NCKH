Job Description Enterprise Data & Analytics is responsible for providing Districts and Customers with exceptional data analytics support for planning, root cause analysis and process improvement activities that accelerate Expeditors’ business. The Data Analyst position is a broad-spectrum analytics role responsible for leveraging all available data and has a wide range of responsibilities including the collection, cleansing, analysis, reporting, interpretation and presentation of data. Data Analysts have the ability to develop end-to-end data solutions. Key Responsibilities:﻿ Scope, plan, develop and deploy impactful data-driven solutions for our Districts and Customers Adhere to a defined analytics development procedure and fully document solutions Participate in project requirements gathering and solution design Complete projects on-time and provide frequent progress updates to project stakeholders Prepare, cleanse and augment data in preparation for analysis and usage Develop meaningful and intuitive reports, summaries and dashboards Provide support for data and reporting infrastructure such as scheduling and access Leverage advanced analytics tools and techniques to extract insights from data Share solutions and collaborate with other analysts in the network Manage projects, tasks, time and timelines within our standard project management tools Provide ongoing training to Districts on available reporting and self-service analytics tools Cross-train team members on new tools and analytics procedures Support our District as a subject matter expert on our data, tools and environments Ensure solutions are built to scale and use enterprise approved technologies Qualifications minimum 3-5 years or related experience Bachelor degree or higher in the area of information systems or analytics Advanced proficiency in Microsoft Excel (formulas and pivot tables) Advanced proficiency in various analytics tools (i.e.; Microsoft Excel, SAS, R, Python, SQL, Java) Advanced proficiency in various business intelligence platforms (i.e.; Inetsoft, PowerBI, Oracle BI) Ability to clearly communicate and present to small and large groups Additional Information Successful candidate must pass the company's defined background check. All your information will be kept confidential according to EEO guidelines.
