Emser Tile, LLC, the nationwide supplier of high-quality floor covering products servicing world-leading retailers, builders and contractors seeks an experienced INSIDE SALES REPRESENTATIVE- HOUSTON, TX. The Branch ISR must maintain an awareness of pricing, margin, sampling expense and all other potential controllable expenses within his / her purview, and take appropriate actions (with Branch Management approval) to enhance Branch and Company profitability. The Branch ISR, should focus on the full cycle of contractor deals and be able to flex to the counter for additional support to the Customer Support Representative.
