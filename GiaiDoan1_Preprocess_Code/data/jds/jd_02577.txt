The Warehouse Management Support Analyst will be responsible for supporting Mopar’s Warehouse Management System (WMS) – DView throughout NAFTA region, 24 by 7. Responsibilities will include monitoring weekly metrics and provide guidance to enable improvement in the metrics, troubleshooting (WMS) issues, participating in training and the creation of data related reports. This individual will also support vendors on the development and testing of new enhancements and bug fixes, which will include the creation of test and launch plan. The applicant will require expensive knowledge of SQL, understand of relational databases, strong decision-making skills and be able to interact with personnel at all levels of the organization.
