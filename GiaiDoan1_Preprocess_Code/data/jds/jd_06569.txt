The greatest ideas in the world will die on the vine if not properly implemented. At BTS, we turn our clients’ ideas and strategies into results. We do this by designing customized learning experiences that drive innovation, alignment, and performance. We work in small teams to develop business simulations and other learning solutions. Think board games with financial models or app-based, digital learning. Then we facilitate learning journeys directly with our clients in a workshop setting that incorporates humor, fun, and competition. As a BTS intern, you’ll gain invaluable educational experience through exposure to BTS clients – some of the world’s leading corporations, Global 100 and 500 companies – as you observe and help our consultants change the behavior of these organizations. You’ll learn their strategies and how their business works and shape the leadership capabilities of their senior management. With the goal of making your internship experience mirror that of a full-time BTS consultant, you’ll work on different pieces of several projects at different phases. For example, you may help create a proposal for a new project or conduct research and analysis that helps create content for the development of a project. During your internship, you’ll work side-by-side with BTSers to create these amazing learning experiences, including interviewing clients, dissecting different business models, designing and testing programs, and having a lot of fun. Our entrepreneurial culture of freedom and responsibility will empower you to contribute from your first day. Are you ready for an adventure?
