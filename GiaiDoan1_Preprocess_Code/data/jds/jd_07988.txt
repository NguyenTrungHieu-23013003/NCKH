JOB SUMMARY: Responsible for planning, directing and coordinating processes for the production of new and existing product literature that comply or exceed established industry and company standards. ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS, ROLES AND RESPONSIBILITIES: Essential duties and responsibilities include the following. Complies with all safety, health and environmental requirements, policies and procedures relevant to the position. Writes and publishes installation procedures and maintenance manuals for new product designs. Creates of top-scale quotation and presentation drawings. Produces color block library for quick assembly of color quotation drawings. Promotes and assists in the continuous improvement of products and services. Develops or reviews designs, drawings, specifications, etc. of existing or new products; ensures that assignments meet standards and performance requirements. Interprets customer requirements and applies industry and company standards to provide product design and documentation. Continually evaluates new and existing products to meet changing market and/or company requirements. Sets objectives for assigned team, manages project schedules, and ensures deadlines are met. Travels to branch locations as needed. Special projects or assignments as directed by management. EDUCATION, TRAINING, EXPERIENCE: Completion of CAD solids drafting technical training; experience in AutoCAD, SolidWorks, and InDesign. Minimum 10 years wellhead-related experience. CERTIFICATIONS, LICENSES, REGISTRATIONS: None required JOB KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS, ABILITIES: Wellhead-related equipment knowledge. Basic mechanical aptitude and knowledge. Specialized computer skills, Intermediate language and mathematical skills, and Advanced reasoning ability. Basic knowledge of Excel and Word programs. SUPERVISORY RESPONSIBILITIES: Supervises work assignments and training for technical writing quotation graphics personnel in areas of product design and usage.
