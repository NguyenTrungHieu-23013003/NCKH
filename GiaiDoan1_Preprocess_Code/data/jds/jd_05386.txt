The role entails providing Quality oversight in a Continuous Manufacturing setting to the life cycle of PLC (programmable logical controller) based automation systems, including PAT (Process Analytical Technology) Management Systems and SCADA (Supervisory Control and Data Acquisition) Systems. The role will also conduct periodic reviews, vendor audits for GxP computerized systems and provide support for internal audits and regulatory inspections. This position reports to the Associate Director of Computerized Systems Quality Assurance. Key Responsibilities: The responsibilities of this position includes, but not limited to the following: The role serves as Quality Lead for system implementation & lifecycle management of GxP impacting automation and computerized systems. The Quality Lead will be responsible for: Collaborating with Global Information Systems (GIS) technical leads, Business Areas and Other SME’s as required to perform review and approval of validation deliverables Performing impact assessments for GxP computerized systems, Executing GxP Criticality Assessment Providing Regulatory Inspection / Audit Support: Serves as Subject Matter Expert (SME) for Electronic data integrity during inspections Participates in inspection readiness and provide status updates to management Provides support for GxP regulatory inspections Provides both front and back room inspection support Participates in preparing audit responses and addressing findings. Conducting Periodic Review: Collaborates with stakeholders on Periodic Review activities for GxP Computerized Systems Performs periodic reviews activities as needed on GxP computerized systems Identifies risks/ communicates gaps to QS Owner Monitors and trends events related to their process and identifies/ proposes Corrective actions to address May serve as CAPA Owner Supporting and Executing QMS related activities: Reviews work process documents (SOPs and Work Instructions) related to business / quality workflows across all GxPs, Participates in collaborative reviews of SOPs/WIs, Review and approval of events and CAPAs associated with GxP computerized Systems. May participate on process improvement initiatives Liaising with key stakeholders; Engages with business and Global information System (GIS) to understand business processes and ensure quality/compliance expectations are integrated effectively. Conducting Audits –Electronic Data Integrity Conduct electronic data integrity audits for CxO’s that utilize Computerized Systems for GxP Operations, Custom software suppliers and cloud based service providers. Generate Reports for audits and ensure that observations are followed up and closed on time. Project Management Manage and participate in continuous improvement projects and ensure that assigned tasks are completed per schedule. May be responsible for leading projects Performing data analysis using Excel or any other tool to develop and report metrics.
