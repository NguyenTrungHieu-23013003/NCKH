The Quality Assurance team ensures we are maximizing the contributions of our people in how they engage with our clients. The Quality Assurance Manager will build a strong team and vision that aligns expectations of our client communications with the quality and procedural expectations of business partners and the Enterprise. You will provide an appropriate level of consultation within the organization to understand needs and develop a strategy in support of Customer and Operational Service’s business model and feedback from key stakeholder groups. You will also keep abreast of best-in- class and current trends in quality assurance best practices and make recommendations to improve or better align offerings with the goals of the division. The successful candidate will manage a team of Quality Assurance analysts who evaluate the quality of the communication through a subjective lens in alignment to our core customer excellence beliefs. You will ensure the team is operating in the most effective way possible and have the appropriate level of skills and capabilities to be successful. The manager will demonstrate the ability to identify, evaluate and to communicate individual and departmental performance utilizing tools such as quality assurance reviews, quality control and data analysis in addition to any functions assigned to support the overall objectives of Customer and Operational Services. This position requires the ability to collaborate positively and to build trusting and supportive partnerships within the division. Strong written and verbal communications are required with the ability to provide performance feedback in a constructive and useful manner. Diligent analysis of trend data and the ability to identify opportunities to lessen client level of effort is a critical component of the role. Responsibilities include: Oversee the team reviewing live and recorded client communications to measure the total customer experience, quality and adherence to policies and procedures. Provide quality and constructive performance feedback to agents and leadership in a business-like yet empathetic manner Use data to help drive strategy and direction Positively collaborate internally
