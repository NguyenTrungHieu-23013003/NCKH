Job Summary The Procurement Data Analyst is responsible for the maintenance of all vendor and internal business user data. Further, this role will provide ongoing maintenance/updates for all corporate catalogs and will take the lead for supplier enablement activities. The Procurement Data Analyst will be a member of the Enterprise Procurement team and will serve as the primary point of contact for vendor and internal customer support. Ariba, procure-to-pay, supply chain, data, analyst, analysis Essential Functions Maintain supplier master data, commodity tables and catalogs and create templates as required Work within the HTH procurement team to identify vendor master data improvement opportunities including data intake, maintenance, what data we capture for reporting, etc. Responsible for ensuring seamless onboarding with suppliers into the Ariba portal once the supplier is approved by procurement and vendor risk management. Activities include monitoring the supplier enablement setup process and validating that new suppliers are set up and active in the P2P and ERP systems.
