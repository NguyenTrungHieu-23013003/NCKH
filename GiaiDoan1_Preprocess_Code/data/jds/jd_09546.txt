The Senior Infrastructure Engineer is accountable for ensuring all project solution development is created with best practices, scalability, and meets the clients expectations. The Senior Infrastructure Engineer will have project and support services remote work, and onsite as-needed, as well as consulting and training services to clients, potential clients and the public utilizing that skill, knowledge, and established team and company policies and procedures. The Senior Infrastructure Engineer performs internal and external training and knowledge transfer as well as perform implementations, integrations, and migrations. The Senior Infrastructure Engineer will also serve as subject matter experts and provide mentoring and assistance to the team and clients. The Senior Infrastructure Engineers also are also part of the high-level escalation path and incident response team for the support services team, and can be called upon for issues that are not able to be resolved through the highest level of support services.
