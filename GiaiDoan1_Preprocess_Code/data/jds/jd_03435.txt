About Kabam Kabam is a world leader of massively multiplayer games for mobile devices. Console quality graphics and next-generation technology are the hallmarks of Kabam games. Kabam merges the art of game design and the science of consumer behavior to create immersive games enjoyed by players around the world. Kabam’s games have generated hundreds of millions of downloads including Marvel Contest of Champions and Transformers: Forged to Fight and won several awards including Apple Editor’s Choice and Google Play’s Best Game of the Year. Kabam has studios and offices in Vancouver, San Francisco, and Austin. Kabam is a wholly-owned subsidiary of Netmarble Games.
