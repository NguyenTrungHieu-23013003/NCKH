PRIMARY FUNCTION Responsible for the maintenance and support of computer systems, and assistance of local and remote users. TYPICAL DUTIES Troubleshoot problems reported by users for desktop computers, printers, mobile and desktop phones, software deployment, security updates and patches. Monitor networks to ensure security and availability to specific users. Isolate and analyze issues. Identify user needs. Maintain integrity of the network and security infrastructure. Assign permissions to company resources to users. Support LANs, WANs, Internet and Intranet systems. Performs additional duties and projects as required or directed by the supervisor. Job Qualifications: SKILL, KNOWLEDGE, EDUCATION AND EXPERIENCE Obtaining a bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or related field. Pursuing MCP certification preferred. Experience in the following systems required: Microsoft Office 365 Microsoft Server 2012 Active Directory 2012 Excellent interpersonal, communication and customer service skills conducive to a help desk environment. 1-3 years of professional experience in supporting a multi-office environment preferred. Other Job Information (if applicable): PHYSICAL REQUIREMENTS Ability to lift 50 pounds. Ability to travel between offices. Visual acuity to perform responsibilities.
