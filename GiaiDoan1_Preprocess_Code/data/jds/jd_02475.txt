This Business Analyst (BA) will be responsible for setting, prioritizing, and evaluating work to engage the membership team in efforts that deliver features and functionality which represent key business strategies. The candidate that fills this role will support multiple customers and stakeholders throughout project and product release cycles. This individual will work closely with end users, business unit representatives, Information System resources and other relevant resources in all stages of the agile project life-cycle. The BA will demonstrate the highest levels of customer service, communication, productivity and professionalism. In addition, the chosen candidate will have the ability and aptitude for leadership, self-motivation, and strong analytical skills.
