The Applications Analyst, Senior will work with external vendors, system owners, community partners, contractors and Health Science Campus leadership to design, develop, implement and make operational information systems. This incumbent will Identify and document information technology design specifications based on analysis/assessment of user needs and generate need- gap analyses. This person will use expert knowledge to generate and develop system scope and objective, analyze and evaluate existing or proposed systems and devise or modify procedures to solve problems using data processing. The Applications Analyst, Senior will work independently on complex system activities and serve and project manager. Incumbents in this role will also provide training, work review and may serve as leads to staff. Minimum Education: Bachelors Degree in related field or equivalent experience or certifications. Minimum Experience/Knowledge: 5 or more years of related experience including system design, documentation, maintenance, upgrades and troubleshooting. Healthcare experience strongly preferred. Required License/Certification: Fire and Safety Certification. If no card upon hire, one must be obtained within 30 days of hire, and maintained by renewal before expiration date.
