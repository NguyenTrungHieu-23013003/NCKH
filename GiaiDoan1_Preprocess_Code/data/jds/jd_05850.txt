Title: Production Coordinator Closing Date: 8/29/2018 Department: Election Commission Hours: Full-Time: Monday-Friday 8:00 a.m.-4:30 p.m., including evenings, weekends and holidays as required Salary: $34,428-$57,381 annually There is one position open at this time.
