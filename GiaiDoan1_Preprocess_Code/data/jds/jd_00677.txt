The primary function of this position is computer modeling of ultrasound imaging and therapy. The responsibilities include computer programming and data analysis. Experience with programming in IDL, the ability to develop physics based propagation models and the experience in handling large datasets are strongly preferred. Excellent interpersonal and communication skills (written and verbal), manage multiple projects and meet deadlines are essential. The successful candidate will gain hands-on experience in ultrasound imaging and therapy and will work closely with a multidisciplinary team of medical doctors and researchers Qualifications A Bachelor’s degree and 2 years to 3 years of experience or an equivalent combination of education and experience required.
