Business Analytics & Research (BAR) is part of the Creative Content Protection organization (CCP) within the NBCUniversal Legal Department. Within CCP, the Business Analytics & Research team provides research, reporting and advanced analytics capabilities to examine online consumer behavior, global digital distribution strategies & content licensing, monetization of user generated content on YouTube, and ROI of anti-piracy enforcement.
