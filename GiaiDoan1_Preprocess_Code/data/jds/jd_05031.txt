The Application Administrator is responsible for implementing, administering, maintaining and supporting a variety of applications used in conjunction with agency business processes and critical systems. This role will act as subject matter expert (SME) for a variety of third-party applications, and will help determine the best application solution to meet specific client or internal business needs. In addition to administration, the Application Administrator will be responsible for the establishment and execution of best practices and procedures around various aspects of business data and data hygiene for both Periscope and our clients.
