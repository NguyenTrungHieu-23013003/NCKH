Position Overview The Director, Data Center is a role responsible for driving the sustainable enablement of our sales and technical teams in support of our Data Center and / or vertical strategy. The Director, Data Center must serve as an evangelist, thought leader, and enabler for the technology both internal and external to the company. As the leader and evangelist, the Director, Data Center creates awareness and builds a market for the solution. As a thought leader, the Director, Data Center is the company’s top resource for strategic direction and tactical solutions across our managed partner ecosystem. As an enabler, the Director, Data Center creates tools, cultivates skills, and designs the GTM specific to the Data Center or vertical segment. The Director, Data Center serves in a Companywide position but must work closely with the regions and therefore must be able to create value through both direct and indirect team building. Including and not limited to interfacing and working closely with our Strategic Technology Partners.
