As the Product Architect you will provide leadership and architectural direction for multiple software development teams for multiple Web based applications. The Product Architect takes ownership for all technical matters in their area and will resolve or escalate all team issues. You will also be responsible for creating and implementing a long-term vision, orchestrating high-quality software implementation, and maintaining, guiding, and improving architectural integrity and evolution for their area. Additional expectations include the ability to fosters high team morale, mentor and coach team members, and ensure best practices and architectural guidelines are followed your product area. In doing this, you will be expected to continually contribute to and lead process improvement activities. Team This requisition is part of the team, which supports the public facing candidate and client web applications that are essential components of Pearson VUE’s core business. One of the team’s primary responsibilities is supporting a number of web based registration applications and client self service portal. The Product Architect will: In this role you will work with both Engineering Leads as well as Product Manager by providing architecture solutions and leadership at the product level. You will do this through overseeing implementation of software that achieves the goals of the architecture in your product area.
