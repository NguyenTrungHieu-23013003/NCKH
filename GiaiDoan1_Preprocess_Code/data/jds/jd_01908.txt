The Star Tribune Sports Department has two temporary openings for clerks who will typically work 12 hours a week, generally in three four-hour shifts 7:30 to 11:30 p.m. Availability to work nights and weekends is a must. These positions will start near the end of August and continue into mid-June 2019. The pay for this position is $15.42/hr. The Prep Sports clerks will answer telephones and take and compile high school sports box score information in a timely and accurate manner. The clerks must have the ability to use internet sources, including Twitter and other social media, to quickly track down results on deadline. They also will discern highlights for use in high school sports roundups and be able to write short summaries about games based on highlights. They also help compile statistics and school contact information for our high school sports database. In that compilation, they will be in constant contact with high school coaches and will be asked to provide written content for our website based on information gained from these conversations. They also will provide timely support for all-metro player and team selections, including preparing and collecting information from coach ballots, and entering player/parent contact information in a database used for the Star Tribune All-Metro Sports Award gala. Qualifications: Successful applicants must be able to answer phones in a courteous and professional manner. Must be able to work efficiently and accurately under pressure. Requires a valid driver's license and proof of insurance. Must have proficient and accurate typing skills along with familiarity with computers in general and knowledge about high school sports, particularly in Minnesota. EOE
