The Architectural Intern performs elementary architectural assignments and works from the designs of others, compiles data, performs elementary computations and prepares architectural plans and renderings. Teamwork oriented with good technical and CAD skills. Reports to Architect, Project Architect, Project Manager or other suitable supervisor as assigned. Job Qualifications: A minimum of 2 years completed in an accredited Architectural program Knowledgeable in current version of AutoCAD, 3D software, MS Word, MS Excel
