The role of the Telehealth Coordinator is to ensure delivery of high quality telemedicine services within Alliance Health. Experience within the health care setting as well as the flexibility to respond to a fluctuating workload and changing environment are important qualities for a Telehealth Coordinator and will contribute to the overall success of the program. Roles and Responsibilities Telemedicine Service and Program Development: Support the telemedicine program within Alliance Health Advocate for, and contribute to, the establishment of organizational structures and resources to support the development of Telemedicine in keeping with the direction and priorities of Alliance Health Develop processes and protocols for effective Telemedicine clinical consultations. Promote Telemedicine to healthcare professionals, patients and the community Field queries regarding organizational telemedicine services Maintain current knowledge base with respect to the field of telemedicine and incorporate national and/or provincial Telemedicine resources to organization specific process. Serve as an organizational point-of-contact for telemedicine: Responsible for the delivery of high quality telemedicine services within Alliance Health Observe organizational obligations with respect
