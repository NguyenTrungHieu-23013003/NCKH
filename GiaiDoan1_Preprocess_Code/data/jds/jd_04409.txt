The SAP Business Systems Analyst – PP/QM/WM will work with team members in managing enhancement and new functionality projects in our SAP ECC 6.0 system. Farouk Systems’ SAP environment is based upon core financials and utilizes the balance of ECC functionality for manufacturing (PP), & QM), warehousing (WM), and Inventory management. Duties & Responsibilities: Work closely with the Accounting, Finance, Marketing, Manufacturing & Operations, and Administrative Services groups to facilitate effective implementations of business solutions and provide knowledge transfer of system functionality to designated Super Users, Subject Matter Experts, and End-Users Analyze business process gaps, identify solution alternatives, and provide guidance to business and IT stakeholders for selecting the best solutions necessary to close the gaps Lead discussions across the business units to analyze, configure, and model processes in SAP Modify, test, and validate system configuration. Prepare detailed functional specifications for development activities including reports, interfaces, conversions, enhancements, and forms (RICEF) Proactively participate in the integration between all related SAP functions with other Business Systems Analysts Provide functional support for Master Data governance and Master Data troubleshooting Build and execute unit testing, integration testing and regression testing (build test cases, perform tests, and make changes to configuration or processes based upon results) Work closely with ABAP Development to define programming requirements and coordinate User Acceptance Testing with the appropriate Business Process Owners Facilitate knowledge transfer, communication, and interaction between various functional business process owners and the IT department Document and control all changes made to the SAP production systems via change control process Work directly with management and end-users to prioritize and manage the implementation of support packages, hot packs, etc. that prove to be relevant to the SAP environment. Track and report SAP PP/QM/WM metrics before and after each implementation effort. Design and create training documentation for new functionality and projects Lead training sessions with end-users on new SAP functionality Gain user approval by developing system prototypes to present to end-users for their review Communicate project status by creating written status reports on a predetermined schedule for large development efforts Responsible for the configuration and daily support of the SAP PP/QM/WM modules that are already implemented Assist end-users in determining the best method for data reporting, either via standard reports, table downloads, ABAP query, or custom program Troubleshoot end-user issues Conduct Research in SAP OSS and Implement OSS notes as required and approved Mentor end-users and teammates on PP/QM/WM functionality Other duties as assigned.
