The Global Security Analyst (Investigations) produces and disseminates time- sensitive investigative analysis relevant to the safety and security of company personnel, assets and operations. The Global Intelligence Analyst is proactive, consultative and business-minded, and reports to the Manager of Global Security Analysis. Responsibilities Monitor Tesla security investigations world-wide, using industry tools and proprietary information; track company-wide trends as well as individual investigations Produce finished investigative analysis for dissemination to company consumers on the safety and security of company personnel, assets and operations, including impact assessment and mitigation recommendation Include solution-oriented recommendations in all analysis, as appropriate Collaborate with Tesla internal investigations; identify opportunities to predict and prevent future security issues and/or incidents Collaborate with the security team to constantly improve analytic standards, workflows, and success metrics and develop/improve analytic products as appropriate Build collaborative relationships with other security analysts in the region; build expertise on security analysis best-practices and innovations Requirements Bachelor's degree required; Master’s preferred or equivalent in experience Evidence of exceptional ability Minimum of 5+ years’ experience as an analyst or targeting officer with local, state or federal law enforcement, the intelligence community, military or corporate security; previous investigative experience highly preferred Substantive experience initiating and conducting complex investigative analysis, and communicating findings to consumers Exceptional communication skills; demonstrated success writing and briefing for a variety of audiences Previous experience integrating data analysis into workflow; prior exposure to Python, R or SQL preferred Proven track record managing multiple projects, and the ability to flex quickly as required by evolving corporate priorities Approximately 10% global travel required Apply Tesla participates in the E-Verify Program
