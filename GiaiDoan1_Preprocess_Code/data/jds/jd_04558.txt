Position Overview: The Retail Operations Analyst is a member of the Field Operations team and reports directly to the Director of Field Operations. The position is an exempt, salary position whose primary duties are to establish retail operations targets across the Retail channel of an omni-channel organization and to monitor performance of those financial targets. Additionally, this position is responsible for providing both analysis and data-based recommendations to the Retail Operations teams in support of making strategic business decisions.
