We accomplish this through innovation, constant process improvement, and through an uncompromising commitment to hiring, retaining and rewarding the best talent available. General Statement of Job The Client Services Administrator is the direct liaison to the client responsible for receiving, processing, revising and completing all incoming requests for additional services from various stakeholders group within the client’s organization. The ideal candidate must have great interpersonal and communication skills with the ability to work in a fast paced and dynamic work environment. The Client Services Administrator reports directly to the Special Projects Watch Commander. Specific Duties and Responsibilities Essential Job Functions: Receive, process and complete all incoming requests for additional services in an efficient and timely manner Conduct quality control and assurance of all incoming service requests for completeness, accuracy and conformance to established standards and protocols Review service requests to determine personnel and resource requirements and priorities Create post orders for all service requests utilizing established policies and procedures Provide completed service requests to SIS Administrative and Operations leadership staff Document security administrative procedures and protocols in the Support Wiki Update the database (Filemaker Pro) and contractor log report daily or as needed Coordinate with SIS leadership staff to assess progress of requests and discuss needed changes Revise service requests when required due to client request, personnel shortages, backlogs or other interruptions and provide updates to the client and SIS leadership staff Develop an excellent working relationship with the client, understanding their expectations and utilizing this knowledge to process incoming service requests timely Provide excellent customer service to all client requestors on all matters related to additional service request standards and protocols Additional Duties: Perform other related duties as required
