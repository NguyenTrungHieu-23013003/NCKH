HR Services is critical to the overall effectiveness of the HR experience across Microsoft. HR Services brings the HR support model and experience to the scale of the global Microsoft employee population by providing the platform for end-to-end operational services and employee experiences with responsibilities spanning employee self-service, assisted support, and employee relations. HR Services is responsible for the operational delivery across a broad range of employee life-cycle experiences such as onboarding, performance and development rewards, talent movement and offboarding. As the catalyst for HR digital transformation, HR Services is also leading the Human Capital Management solution strategy with the global launch of our new HR Core Solution. HR Services holds the key accountability for HR privacy, risk and compliance. The HR Service Compensation Architect is an integral member of the HR Services organization and plays a key role in the delivery of high quality HR Programs to business leaders, managers and employees, as well as to the HR Community. This role will be responsible for working with HR CoE programs to develop, design, implement and ensure the management of complex HR programs and solutions as an integrated solution within our connected employee support eco- system. This will include working over a wide surface area, building partnerships across the organizations and driving impact through leveraging insights. This role requires a problem-solving, programmatic approach with industry thought-leadership to drive consistency in processes & services aligned to Global/Functional/Local policies to exceed performance expectations. Translating programs into a delivery solution requires a mixture of scalable delivery management models working collaboratively across geographies and functions and use of metrics and voice of the customer framework to identify trends and improvement opportunity areas. To be successful in this role, the HR Service Compensation Architect will need effective program management skills, communication skills to align team around strategy and goals, an ability to develop a diverse and strong team, excellent stakeholder engagement skills, a high level of attention to detail, passionate about continuous improvement approach, and to model the Microsoft values and behaviors.
