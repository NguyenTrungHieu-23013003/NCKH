Job Overview: The Data Quality Analyst is responsible for structuring and management of product specifications on bhphotovideo.com. S/he collaborates with internal departments and utilizes their product knowledge to normalize existing specifications and build standard specification templates making sure that product descriptions are consistent across a given category. They ensure that product data becomes and remains clean and accurate. Essential Responsibilities: Use B&H;’s internal resources and your product knowledge to clean unstructured product specification information into standardized values for use as data. Build structured “clean specifications” and information entry templates for respective teams to fill in. Collaborate with internal departments to audit existing product information for accuracy, completeness, and adherence to B&H; style Manage update requests from internal departments and modify older clean specifications and templates to conform them to evolving standards. Additional Responsibilities: As assigned by the manager Specific Knowledge, Skills and Abilities: In-depth knowledge of the following product categories: Computers/Home Entertainment Strong research skills and ability to differentiate between quality information and marketing buzzwords.
