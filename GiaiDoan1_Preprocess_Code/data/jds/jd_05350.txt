Summary Manage multi-skilled Quality Team to execute Quality System objectives to improve efficiency, eliminate waste, reduce cost, and implement new Programs. Responsibilities Develop and Manage PQI Team members to flawlessly execute Quality Control/Quality System Requirements as defined in AWPC Document C740-02/15. Define and execute Quality System requirements on new Production, Experimental, and Prototype Projects and Programs. Champion Quality Investigations that target root cause and corrective analysis, across departments, to reduce Product and Company Risk, and promote Customer Satisfaction. Lead by example to instill a culture of accountability and compliance to Quality System Requirements. Serve as a Change Agent to develop and promote continuous improvement initiatives that increase shareholder value. Education Bachelor’s degree in a Manufacturing discipline preferred or equivalent combined Aerospace experience. Experience Minimum 7-10 years related experience in an Aviation Manufacturing Environment. Aerospace Quality Management System Execution. Working knowledge of Lean Manufacturing or Six Sigma-based continuous improvement methods. Leonardo Helicopter Product Experience is a plus. Leonardo Quality System Experience is a plus. Hands on aircraft experience desirable. Competencies Computer proficiency, particularly with Microsoft PC-based software, especially spreadsheets. SAP knowledge preferred. Ability to read and understand Design Data. Strong Verbal and Written Communication Skills. Embraces a diverse and team-oriented approach. When requested, be able to assist on-site customer acceptance process and initiate relevant corrective/preventive actions.
