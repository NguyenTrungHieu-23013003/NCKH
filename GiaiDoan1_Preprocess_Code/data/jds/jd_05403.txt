Quality minded, tactical and product focused. They will coordinate the quality activities on the production floor and provide useful data to the manufacturing teams. The Quality Coordinator II ensures all associates are aware of product standards and what they can do to ensure that our products meet our customers’ high expectations. This position will also facilitate continuous improvement culture and activities and perform medium complexity problem solving. This position reports to the Quality Control Manager at MSP. Responsibilities include : Coordinate activities on the manufacturing floor as related to maintaining and improving product and process quality Assist in developing and utilizing tools that monitor real-time adherence to (for example, Control Plans, monitoring and measuring and audits); Collect, document, analyze, investigate, report, and maintain trend analysis of nonconformances and customer complaints associated with product quality Present data and trends from metrics and monitoring activities to management and manufacturing teams Assist manufacturing with both short term triage, root cause analysis and long term continuous improvement efforts through meetings, coaching, training, escalation and information Assist with testing and validating material and process changes Skills and Attributes Needed Strong reporting and data analysis skills Ability to communicate efficiently and effectively with both operators and management levels Positive attitude, self-motivated, takes pride in his/her work Strong computer skills with MS Office (emphasis on proficiency in Excel spreadsheets) Some flexibility to collaborate across shifts as needed Familiarity with quality planning tools Ability to manage time and tasks and communicate gaps and constraints Able to learn on the fly, adapt to change and deal with ambiguity Open-minded, empowered to question the status quo and "think outside the known" Customer-focused (both internal and external) Experience and Qualifications 2+ years degree or equivalent experience Experience with digital printing, lean manufacturing, and ISO 9001:2008 a plus ASQ - CQIA Certification a plus Six Sigma Greenbelt certification a plus
