A Day in the Life of a Delivery Lead - EIM: The EIM Delivery Lead has dotted-line supervisory responsibility of a cross- functional team of Data Analysts and Developers within the EIM team. This position is responsible for planning and managing the delivery of complex Enterprise Information Management projects on time, within budget, and with high quality. This role works closely with Project Management to ensure all tasks are accounted for and that the work is assigned to appropriate resources. Tasks in the project plan need to include estimates for requirements gathering, analysis, design, solution design, system and application configuration, development, unit testing, quality assurance testing, and implementation. The EIM Delivery Lead drives collaboration with the business, infrastructure, within the EIM team, and technology vendors to ensure that all project participants are effectively communicating and executing tasks as specified in the project plan.
