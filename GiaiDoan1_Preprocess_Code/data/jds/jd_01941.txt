Hiring Salary Range $16.70 - $21.71/per hour DOE Grade 110 Work Schedule Monday - Friday, 8am - 5pm College Summer Hours: Monday - Thursday, 7am-6pm Work Calendar 12 Months Job Summary This position provides administrative support to the Associate Vice Chancellor for Human Resources (AVCHR). It serves as the main, critical point of contact for administrators both within and outside of the HR Division. The position supports the office of the Associate Vice Chancellor in all areas of which the AVCHR has oversight and responsibility. The position may also provide support to the HR Director for HR Solution Center, HR Business Partners and HR Administration/Strategic Staffing Administrative Assistants as needed or requested. Under general supervision of the Associate Vice Chancellor for Human Resources, this position serves as the Sr. Administrative Assistant. Responsible for coordinating all activities on behalf of the AVCHR and/or which require the attention of or engagement thereof. Serves as primary representative and point of contact for the HR Solution Center. Essential Functions 40% - Provides communication and liaison support to internal and external customers; responds to inquiries related to policy and procedures; distributes approved District-wide information releases; administers, maintains and monitors various databases utilized in labor relations, employee relations, and performance management including grievance tracking database, corrective action and annual performance evaluations database; generates regular activity reports to analyze trends and compliance; proactively manages projects/deliverables/due dates – following up with project owners, key stakeholders, and employees as needed to meet assigned due dates successfully; Creates, edits and/or verifies documents, reports, presentations, correspondence and related materials; develops and maintains filing and record-keeping systems according to established procedures; including electronic policy and case management systems in accordance with employment law requirements and internal records retention policy; coordinates deadlines by prioritizing work demands and assigning/monitoring work performed, as assigned; conducts research related to assignment; identifies and recommends process improvement for implementation; provides recommendations for reports, tracking tools, etc. 30% - Attends and contributes in meetings related to assigned projects, programs or function; prepares and maintains schedules, coordinates calendars, researches and makes travel arrangements for the AVCHR; serves in confidential capacity for personnel issues, complaints, investigations, and grievances and hearings, etc.; conducts in depth research and provides technical answers to complex inquiries from employees and officials related to the specialty area; assists in the development and documentation of new policies and procedures and reports; creates reports to monitor compliance and ensure accountability 25% - Monitors and tracks statistics, budgets, funding and expenditures; assists with preparation of budget and monitoring reports by gathering, compiling, and ensuring accuracy of data from multiple resources; designs and formats complex documents, reports, correspondence, and/or other communications; including highly confidential documents; assists with special projects and ad-hoc reports, including data research and analysis, and data clean-up; prepares presentation and reports on quality assessment results to key stakeholders or owners of the process/compliance requirements; provides technical support in preparation for grievance meeting, opens records requests or other administrative proceedings; collects and reviews supportive documentation for grievances, open records requests, and other administrative proceedings 5% - Coordinates the inventory and replenishing of office supplies and office equipment maintenance services; adheres to and ensures compliance with applicable policies and procedures; coordinate with other Administrative Staff to cover the area that is unavailable due to illness, vacation or other special circumstances; performs other duties as assigned Minimum Qualifications Associate’s Degree and two years of administrative office support or related experience providing a wide variety of secretarial, administrative office support and/or administrative coordination activities. OR An equivalent combination of education and experience sufficient to successfully perform the essential duties of the job such as those listed above, unless otherwise subject to any other requirements set forth in law or regulation.
