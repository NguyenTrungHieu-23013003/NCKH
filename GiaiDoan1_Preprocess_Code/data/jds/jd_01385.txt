Job Description As an engineer on the Data team at Illumio, we expect you to have a basic system understanding and build new components from the ground-up with guidance from the engineering leads. We seek self-driven, independent, engineers who want to be hands-on and to deliver infrastructure as well as features in the above space. We want you to ask the right questions, articulate design trade- offs and propose solutions. We expect you to write and own code, both during and after release. We want you to move the product forward, and embody our ‘Customers First’ philosophy. We want you to have experience building large scale and performant distributed systems, and building REST-ful web services and have the ability and desire to mentor interns, collaborate with peers within and across teams, and leaders in our organization. At Illumio, we design systems and write code that runs both in our own multi-tenant cloud service and in customer datacenters around the world. We write unit and integration tests, as things need to just work ™. And we’re kept honest by a world-class QA team who will let us know if it doesn’t. Our Data team codes mostly in Ruby, and do not use much of Rails - web services are either Passenger or Thin-based. Asynchronous coding is common, and enabled via EventMachine. Our primary databases are postgresql; we use Redis extensively for caching and data aggregation. As we make our roadmap a reality, we are looking for someone who can work with these tools, and is open to working with new technologies as required.
