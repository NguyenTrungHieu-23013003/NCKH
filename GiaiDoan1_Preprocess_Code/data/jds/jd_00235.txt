The Research Data Analyst will play a pivotal role in supporting the research and educational agenda for all departments at UCSF Fresno. The Analyst will use skills as a seasoned, experienced research professional with a full understanding of in-depth statistical analyses and / or research software programming techniques. The Analyst will demonstrate good judgment in selecting methods and techniques for obtaining solutions. Please Note: This position is located in Fresno, CA. UCSF FRESNO UCSF Fresno is a major educational, research and clinical branch of UCSF that is located in the Central San Joaquin Valley and is administered by the Office of the Dean in Fresno, CA. Programs and affiliations of the School of Medicine (SOM), Pharmacy (SOP) and Dentistry (SOD) are administered by UCSF Fresno. ABOUT UCSF The University of California, San Francisco (UCSF) is a leading university dedicated to promoting health worldwide through advanced biomedical research, graduate-level education in the life sciences and health professions, and excellence in patient care. It is the only campus in the 10-campus UC system dedicated exclusively to the health sciences.
