McDermott Will & Emery LLP is a leading international law firm with a diversified business practice representing commercial, industrial and financial enterprises both publicly and privately held. The Firm has a long history of social responsibility and supporting the communities in which our people live and work. The Business Analyst collaborates with business units and stakeholders across the Firm to identify, evaluate, and recommend industry best practices and technology to determine solutions to enhance business processes. This position facilitates the design and implementation of projects that focus on attorney productivity.
