JOB DESCRIPTION The Business Analyst will work with business stakeholders across the organization to create and execute on solutions expected to drive product, team, and org-wide improvement. Will perform analysis, translate business needs into technical/functional requirements, and measure the success of their solutions. The Business Analyst will be a critical contributor and driver of high- visibility systematic improvements across new and existing tools/platforms. Some of the responsibilities of the role include, but are not limited to: Intake and critically assess process/platform enhancement requests; Work with Tech team partners to design a solution fitting to business needs and validate with impacted business partners. Proactively identify opportunities to enhance existing business processes and/or system features; Document business requirements focused on further automating processes and improving overall user experience. Assist our Tech team in troubleshooting platform issues as they arise through through support channels and help perform root cause analysis to diagnose the impact. Assist in the business prioritization of process/platform enhancement requests; Develop and drive a new method for communicating timing/release of enhancement requests to our business partners. Support alternative BizOps sponsored projects in conjunction with normal responsibilities (as needed).
