SYSTEMS ADMINISTRATOR , _Economics_ , to play a key role in designing, implementing, and maintaining a wide range of IT infrastructure. Will also be responsible for the administration of department database systems and act as backup for dedicated desktop support position. A full job description is available at https://www.dropbox.com/s/d9vns4qcuae455q/econ_sysadmin_posting.pdf.
