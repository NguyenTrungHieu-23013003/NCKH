PRIMARY FUNCTION : This position includes responsibility for the administration and planning of Jefferson’s Apple Mac OS-based server and Mobile Device Management environment. This includes management of the server hardware, OS, and backup clients for all production and development systems. Also, supports associated technologies for Apple infrastructure. ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS: Implements & maintains Jefferson’s Apple based services (active directory and software update service) and Airwatch environment. Leverages JAMF Casper management technology to administrate, patch and report on Apple Server/Desktop environment. Creates and maintains scripts and system documentation. Monitors and maintains Apple backup clients. Performs OS tuning and deploys and maintains proper server patches. Maintains server uptime through use of various monitoring, reporting, and alerting solutions. Works with clients and other technical support staff to resolve user issues, schedule downtime for updates, patches. Develops scripts for consistent and secure execution of common tasks. Addresses issues if specifications, architecture, or implementation strategies will lead to quality or system performance issues or problems. Participates in technical design reviews, functional specification reviews, and contributes to ongoing project estimates, scheduling, test plans, and code reviews. Mentors and recommends training for team members as well as members of other departments. Provides technical support and direct client services in response to service requests, break/fix work orders, user questions, and other ticket-based or project-based desktop hardware, software, and networking tasks. Supports the mobile computing devices of the IS&T; client base including notebooks, tablets, and mobile devices. Manages escalations and facilitates resolving service delivery and support issues. Identifies and learns appropriate software and hardware used and supported by the organization. Interacts with co-workers, visitors, and other staff consistent with the iSCORE values of Jefferson. OTHER FUNCTIONS AND COMPETENCIES: Documents and applies domain operational policy and procedures. Monitors ticket queue and resolves/assigns tickets as necessary as well as providing assistance to internal IS &T; technicians, as needed, for Apple and Mobile Device Management related requests, application related requests, issues and questions. May also be called upon to perform projects, systems management platforms, and other activities by management. EXPERIENCE REQUIREMENTS: 2+ years’ experience in Apple systems administration or systems design and implementation in a data center environment. 2+ years’ hands-on experience configuring, deploying and managing Macs in an enterprise environment. Experience in Mobile Device Management design and implementation. Experience in packaging & deploying software & applications to Macs through automated means. Experience with Active Directory integration on Macs Experience in scripting for Macs (such as Automator Workflow, AppleScript, Shell Scripting) ________________________________________________________________________ ADDITIONAL INFORMATION: Familiarity with TCP/IP network environments, including DNS, DHCP, and Firewalls. Experience in the enterprise server management, including the racking and wiring of servers. Maintains a broad knowledge of PC and Apple software technologies.
