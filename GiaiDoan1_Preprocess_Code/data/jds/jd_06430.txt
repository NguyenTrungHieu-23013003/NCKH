The ESS Support Analyst will work with a number of business partners in the Finance, Accounting, HR, BI, and Timekeeping space to support a variety of back office applications which have a great impact on Whole Foods Market’s ability to operate in the market today. They will be responsible for identifying and troubleshooting complex system issues across a range of technologies. The ideal candidate will have a good understanding of the business processes related to financial systems and be able to communicate and translate system impacts to non-technical and business users. They should be able to work with Microsoft and Oracle Databases at a high level to query multiple tables and research data issues with support from our business partners. An understanding of Agile and ITIL is strongly desired but not required. A cursory understanding of SOX compliance is also preferred.
