Job Description The Technology Consultant is a member of Workday’s Professional Services organization focused on the implementation of our cloud based Human Resources, Financial Management, and Student Information Systems. This role works closely with the client’s project team to design, develop and deploy solutions for one or more of the following focal areas: Integrations, Data Conversions, Reporting and Analytics. Focal Areas: Integrations : The integration consultant develops solutions to synchronize data between Workday’s cloud-based software and external systems. This role partners with client teams to gather design specifications, utilize workday development tools to build and test working solutions, and ensure overall project success. Data Conversion : A data conversion consultant interfaces directly with client and project team experts to design and execute highly strategic data conversions data loads for Workday implementations. This role advises and executes extraction, transformation and load of legacy data via various integration products, tools, and services to ensure the successful deployment of our customers’ data into the Workday tenant. Reporting and Analytics: The Reporting and Analytics Consultant works closely with the client's project team to determine reporting needs and best practices. This role is responsible for creating not only quality data display but insightful analysis and evaluation reporting solutions. Activities include gathering specifications, deliverable management, advanced and composite report building, and data analysis.
