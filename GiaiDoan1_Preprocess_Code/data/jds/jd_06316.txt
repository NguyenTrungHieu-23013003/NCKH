Demonstrated ability to work effectively and efficiently in a cross-functional team environment. Effectively communicates with technical and non-technical stakeholders. Anticipate potential issues and proactively present solution scenarios. Position Impact to Business The Customer Integration Engineer contributes to business success by minimizing opportunities for costly delays and customer disruption through proactive planning. Also, the successful execution of the role increases earlier achievement of Test of Record status impacting customer satisfaction and revenue generation.
