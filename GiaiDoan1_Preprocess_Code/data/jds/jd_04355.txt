Position: Senior Business Intelligence Reporting Analyst. Duties: Design and develop Tableau reports to ensure all the business requirements are met and provide enhanced Solution that meets client requirements. Design and implement Business Intelligent (BI) reporting applications using Tableau and Cognos. Responsible for creation and Enhancements of SSRS (SQL Server Reporting Services) reports as per the user requirements. Enhancement of existing SSIS (SQL Server Integration Services) packages and configure the data loading as per the report requirements. Quality checking the Health Care Data from different markets and creation of ETL (Extract, Transform, Load) packages to load them in to company database. Provide mockup dashboards to business in Tableau. Combine views and reports into interactive dashboards in Tableau Desktop that are presented to Business Users, Program Managers, and End Users. Participate in business analysis activities to gather required reporting and dashboard requirement. Design, implement, and support a platform providing ad-hoc access to large datasets. Use data mining, model building, other analytical techniques to develop and maintain customer segmentation and predictive models to drive the business. Translate business requirements into specification that will be used to implement required reports and dashboards, created from potentially multiple data sources. Identify critical areas of the data structure and recommends improvements to existing reports and reporting processes. Job Requirements: The minimum requirements for this position are a bachelor’s degree in Computer science, electrical engineering or related field and 4 years of work experience as BI developer, software engineer or closely related field.
