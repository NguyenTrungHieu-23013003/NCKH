POSITION PURPOSE: Support support for TechnipFMC’s global network infrastructure. Troubleshoot and resolves technical issues as they arise. Ensure optimal network performance and ensure maximum network uptime. POSITION DUTIES: Act as the owner of problems through resolution. Analyze issue and take actions to resolve problems (including Root Cause Analysis and Problem Remediation). Design, deploy, implement and maintain network solutions to meet business needs. Ensure reliability security and performance for TechnipFMC’s global network. Provide end-user support and problem resolution for network incidents. Maintains and administers LAN, WAN, wireless, load balancing and traffic optimization infrastructure. Monitor and document network solutions. Participate on 24x7 on-call rotation. POSITION REQUIREMENTS: Minimum of 5 years of experience managing and troubleshooting global complex network environments. Minimum of 5 years hands-on experience with advanced network technologies and solutions including IPv6, MPLS, BGP, EIGRP, OSPF, HSRP, policy-based routing, ether-channel, QoS, load balancing, Spanning-Tree, VLANs, Port channels, Firewalls and VPN tunnels. Understanding of basic network services: DNS, DHCP and Active Directory. Minimum 3 years hands-on experience with Cisco wireless solutions. Minimum 3 years experience serving as a Network Engineer or Network Architect in a global network environment. CCNP certification or equivalent experience. Ability to lead projects and communicate technical solutions to business stake holders. Experience with F5 APM, LTM, GTM considered a plus.
