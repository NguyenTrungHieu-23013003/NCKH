The Data Entry Manager carries out the mission and goals of the Data Entry team and develops and maintains a culture that emphasizes quality, continuous improvement and high performance. They foster a spirit of teamwork and unity among department members that allows the team to effectively work together to enable each employee and the department to succeed. The Data Entry Manager ensures timely turnaround of tasks, accuracy of data entry and communication to management that meet service levels and quality standards. The Data Entry Manager is also tasked with collecting feedback from team members and implementing process improvements where applicable.
