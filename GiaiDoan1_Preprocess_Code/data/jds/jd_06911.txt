Function/Purposes: Typical project engagements include: primary responsibility for client relationship management, project quality, project schedule and project budget control. _IT Area Explanations:_ _IT Governance and Policies_ encompass the processes, roles, and tools to support IT decision making, planning and investment for the organization. The effective use of technology depends on an organization’s ability to make knowledgeable IT investment decisions that align with its business needs. From a strategic perspective, IT decision making represents a critical area, as it determines how an organization plans for, allocates, and manages its IT resources and investments. _IT Organization_ refers to the people that provide IT support services aligned with business objectives and associated service demands, and that follow defined policies and practices _IT Infrastructure_ refer to the computing platforms, networks, communications, and operating systems on which the organization’s applications and data reside, including remote connectivity and internal connectivity to external cloud-based services, data sets, etc. and to the Internet in general. An organization’s technical infrastructure provides the critical foundation for connectivity and processing power to support the systems necessary to run business operations. _IT Applications and Data_ includes the organization’s software application tools and data sets used for business functions. Applications software includes such tools as the financial accounting system, the maintenance management system, the process automation system, and the Email system.
