Responsibilities may include the creation and administration of databases and/or support of computer programs and applications needed to achieve department goals. ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS: Consistently exhibits behavior and communication skills that demonstrate DaVita Medical Group's commitment to superior customer service, including quality, care and concern with each and every internal and external customer. Develops and maintains organized documentation for efficient department operations. Supports performance improvement duties for the department.
