POSITION SUMMARY The Community Investment Consultant will have primary responsibility to support the review of competitive Affordable Housing Program (AHP) applications, and provide technical assistance to internal and external parties as it relates to the use of the Bank’s community investment products and services. ESSENTIAL DUTIES & RESPONSIBILITIES Train, mentor, and build capacity of team members. Provide quality customer service and technical assistance to program users including guidance on submitting requests and supporting documentation, and maintaining regulatory compliance. Review competitive AHP applications and provide advanced technical support to others charged with the same. Identify industry training opportunities for group members. Routinely review existing processes for efficiency, assist with the development of new processes, make recommendations to management on program policies and procedures, provide research and support for training efforts. Assist management in creating a highly functioning, autonomous, confident, high morale team. EXPERIENCE & EDUCATIONAL REQUIREMENTS 5+ years experience in multi-family rental affordable housing finance. Bachelor degree with a concentration in Business or Public Administration, Finance, Real Estate, Urban Planning, Community Development, or other allied field. Broad range of skills including an understanding of local, state, and federal housing and community development and affordable housing programs (e.g., Affordable Housing Program, Low Income Housing Tax Credit, Historic Tax Credit, bond financing, tax increment financing, down payment assistance programs, etc.). Demonstrated project leadership and management skills; strong interpersonal skills; ability to plan and prioritize work requiring input from several areas within the Bank. Quantitative analytical skills and problem solving
