Computer Operator 1728BR Information Systems Regular Job Description The Computer Operator monitors the production, backup, and development of iSeries technology and addresses any system performance or other related issues that may arise during the 3rd shift. This position assists with managing the daily, weekly, and special systems backup for DON’s Production and Development environments as well as the management of iSeries tapes at Off-Site and Iron Mountain locations. This role is also responsible for logging and communicating issues with iSeries systems including network printers. The Computer Operator supports the Operations Supervisor in all operational activities related to the 3rd shift and provides a clear turnover to the 1st shift Operator during shift change. Hours for this job are Sunday through Thursday from 12 Midnight to 8:30am RELATIONSHIPS This position reports to the IT Operations Supervisor and interacts with other members of the IT Operations team and other internal business units as needed. The Computer Operator is responsible for maintaining positive business relationships with both internal and external customers and will work with 3 rd party vendors on production issues, tape management, and invoice management.
