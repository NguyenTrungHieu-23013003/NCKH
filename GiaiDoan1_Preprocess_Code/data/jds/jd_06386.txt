Duties Summary About the Position: Manages and maintains the district's Hydraulics and Hydrology Office's field data collection program and equipment. Responsibilities Plans, prepares, and leads field related activities on large river systems. Uses acoustic Doppler current profiler to measure discharge on large river systems. Performs repair and maintenance assignments at various gauging stations. Manages property accountability for all gage and related equipment. Collects, compiles and disseminates hydraulic, hydrologic and hydrometeorology data for the Great Lakes basin. Makes independent field investigations. Posts appropriate data streams to the web pages for public dissemination. Assembles necessary field equipment and supplies. Prepares instructive material, oversees upkeep of equipment, and ensures the proper adherence to safety practices. Travel Required 25% or less - Up to 25% Business Travel may be required.
