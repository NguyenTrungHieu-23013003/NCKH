Central Avenue Phoenix, Arizona, 85012 www.azdes.gov First review of resumes 6/15/2018 JOB SUMMARY: This position is a member of the Information Technology Leadership Team, reporting to the Department of Economic Security, Division of Technology Services, (DTS), Assistant Director/Chief Information Officer. This Division spans the full spectrum of IT support for an agency with dozens of lines of business, approximately 8,000 staff and $4.1 billion in annual expenditures. The Division's major functions include, strategic planning, customer engagement and support, end-user service delivery for devices and services, help desk, PC maintenance, etc., operations, planning and management of all infrastructure such as networking, servers, storage and data center, development and maintenance of over 100 applications, enterprise program/project management, enterprise architecture and standards and modernization of existing technology. This is a complex environment that requires a leader who understands how the components work together for improvement of processes. The Deputy Chief Information Officer will provide 'informed leadership' with a combination of functional expertise, leading people, and managing change. This position is responsible for the overall rhythm of the business, business reviews and oversees the day-to-day business activities.
