Are you passionate about helping organizations use data to more effectively market to customers? Want to help optimize websites to drive engagement and real business impact? Up for the challenge of leveraging different technologies and aligning multiple data sources to paint a singular view of the customer? The Digital Analyst works across nearly all parts of Endurance International Group, supporting marketing efforts across our brands with data-driven recommendations on opportunities for growth. The Role: As a key member of the Digital Analytics team under the Marketing Department, you will become a subject matter expert on everything encompassing the digital experience for our customers. You’ll be collaborating with: Engineering and Development teams to ensure accurate and comprehensive data capture, Acquisition teams to enable more effective channel analysis, Optimization teams to design, build, and analyze tests to drive corporate KPIs, and other Analytics teams to help use clickstream data to inform predictive models for churn and lifetime value, among many others. What you will do: Identify opportunities for analysis and optimization on the sites that increase conversion or drive revenue Develop and maintain clear, concise reporting on KPIs measuring marketing performance Provide insight into site visitor behavior and engagement in order to inform site design changes and marketing initiatives Use quantitative and qualitative data to identify A/B and Multivariate testing opportunities and establish success metrics, help deploy and analyze tests Coordinate with technical teams to ensure accurate and comprehensive web analytics implementation and successful deployment of new solutions Collaborate with BI and Analytics teams to leverage clickstream data with other sources to help inform predictive modeling of visitor quality, conversion, retention and more Evangelize digital data and utilization of Web analytics tools by other teams through training and facilitation What you need: Bachelor’s degree (preferably in a quantitative discipline; business, math, statistics, computer science) 2-5 years of experience using leading web analytics and optimization tools (Adobe/Omniture/SiteCatalyst, Google Analytics 360, Adobe Test & Target, Monetate, Optimizely, etc.) Proficiency with Microsoft Excel (or similar i.e. Tableau) for data manipulation and visualization Proficiency with SQL for data analysis; ability to write basic queries and understanding of database concepts Prior experience with digital marketing channel analysis (PPC, SEO, Display, Social) Understanding of analytics implementation and tag management, HTML/CSS/JavaScript and standard coding practices Excellent analytical and problem solving capability Excellent verbal, written and presentation skills Knowledge of hosting-related SAAS products a plus Sense of humor a must!
