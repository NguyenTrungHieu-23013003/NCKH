Provide analytical and administrative support to department or division management. Use analytical and reporting tools to compile, analyze and present data for consideration, with specific recommendations. Design and generate regular and ad hoc reports. Oversee the content, format and production of regulatory reports. Participate in, and may facilitate, work groups and committees. May provide project management support. May design and maintain the department or division web page. May supervise staff. Essential Responsibilities: For a department/division, abstracts data from various KFHPW databases, analyzes & presents data in a standardized format that is useful & easy to understand. May meet with managers and/or work groups to interpret results. Designs, formats & generates daily, weekly, monthly, quarterly and/or annual reports using appropriate analytical methodologies & various applications. Ensures report integrity & reliability. Coordinates the ongoing distribution of pertinent reports/data. Defines, clarifies & responds to client questions, concerns or requests. May provide ad hoc data & reports by analyzing, verifying, manipulating & maintaining data in a variety of applications. May be involved in analytical or other work specific to the department/division including coordinating computer hardware/software upgrades; assisting with annual budget process; developing & administering the budget; designing & updating departmental/division web pages & other communication materials; working with ISD on troubleshooting system problems; coordinating/facilitating communication with other departments/divisions; and/or supervising non-exempt staff. At the direction of and in collaboration with departmental/divisional leaders, develops systems to capture clinical, financial, service and/or quality information to use in evaluating goal attainment. May maintain a system unique to the department/division.
