The Technology Operations Associate is responsible for providing 2nd-level operational hardware and software support in a fast paced business environment for multiple customers. This position requires very strong communication skills with a focus on customer service and problem solving. The individual must be team, process and project oriented, and must be efficient in completing assigned tasks individually or as part of a team. The individual must possess strong technical knowledge in a specific technology area and be able to use that knowledge to analyze and resolve technical issues efficiently with minimal supervision. This individual is a source of improvement ideas and consistently documents solutions and systems. There is significant contact with the customer community. The ideal candidate will be highly motivated and self-reliant with a desire to learn. Essential Job Functions Issues Management and Resolution: Solid Knowledge of Windows and Mac/OSX operating systems Analyze issues and formulate solutions Troubleshoot system, application and hardware issues Update the customer & service desk with status and resolution Work directly with customers to solve problems Use external information to research and resolve issues when needed Consistently update the ticket tracking system with status and fixes Systems Administration: Assist in performing routine desktop systems maintenance Install/configure desktop hardware and software Assist in documenting processes and systems Track assets Perform windows file restores Communication: Strong interpersonal skills with heavy focus on customer service Demonstrate sound written, oral and organizational skills Document solutions, systems and procedures Consistently share solutions with peers Works effectively as an individual and as part of a team Adhere to team policies and organizational reporting structures Resource & Project Management: Complete assigned tasks on time Ability to follow documented commonly-used concepts, practices, and procedures Ability to manage multiple constituencies, issues, priorities and goals Qualifications Education: Bachelor's Degree from an accredited University, preferably in Computer Science, Information Systems or equivalent program or IT experience required Basic technical certification in field of expertise preferred Work Experience: Minimum 1 - 3 years IT experience Experience working in a heterogeneous technical environment MAC/PC cross platform skills a strong plus Skills: Experience in the Advertising and/or Marketing industry preferred At least 1 year of experience delivering IT solutions within a corporate and/or high volume environment preferred
