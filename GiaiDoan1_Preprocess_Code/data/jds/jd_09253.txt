The International IT Analyst is a key contributor from a solutions and project perspective in the work between in-country IT and US IT teams in order to align with the strategic direction of Global Planning. This person will be responsible for consulting internally and with in-country teams to capture technology needs, help prioritize them in the global planning process, and facilitate the analysis, submittal, and approval of new initiatives. This person will lead efforts to define and manage technology solutions, project initiatives, business architectures, technology objectives and strategies, and represent these to business areas and IS leadership in the US as needed. In addition, the International IT Analyst will represent the international teams in project initiation and change management in the US through ensuring project scope and features accurately meet their needs. The International IT Analyst will become an expert in business procedures and processes and participate in business transformation and change management activities. This individual should demonstrate the highest levels of customer service, communication, productivity, and professionalism as well as understanding of different cultures and customs in countries where Costco does business around the world. In addition, the candidate will have the ability and/or aptitude for leadership and self-motivation, possessing analytical skills and Costco business expertise. The candidate should be comfortable with speaking in front of groups of people and leading meetings in person as well as on conference calls with all levels of IT and business teams.
