Data Engineer In this role, you will support Tesla’s firmware organization in building out data pipelines that will utilize large-scale data sets from our vehicle fleets to quantify product performance, and thereby inform engineering efforts. You will collaborate with teams developing our data platforms, vehicle firmware, service, reliability, and others to continually expand insights into our products and improve the customer experience. Role and Responsibilities: Build data pipelines to support large scale data analysis and data visualizations. Develop methods for analysis of vehicle usage trends at scale. Provide development teams insight into vehicle usage & performance in real world environments. Generate reports for development, management, and executive teams to inform decision-making. Collaborate across teams to gain insights into how products will perform for customers, and advocate continual improvement. Required Experience: B.S. or higher degree in Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, or similar. 2+ years’ experience working in a data engineering or analyst role. Proficiency in: Python, SQL, data visualization. Experience with ETL pipelines, database design and implementation. Experience with big data ecosystem (Spark, Hadoop, etc.), software development best practices, data analysis at scale. Ability to identify and communicate data-driven insights efficiently. A passion for data! Nice to Have: Experience with NoSQL databases, such as MongoDB. Experience with Airflow. Experience with Tableau, and/or other visualization libraries.
