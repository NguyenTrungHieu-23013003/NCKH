Overview The Assistant General Counsel will be part of a diverse and talented legal team responsible for leading and advising the business on innovative uses of personal data in our multi-product ecosystem. The AGC reports directly to the Chief Privacy Officer and joins a world class privacy team. The AGC will have a strong background in legal product review, technology, and possess a significant in-house experience with technology and heavy data and privacy issues – able to understand the most important privacy-related laws, regulations and standards that apply to Intuit’s businesses, and be accountable for training both business and legal partners on those rules. The AGC will help drive Intuit’s data stewardship vision and collaborate on data privacy and interrelated money movement, financial and, regulatory issues - driving, partnering, and helping develop innovative approaches to complex issues. The AGC in close partnership with legal and compliance will collectively help ensure compliance with privacy and tax regulations by closely partnering with the business while enabling data innovation and experimentation, Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning insights, open platform strategy, and data sharing across our ecosystem of multiple products and services.
