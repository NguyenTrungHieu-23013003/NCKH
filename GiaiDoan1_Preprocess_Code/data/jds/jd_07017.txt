Job Description JOB SUMMARY: Ensure the health, stability and integrity of company technology systems. Monitor, maintain and repair information systems that support core organizational functions. DUTIES & RESPONSIBILITIES: Support and monitor servers / network infrastructure. Monitor and maintain network infrastructure/desktop and server operating systems and other applications used by the firm. Proactively identify opportunities to correct network infrastructure failures/anomalies. Collaborate within Information Services and interact with Service Desk and appropriate department resources to keep systems operational 24x7 Analyze and resolve issues in order to maximize the benefits of all systems to ensure firm operability and profitability. Generate practical solutions to problems while maximizing existing resources. Effectively communicate with clients and borrowers to ensure excellent customer service and accuracy of requests. Assimilate new technologies, information and methods needed to maintain servers and network infrastructure. Knowledge and use of the following Systems Admin technologies: Windows Server Operating Systems, Server 2012 and 2016 preferred Active Directory & Group Policy experience Moderate VMware knowledge (ESXi 5.5, 6.0 and 6.5 preferred) Beginner to moderate SAN storage systems knowledge (3PAR; Nimble; EqualLogic) Microsoft Office 365 (email) Moderate to Advanced Veeam Backup expertise Microsoft System Center Suite 2012 or 2016 (SCCM, SCOM) Basic SharePoint (onsite and cloud-based) Cloud Computing (Azure, AWS) preferred Some experience with networking components: switches, VPN Sophos AV (or similar products) SUPERVISORY RESPONSIBILITIES: This job does not require the exercise of supervisory responsibilities.
