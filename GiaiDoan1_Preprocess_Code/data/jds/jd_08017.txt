Managing the onboarding, distribution, and tracking of engineering and retail resources used for the creation of Windows is a challenging task. The Windows Engineering Systems Platform (ESP) team offers the opportunity to help drive and evolve this service for the Windows Engineering Services team. The ESP team is in the business of ensuring fast turnaround and accurate tracking of engineering and retail resources while providing Self-Servicing capabilities for the end user along with the ability for Managers and team delegates to quickly and easily access resource allocations to their team(s). We are seeking a highly driven and motivated individual who will be responsible for all aspects of engineering resource life cycle management (for example - distribution planning, onboarding, communication, distribution, documentation, reporting, and retirement) You will be actively involved in the life cycle management of engineering resources You will responsible for driving and maintaining KPI's for life cycle management You will use metrics to gather and provide feedback to your team and the service You will use technologies, processes, tools, and methodologies to provide tactical solutions for immediate issues/needs You will be customer focused Responsibilities Responsibilities/Skills Design, create, and report on Life Cycle management KPI's and Metrics through the use of PowerBi or similar technology Engage in capacity planning and demand forecasting with ES and partner teams Make recommendations and drive improvements into the Life Cycle management service/portal Partner with internal teams to streamline resource acquisition process Own communication(s) to customers and resource owners Create and implement procedures and processes that ensure smooth, consistent, and reliable management of engineering resources.
