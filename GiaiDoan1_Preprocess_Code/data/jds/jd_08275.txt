Operations Analyst needed to support property management department of a growing and fast-paced real estate and investment organization. Ideal candidate should not only have an analytical and detail oriented mindset, but also the ability to problem-solve with a big-picture view. Team point person for data integrity, accuracy and timing, and will perform validation of Data Warehouse, Salesforce, etc., data as needed. Assist in creation of more streamlined internal processes, draft instruction manuals and train internal and external team members as new systems/processes are implemented. Produce ad hoc analyses (Lease Renewal, Hold/Sell modeling, etc.) as needed. Maintain complete and accurate files for all matters including files contained in electronic database. Educational/Experience Requirements (including certifications, licenses, etc): Bachelor’s degree or equivalent industry experience. 2+ years of commercial real estate experience preferable. Technical Skill Requirements: Proficiency with Microsoft Office Suite including a strong working knowledge of Excel. MRI and/or Salesforce experience highly desirable.
