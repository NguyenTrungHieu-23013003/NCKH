Overview The Logistics Supervisor is responsible for monitoring and coordinating the activities of a component or group of component logistical operations, such as set up, delivery or warehousing functions within a branch operation. The Logistics Supervisor may alternatively be responsible for the total logistical operation of a single location within a larger branch operation and/or assisting the Logistics Manager in coordinating all branch logistics operations. Responsibilities Supervises a staff of logistics operations employees May assist in the purchasing of equipment, parts and supplies May assist Logistics Manager with inventory control process May oversee or assist Logistics Manager with oversight of warehouse and delivery operations May assist in facility maintenance and coordination of all necessary branch repairs Must be willing to learn and become proficient in SAP and other Branch software including but not limited to Inventory management and procurement. Be able to generate and interpret reports for functions under supervision to support business efficiency. Support Branch Management requests for ad hoc data and reports Qualifications 5-7 years experience in warehousing, delivery services, inventory coordination or related fields Strong knowledge of basic warehousing procedures including; record keeping techniques Experience in utilizing warehouse equipment including hand truck and carts Solid knowledge of proper methods of stacking, storing, lifting and loading parts and supplies. General computer knowledge including the use of Microsoft Outlook, Excel, and Word. Excellent communications skills both written and oral Excellent decision making ability, including the ability to work independently with minimal supervision College degree preferred Sharp Electronics Corporation is an equal opportunity employer – minority/female/disability/veteran.
