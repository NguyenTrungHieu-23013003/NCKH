Summary The incumbent uses knowledge of quality tools and their uses to support both EdgeOne and Client quality improvement projects as well as product development projects. We are looking for a highly driven individual that will be responsible for revising, implementing, and auditing the Quality Management System including developing quality procedures, implementing quality training, and communicating the results of quality efforts for the division. processes to identify improvement opportunities and review new processes for possible integration into systems Maintain the company’s Quality Management System, including the development and implementation of policies and procedures for compliance to 21CFR820, ISO 17025 and ISO 13485 Ensure compliance with national and international standards and legislation related to Quality System requirements, while maintaining functionality and flexibility according to the EdgeOne Medical business model Manage document control and maintenance of quality records Maintain the internal quality audit program and manage associated corrective and preventive action plans, while also maintaining the facility in a ready state for external and customer audits Oversee the qualification of suppliers, including audit of supplier systems Analyze and solve quality problems Manage and deliver training for QMS procedures and testing protocols Analyzes quality data to support ongoing quality system improvement and report status to upper management Plan, promote and organize training activities related to Quality Program Track equipment calibration requirements Skills and Abilities:
