PURPOSE AND DESCRIPTION OF JOB Responsible for moving and storing materials, supplies, and products throughout the facility in a safe and controlled manner, loading and unloading trucks, trailers and railcars, and monitoring and maintaining inventory accuracy. RESPONSABILITIES Work safely at all times, safe work practice policies and procedures and Good Manufacturing Practices (GMP). Separate and palletize finished goods coming from the sealing station to the transfer trailer. Maintain inventory accuracy. Load and unload raw materials from railcars and trailers. Maintain daily forklift check list. Supply production floor with materials and supplies to work stations. Fill orders of end board and ring stock from in-process area. Put away and retrieve material Pull orders from Nestyle blanks rack area. Participate in group problem solving and special projects. Work with Planning for daily work schedule Trains other employees in all areas of warehouse operation. Understand internal and external customer needs. Maintain department cleanliness to SQF (Safe Quality Food) standards. Other duties may apply. Perform weekly cycle counts Enters data into BPCS, Transition Works Provides relief for sealing station when needed Drives forklifts in the operation of their work SUPERVISORY RESPONSABILITIES This position has no supervisory responsibility
