School Description California South Bay University is a learning community that seeks to serve society by educating the leaders of tomorrow and extending the frontiers of knowledge. At California South Bay University, we take seriously our mission to provide a synthesis of innovative and traditional education leading to outstanding professional opportunities for adult learners. As we continue to grow, we are looking for individuals who thrive on challenge, excellence and teamwork for continual improvement. California South Bay University is an equal opportunity employer. To apply, please E-mail resume and cover letter to hr@csbu.us. In the subject line, please indicate the position you are applying. Job description Seek and cultivate new job openings through online and publications; notify students and alumni of job openings; offers resume review, interview techniques, phone shills, and mock interviews for students and alumni; answers students and employer questions regarding the externship, employment and placement processes; generate employer database through phone call, mailing and site visit; establish a working relationship with existing and new companies; Compile and keep current job and internship placement statistics; Conduct employer surveys. Job Requirement Bachelor’s degree Minimum of three years of customer service experience Computer Skills including Microsoft Office and Internet Ability to communicate effectively and professionally Jobsite & Interview Campbell, CA
