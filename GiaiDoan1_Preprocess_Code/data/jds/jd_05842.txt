Description Enterprise Data Architect contributes to creation of strategic data architecture for the organization. Enterprise Data Architect works with Data Governance & Analytics team, Enterprise Architecture team, and other IT teams to define the principles, standards, and guidelines regarding data flows, data aggregation, data consumption, and data placement. He/she will provide guidance regarding data architecture for critical programs and for critical data quality remediation activities. This position will support the Chief Data Officer office with the following: Leads definition & development of enterprise data architecture concepts and standards Participates in design and development of the Enterprise Data Architecture framework for existing as well as new data platforms Provides guidance and counsel to project teams to ensure that projects and deliverables align with BMO target state architecture Provides effective challenge during independent architecture validation during architecture review process Researches and monitors industry activities to keep abreast of relevant data technologies and recommends technology direction to the Enterprise Data Architecture leadership team regarding the feasibility and applicability of such technologies for BMO Leads identification of data architecture opportunities for a broad, diverse, and complex set of business functions and processes, and recommends capitalization of these opportunities to the Enterprise Data Architecture leadership team Works with Business & Technology teams drawing upon technical and data processing knowledge to design & build solutions Builds and sustains day-to-day communication with business partners at various levels of the organization, including Data Governance Leads, Line of Business partners, other Data Governance Analysts, as well as other internal and external partners Participate in regular Data Governance team, project, and leadership meetings Qualifications Qualifications Skills and Experience Possesses a university degree in Technology, Business or related field, and/or 10+ years relevant work experience 5+ years in data engineering, data analysis, business intelligence/data warehousing experience or related experience Deep expertise in data architecture, data modeling, and data science platforms Hands on experience with designing Hadoop based “big data” solutions Practical experience with NoSQL/Graph databases -
