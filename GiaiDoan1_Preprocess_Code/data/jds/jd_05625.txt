Ambassador engineers demand quality and efficiency, and love taking on responsibility with the freedom to make a large impact. As an Ambassador engineer, you will EXECUTE WITH URGENCY by shipping code within your first week and immediately begin contributing meaningful value to the team. In a company where we THINK LONG TERM, Back-End Engineers will receive mentorship from day one and will eventually be shaped into a mentor for future employees. In your 1st Month you will… Practice disciplined software engineering (automated testing, code reviews, and writing clean, pragmatic code). Collaborate with cross-functional teams to define, design, and ship extraordinary products. Be flexible, grow, and learn fast in a fun yet professional startup environment. In your 3rd Month you will… Begin participating in code reviews, project meetings, and design discussions. Develop technical skills in order to write more complex code. Design and build scalable services and drive major architecture implementations. Rapidly iterate to improve performance and incorporate user feedback and insights. In your 6th Month you will… Begin writing tech specs. Continue to write complex code while enhancing your capabilities. OWN IT by architecting a product release from start to finish. Attention to detail, good judgement, excellent communication, and a great attitude.
