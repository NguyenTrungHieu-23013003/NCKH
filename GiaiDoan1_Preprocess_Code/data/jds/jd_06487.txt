The Elementary Instructional Technology Specialist (ITS) is the leader at each elementary school for: the integration of technology in classrooms around the building , the use of technology for advancing learning, enhancing communication and making the jobs of the constituents more effective and efficient through technology use, the instruction of new uses of technology to both staff and students, the upkeep of the fleet of devices, software and the network in the building, the joint work of the Digital Literacy Team by partnering with the building library teacher to advance research and technology skills in the building.
