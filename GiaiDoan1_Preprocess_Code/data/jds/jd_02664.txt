Job Description Customized Energy Solutions is hiring a Business Analyst in its Philadelphia office to provide support to its Renewable Energy and Compliance team. The role will be responsible for all requirements specifications, high-level design concepts, and quality assurance testing work in the development of moderately-complex web-based tools to track and manage client positions, renewable energy credits, and regulatory and compliance processes. The successful candidate will be a highly-motivated self-starter able to work independently and as part of a team. Being extremely detail oriented and a person of integrity are essential qualities, as is an ability to handle new tasks and manage multiple projects and competing priorities. _Representative Duties:_ Engage directly with Business leadership and subject matter experts to understand scope and elicit business requirements Organize and translate business requirements into functional requirements Engage with Business and IT leadership to define non-functional requirements Work closely with Development resources to create and refine designs for application workflows, data structures, and user interfaces Create and execute quality assurance test plans, cases, and scripts Coordinate with Development and IT release management resources to ensure successful software deployments Participate in production support analysis and remediation activities
