Job Description The Office of the Nassau County Comptroller is seeking a full-time Policy and Data Analyst who will work with our team on policy, data and operations analysis and report writing. Applicants should have a background in public policy, financial, economic, and research analysis and the ability to interpret data to produce written reports and compelling visualizations. We are seeking intellectually curious, proactive, and innovative applicants who can conceptualize recommendations to solve complex problems within county government and work in a fast-paced environment. The Policy and Data Analyst will assist with numerous projects, including: Utilize various accounting & IT systems to extract raw data, analyze information, and build key reports, with supporting information; Assisting with developing methodologies to measure and report on financial and operational performance; Conduct data analysis to generate financial and operational efficiencies, improve service deliveries; Preparing fact sheets, reports, white papers, presentations, infographics, and other work product to summarize key information for County departments and stakeholders; Assisting with policy research, reports, and projects as needed; Performing related duties as required.
