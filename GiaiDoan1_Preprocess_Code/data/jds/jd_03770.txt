The Hardware Testing Team is responsible for coordinating and conducting a wide variety of test activities for Argo Hardware. This includes development testing, qualification testing, and acceptance testing. The team manages test activities with a focus on test for automotive and test for robotics. The team works with a variety of internal and external partners to conduct testing. The team plans, executes, and reports a variety of tests themselves, and they coordinate testing performed by product development teams at Argo and Argo’s partners. The group is responsible for developing test plans aligned with industry standards and creating new test methods unique to automotive robotics.
