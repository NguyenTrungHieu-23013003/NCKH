Powertrain Lab Operations – Test Support Engineer will be responsible for maintaining lab operations for product development engineering teams involved with optimization of engine performance, fuel economy, and emissions. The Engineer’s responsibilities include but are not limited to building engine/powertrain test schedules, diagnosing engine dynamometer test cell systems, coordinating repairs, data integrity review, and maintaining operational efficiency throughout the lab. The Test Support Engineer will manage projects including the design and buildup of fixtures to expand test capabilities, improve data integrity, and increase testing throughout.
