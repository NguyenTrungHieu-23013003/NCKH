Posted June 1, 2018 Position Overview Integration Partners has an exciting opportunity for anyone with interest in the telecommunication industry — an Entry-Level Support Engineer role! This is an excellent career opportunity for an enthusiastic and talented individual to join a team of outstanding professionals. This position offers tremendous potential for professional growth in a great working environment specializing in Data Networking. Responsibilities This Entry-Level Support Engineer will play a vital role in our Network Operations Center (NOC). The duties and responsibilities of this role will help train and prepare an individual for more advanced positions by developing relationships with Integration Partners customers and building strong teamwork skills alongside Integration Partners employees. The Entry-Level Support Engineer will be responsible for providing positive frontline communication during customer support calls, assisting with technical systems alerts, and working with senior NOC engineer on escalated issues. Required Skills and Expertise Bachelor’s degree in telecommunications or Bachelor’s degree in any concentration with a passion for computer networking A basic understanding of Networking is a plus Server skills (Linux, VMware, others) a plus Any technical certifications are a plus Ability to communicate effectively with managers, customers, and vendors Ability to manage technical workload Excellent communication and attention to detail skills Eagerness to provide outstanding customer service High energy and eager to learn
