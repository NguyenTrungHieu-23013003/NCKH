The primary function of the IT Purchasing Administrator is to manage the purchasing process for the IT department. This individual will facilitate and oversee the purchasing and payment procedures and create processes around these functions to improve efficiency. The IT Purchasing Administrator will be responsible for providing data and generating reports for Senior Management relating to IT expenses. This role requires maintaining relationships with members of all departments with an emphasis on open lines of communication with the Accounting Department. Additionally, this role will be responsible for managing the manual inventory of physical devices. Essential Functions and Responsibilities : Manage quotes, SOW’s, purchase orders and invoicing working closely with requesters, approvers, and the Accounting department.
