Job Summary: Assists the Director of Leasing in all aspects pertaining to the leasing and marketing of UI's commercial portfolio. Essential Duties and Responsibilities: _Leasing Duties_ Shows office space upon request Determines needs of each prospective and existing tenant and negotiates those requirements in new leases and renewals Assists with preparing site plans, calculating rent and drafting offers/proposals to be sent to prospective and existing tenants Processes new leases and amendments and certifies information in Yardi Management Software once it has been entered by accounting Negotiates leases within each building’s set leasing parameters Familiarity with UI’s existing and available properties, local trends in the market and general Real Estate principles Creates and maintains documentation such as tenant expiration lists, commission statements and waivers, space availability lists, renewal/option reports, lease proposals/amendments storage and banner agreements Assists in preparation/review of documentation such as estoppel letters and lease content when prepared by outside counsel and provides information to Director of Leasing, Asset Management, Construction and Property Management, when necessary _Administrative Duties_ Answers phone calls and emails daily regarding office space inquiries Directs information to all departments with respect to all lease transactions Responsible for being the contact person when the Director of Leasing is not available Maintains excellent communication with current and/or perspective clients via phone and email Follows up on all correspondence and returns phone calls promptly following the initial contact Coordinates updates to the Broker database and manages lease announcement emails with Marketing Coordinator Prints marketing materials for lease folders Promptly updates listings on _Costar_ and other listing platforms (Truss, 42Floors) Other Duties and Responsibilities: Develops and coordinates efficient and effective operating procedures Creates and maintains leasing files Prepares tenant and broker mailings Prepares and maintain bi-weekly reports for leasing, property management and construction meetings Provide new lease updates to property management and construction departments Assumes additional duties/projects as assigned and/or required
