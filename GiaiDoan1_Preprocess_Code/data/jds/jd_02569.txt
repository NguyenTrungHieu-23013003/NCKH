Job Description Support and implement the annual Business Continuity Management program. This includes performing business impact assessments (BIA), assisting in business continuity plan (BCP) development, and planning, conducting and evaluating BCP exercises. Act as a consultant to internal business stakeholders throughout the organization to lend business continuity subject matter expertise and support Manage large and complex data sets by applying data mining and analytic techniques Create and maintain process documentation for the department using approved process modeling techniques Design reports, dashboards, and infographics using data visualization tools Implement project management best practices to keep internal projects on track Maintain organizational hierarchy and supporting data from the business continuity program in the organizational planning tool Understand the potential impact of relevant laws, industry regulations and government code Championing BCM awareness throughout the enterprise and assigned business units
