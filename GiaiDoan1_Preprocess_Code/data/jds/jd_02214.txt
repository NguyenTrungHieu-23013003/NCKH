The Office Manager will be assigned to the Citywide Case Management Forensics Program (CWCMF) in partnership with the San Francisco Adult Probation Department (SFAPD). The overall goal of CWCMF and SFAPD’s collaboration is to treat high risk clients, promote safe and sustained re-entry into the community and reduce recidivism into the criminal justice system. Under the supervision of the Division Administrator and Community Assessment Services Center (CASC) Program Director, the Office Manager will work with the administrative, operational, clinical and non-clinical aspects of the CASC. Responsibilities will include facility operations, expense reimbursement and invoice review/submissions, PO set-up, database management, client file management, ordering office and medical supplies, filing, Information Technology (IT) help desk support and staff and faculty productivity, billing and contract compliance reporting. The job will involve the ability to lift 50 lbs. and climb stairs. DEPARTMENT OF PSYCHIATRY SFGH SFGH is dedicated to providing services that integrates medical, psychiatric, and addiction treatment. SFGH is the largest provider of acute psychiatric care and the only San Francisco provider of psychiatric emergency care.
