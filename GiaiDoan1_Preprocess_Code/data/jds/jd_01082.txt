Job Description The Office of Diversity & Inclusion is looking for an individual who will coordinate the City’s efforts to comply with and carry out its responsibilities under the American with Disabilities Act, as amended. The duties and responsibilities of the ADA Coordinator shall be to work with internal stakeholders and external consultants to develop a comprehensive plan to address issues related to Title II of the ADA, including managing departments through an in-depth evaluation of their internal policies and procedures, followed by an analysis of city facilities and properties, to ensure that individuals with disabilities participate fully in the City’s programs, services or activities. The ADA Coordinator will also be responsible for working with relevant leadership to develop comprehensive city-wide policies and procedures to address the requirements of the administration under Title II of the ADA. Planning, Coordination, and Implementation of ADA Compliance Projects Develop and implement a long-term plan to evaluate and edit programs and policies from various departments Manage department leadership and other relevant stakeholders through evaluation process Create an internal communications strategy for the ADA self-evaluation process Work with City departments, independent licensed architects, city contracted businesses and IT consultants to plan and coordinate projects to ensure access to City facilities and programs including building improvements and alternative technology implementation Collaborate and consult with the Mayor’s Executive Director for the Mayor’s Commission on People with Disabilities on issues relating to the community ADA Complaint Management Work with internal stakeholders to develop internal procedure for filing, responding to and investigating ADA related complaints Receive, and investigate ADA and relative complaints Internal Review Review City programs, policies, activities, services and facilities for ADA compliance Work with affected departments to develop and implement plans to bring identified violations into compliance Provide assistance with Reasonable Accommodation by City employees and constituents
