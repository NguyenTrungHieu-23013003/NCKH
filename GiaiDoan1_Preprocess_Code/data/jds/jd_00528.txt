As a Data Analyst I at Insight, you’ll process manufacturer rebate information and keep track of data using SAP. To do this, you’ll have to work across various departments within the company and with our external partners. Another of your objectives will be to identify areas in the process to improve and make more efficient. What you’ll do: Connect. Work across the company and interact with our teams in Bid Accounting, Price Protection, Purchasing, Product Management, Bid Desk and Sales. Make sure everyone is on the same page and executing under the same agreement. Review and analyze. Understand contract language in order to effectively manage to the terms and conditions of each program. Analyze new promotions and programs to assess their profitability. Manage and administer accurate special pricing programs. You’ll also be responsible for some quality control process and for pull various ad hoc reports. What we need: High school diploma and 2-4 years of related professional experience. Solid communication skills (verbal and written) and the ability to effectively present information to managers, clients and other teammates. Problem solving, organization and time management skills.
