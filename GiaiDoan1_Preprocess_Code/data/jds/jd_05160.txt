Job Description The Quality Coordinator role is an office based position reporting directly to the Quality and Performance Manager. The primary responsibilities of the position are to assist in supporting quality initiatives, performance improvement plans, quality event tracking/monitoring and quality data management and analysis. Identifying opportunities for quality improvement within our projects Review and report on performance trends of the quality management system Collect and manage client feedback data for evaluating Client satisfaction per project Control the Quality Assurance processes throughout all project life cycles Plan, establish, and direct all quality assurance programs, methods and procedures Assist in conducting Internal Audits Perform periodic quality reviews and targeted audits as determined by Quality & Performance Manager Assist the Project Managers in tracking and monitoring project KPIs Provide detailed analysis of project performance Collaborate with Quality & Performance Manager and project line management on quality initiatives and data needs Lead and track Quality Events and provide a summary for trend analysis Assist with project Operational Readiness Assessments Ensure Lesson Learned sessions are conducted for each project Prioritize and recommend quality improvements through data driven analysis Report all non-conformances or deficiencies discovered into company reporting system
