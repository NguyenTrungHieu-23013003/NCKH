PRIMARY FUNCTION : The Security Analyst is responsible for developing and implementing application and network security measures that will provide detection, prevention, containment and deterrence mechanisms to protect and maintain the integrity of applications throughout the organization. ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS: Reviews IT systems and business processes to identify information security risks and regulatory non-compliance, and guide remedy of issues noted. Assists with the creation, maintenance and auditing of user accounts. Documents, follows, and audits compliance with information security procedures and requirements. Designs and delivers training on Information Security. Maintains familiarity with HIPAA and other Information Security regulations Interacts with co-workers, visitors, and other staff consistent with the iSCORE values of Jefferson. OTHER FUNCTIONS AND COMPETENCIES: Facilitates audits and regulatory requests by gathering evidence, tracking progress, following up on outstanding items, and reporting Generates information security metrics by collecting and reporting on data Supports other information security operations as required EDUCATIONAL/TRAINING REQUIREMENTS : Bachelor’s degree in an information technology field required. _________________________________________________________________________ CERTIFICATES, LICENSES, AND REGISTRATION : At least one information security certification, such as a SSCP, GSEC, or professional certificate required. CISSP or CISA preferred _________________________________________________________________________ EXPERIENCE REQUIREMENTS : One year experience with network administration as well as systems administration required. 3+ years solid understanding of the Information Security & IT controls, Security penetration and vulnerability assessments. Experience with NIST and ISO frameworks. Solid knowledge of HIPAA, HITECH, and other IT security governing bodies.
