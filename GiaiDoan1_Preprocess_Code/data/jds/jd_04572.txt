Job Description The Sales Operations Analyst II works closely with Sales management and other key leadership in the Sales and IT organizations. This role reports to the Global Sales Operations team. The primary responsibility of this position is communicating and implementing corporate sales processes and best practices within Salesforce.com, Tableau, and other sales tools to enable the Sales team. The Sales Operations Analyst II is responsible for driving execution of the global and local sales strategies. The Sales Operations Analyst II will work with various Forcepoint stakeholder groups including Sales, Business Units, Executive Leadership, Sales Operations, Commissions, and Business Intelligence. This role requires previous demonstrated experience in some of the following: sales operations, Salesforce administration, pipeline and forecast management in Salesforce, administration of commissions and sales incentive programs, expertise in Excel and SQL, sales quota development and maintenance, territory assignments, and other leadership attributes.
