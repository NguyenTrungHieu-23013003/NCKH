This position will report to the Manager, Quality Management (QM) Field and have day to day contact with the QM Supervisor of the Locate & Mark Program and the QM Specialists performing the leak survey assessments. The Gas Quality Information Analyst is responsible to request/review information from the operating headquarters, compile documents to develop a quality assessment package, schedule field assessments for the QM Specialists, review completed assessments for accuracy, maintain the QM data base for assessment findings and generate reports. The QM Analyst will work with other QM Analysts and the QM Supervisor to produce monthly reports and analyze the results to identify trends, best practices and lessons learned. In addition, the QM Analyst will track and administer contracts for services related to the individual programs. This includes, working with the individual contractors to produce all the required information needed to write the service contracts, invoice tracking and cost run rates, timely change orders to insure services are not interrupted. Additional duties include, providing timely required written and verbal communications for various levels of operations.
