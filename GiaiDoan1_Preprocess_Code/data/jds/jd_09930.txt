The Senior Network Systems Architect is expected to provide technical leadership and support for our diverse LAN and WAN environment. The Senior Network Systems Architect is responsible for researching and recommending network design, operating procedures, produce monthly trending reports and standards to assure optimum network systems availability and performance. The Senior Network Systems Architect will be an integral member on key projects, including Datacenter design, footprint expansion, and key security management and other special assignments as determined by the Manager. The Senior Network Systems Architect will set the network design and architecture for the LAN and WAN environments, as well as provide backup support and assist implementing our technology initiatives.
