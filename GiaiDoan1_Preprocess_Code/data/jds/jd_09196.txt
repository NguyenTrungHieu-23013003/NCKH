Learn more at www.multicarejobs.org Position Summary: The Epic Security Analyst I role is an entry level position into the information security department. The Security Analyst has primary responsibility for administering user and provider records; and managing role- based security files in Epic. The Security Analyst is responsible for creating profiles and records, maintaining accessibility for qualified users, updating user functionality and resolving security issues for all security aspects of the Epic Ambulatory and Access/Professional Billing applications. Minimum Requirements: 2 years of information technology experience Work experience requirements may be offset through demonstrated knowledge via internship or other work at MHS Education/Licenses/Certifications: Bachelor's degree (BS, BA) in MIS, CS, Business, Healthcare or related field; or 5 years of information technology experience.
