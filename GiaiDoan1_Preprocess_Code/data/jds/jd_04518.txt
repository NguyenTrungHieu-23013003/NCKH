A Day in the Life of a Performance Improvement Analyst: This position works closely with the Performance Improvement Analytics Team Lead and the corporate and state quality teams to coordinate the development, implementation, documentation and evaluation of performance improvement initiatives. The Performance Improvement Analytics Team Lead evaluates and supports all performance improvement functions including but not limited to: QI projects, clinical programs and initiatives, HEDIS, CAHPS and National Committee for Quality Assurance (NCQA), URAC or general accreditation and regulatory efforts. This position is responsible for the evaluation and analysis of program design, financials, effectiveness, and impact on member/provider health, engagement, and satisfaction.
