The Corporate Investigator is responsible for identifying, analyzing, and implementing solutions to routine, but moderately complex, management problems with respect to the protection and safeguarding of personnel, information, and other assets in the area of investigations. The Corporate Investigator will be responsible for investigations in the areas of workplace violence, fraud, theft, and vandalism. This position reports directly to the Site Manager. In this role, you will: Identify, investigate and implement solutions to routine, but which may include moderate-complex and highly sensitive, incidents and issues, requiring established interviewing, researching, and analytical skills and techniques specific to corporate investigations Conduct investigations related to threats originating from external sources outside the company, and internal incidents and issues such as theft of client assets or intellectual property, vandalism, and workplace violence related matters Provide incident, investigative case and/or project management services based on needs that arise (cross-functional initiatives, training and awareness efforts) Develop, track, and maintain reporting of statistical and written analysis of user threats, incidents and investigations for management review Establish and maintain professional relationships with counterparts in related industries/disciplines to obtain a common understanding of like problems and known solutions Maintain direct communication and liaison with corporate and outside counsel members, law enforcement agencies, and private consultants, as appropriate Support the day-to-day administration of the general safety and security awareness program, and related activities Document and publish standardized investigations guidelines.
