Under general supervision, reviews medical records to ensure that records are complete and all appropriate documentation is present. Coordinates and reconciles the retrieval of discharged medical records from inpatient care and surgical units on a daily basis according to established procedures. Assembles patient charts in proper sequence by identification of physician documentation in accordance with hospital guidelines, and reviews for completion at summary level. Analyzes, sequences and prioritizes assignment of deficient records; forwards to designated physician for completion and records deficiencies in Meditech. In compliance with HIPAA policy, retrieves and tracks requests for medical records for patient care and audits as requested by hospital departments and personnel. Qualifications: Minimum Education (preferred)- high school diploma or equivalent. Minimum Work Experience (preferred)- Two (2) years experience in a clerical or comparable position.
