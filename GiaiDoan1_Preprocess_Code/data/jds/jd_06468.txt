The Application Data Coordinator serves as the primary point of contact for the accurate and effective processing of prospective student data, undergraduate admission application records and information. This individual serves as the primary data expert responsible for the coordination and daily management of the technical information systems of the Office of Admission. Job Responsibilities Coordinates the accurate processing of undergraduate admission application records Plans and coordinates the effective integration of multiple interfaces between the student information systems in Enrollment management including but not limited to: Radius, Ellucian Colleague, and any other CRM systems of record Coordinates the planning, design, integration, implementation, and maintenance of integrations between student enrollment systems Executes imports and exports between systems from various external software services and interfaces with the CRM/SIS. Develop and maintain reports from all information systems for the division and the campus Manages the needs and use of both Radius and Colleague systems in maintaining accurate student records as related to the admissions process including data entry and updating of student records and information in the system Creates reports, manages dashboard reporting, and carries out ad hoc reporting Monitors and ensures data integrity Serves as central point of contact for system integration issues, change requests and system alterations. Serves as back-up to all admission operations functions including student list imports, transcript processing, mail processing, etc. Other duties as assigned Education Minimum of an Associates Degree highly preferred Required Skills, Knowledge and Abilities Outstanding attention to detail Demonstrated experience with responsibility for quality of data Excellent organization, communication and writing skills Proven ability to communicate to both technical and business audiences Proficient in the use of computer based programs including Microsoft Office, admissions management data programs and other technologies Strong interpersonal skills with ability to work as a team member and independently Delaware Valley University reserves the right to modify and adjust job responsibilities and qualifications _Delaware Valley University is an equal opportunity employer with a commitment to a diverse workforce._
