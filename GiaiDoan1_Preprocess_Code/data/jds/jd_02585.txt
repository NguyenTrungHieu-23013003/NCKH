A Business Analyst owns the fundamentals of all areas of the IT project lifecycle. The Analyst is involved in pre-project planning, definition, and design, all the way through implementation. Align the goals of business partners with the project’s technology solutions to ensure continuity to objectives and plan for the desired end state Elicit and clarify, secure approval, and complete submission of customer requirements Develop well-defined problem statements to readily address the project end- goals and achieve consensus to accomplish business goals Lead communication and collaboration among multidisciplinary project teams Identify and define project targets and criteria for customer satisfaction Assist in activities related to quality assurance testing and risk avoidance Create and use quantifiable data to measure against quality objectives Identify present-state process flow charts and supporting diagrams, use cases, quantifiable measures, and statistical analysis data Capture relevant changes which impacted the project Create and distribute project communication including deployment notices and release notes Research and analyze markets and vendor offerings and conduct benchmarking and competitive analysis Requirements Bachelor of Science and at least 5 years of similar experience in IT projects Comprehensive understanding of IT processes, systems/methods and development tools Possess exceptional verbal and written communication skills Use resources available within the organization to overcome program obstacles Must demonstrate fundamental expectations of a professional such as collaboration, resourcefulness, team engagement, ethical character, and emotional intelligence Work at a fast pace with a diverse range of projects
