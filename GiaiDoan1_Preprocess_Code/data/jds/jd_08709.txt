Who We Are We believe that automation is necessary to scale Twitter to our millions of users across the world, but human curation allows Twitter to provide context and tell stories. The curation teams at Twitter already touch many product surface areas including Trends, Search, and Moments. As we continue to increase the responsibilities of the curation teams, we also need to grow the engineering team that develops the tools they use 24 hours a day. Who You Are You are a web developer with experience using a modern JavaScript framework like React, Vue, or Angular. You are a someone one who enjoys tight feedback loops with internal users and is always thinking of ways to improve the code and products they build. You are someone that thrives in a fast paced, iterative environment. You are someone who is unafraid to ask questions when working with new people and teams. You are someone that can communicate technical details to non-technical co-workers. Qualifications Familiarity with CS basics: algorithms, design and coding Experience with web technologies including JavaScript, CSS, and HTML Experience with software engineering best practices (e.g. unit testing, code reviews, design documentation) B.S. or higher in Computer Science or equivalent work experience. Bonus Points Experience with full stack web development including APIs and databases. Familiarity with the Node and NPM ecosystems. Data-informed product development experience such as analytics and A/B testing.
