Overview Fairview Health Services is looking for a Network Architect position in our Fairview corporate location in Minneapolis, MN. This is a full-time position working day hours, Monday thru Friday. The Network Architect is responsible for supporting the development of the infrastructure architecture enabling the organization to meet business objectives. The position supports designing technology solutions which will support the enterprise network of the organization.Analyzes system requirements and ensures that systems will be secure and effectively integrated with current network.Ensures that all systems are working at optimal levels and offers support to Network Engineering department regarding new technologies and system requirements. Has thorough knowledge of network, infrastructure architecture, and Application Delivery. Responsibilities/Job Description Architect and create roadmaps for all Network related technology, participates in conversations with vendors and internal business customers on long-term business goals and objectives, support fiscally reasonable designs and architecture, manages Life Cycle Management (LCM) for Network technologies, participates in project management efforts including Intake, Preliminary Design, Gather Requirements, Security/Architecture Review Board (SARB) and project oversight. Secondarily this position will support interactions for all IS initiatives.
