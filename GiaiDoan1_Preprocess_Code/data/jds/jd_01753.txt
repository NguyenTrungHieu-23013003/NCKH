SUMMARY The Energy Authority is looking for a highly motivated individual interested in gaining broad exposure to commodity trading and risk management across North American Power and Gas markets. The multi-month program on our Bellevue trade-floor provides transactional and analytical support to traders, schedulers, and portfolio managers. Successful interns will also have an opportunity to participate in a variety of client engagement and reporting activities. Ideal candidates should thrive in fast-paced collaborative environments and possess excellent communication, problem solving, and time management skills. ESSENTIAL DUTIES & RESPONSIBILIES include the following. Other duties may be assigned. Troubleshoot daily emails/issues experienced by real-time traders and schedulers Ensure trades across a variety of terms and products are accurately recorded in the booking system Analyze deal flow and work with settlement teams to assess liquidated damages Monitor various portfolio strategies and performance metrics, provide P&L; reports to managers and directors Assist with software testing/implementation, as required Identify and implement process improvements; updates and refines protocol documentation catalog Performs ad-hoc analysis as assigned Attend monthly joint-client sessions, tour at least one Northwest power generation facility QUALIFICATIONS EDUCATION and/or EXPERIENCE Working towards a bachelor's or master’s degree, preferably in accounting, business, engineering, finance, economics, math, statistics or related discipline from a four-year college or university. VALUES REQUIRED Customer Focus Integrity Teamwork Ownership Excellence COMPETENCIES REQUIRED In addition to demonstrating TEA’s values the ideal candidate will possess the following competencies: Organization & Planning Adaptability Communication Commitment to Continuous Learning Innovation Business Acumen
