Banner Engineering is a global leader in process and industrial automation, helping customers increase efficiency, reduce costs, ensure quality, monitor and control processes and safeguard employees. Position Summary The Applications Engineer provides both technical product support and customer service as it relates to Sales Operations. Responsibilities and Duties Provides technical consultation and service to Banner Area Sales Managers, Field Sales Representatives, distributors and customers in product sales and applications, including pricing, stock levels and order status. Collaborates with Engineering staff to develop technical modifications to existing products in order to meet unique application requirements; conducts in house tests to solve real-life customer applications. Assists in conducting or conducts product training for internal and/or external audiences. Creates customized reports based on internal sales data to promote sales growth. Supports the sales region with tasks such as order entry, requests for expedited shipping, samples and Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) requests. Utilizes the Customer Relationship Management (CRM) software to input and maintain complete, accurate and timely entries of customer information and sales opportunities for all cases worked. Manages multiple priorities including incoming phone calls, computer tasks, email and responding to sales team member questions or requests. Provides timely follow up as needed to Banner Area Sales Managers, Field Sales Representatives, distributors and customers.
