The Laboratory Clerk maintains documents, files reports and performs data entry. ESSENTIAL JOB FUNCTIONS: Maintains and files test reports Maintains and updates various laboratory information log books Prepares monthly summary of test statistics report Receives and scans lab specimens Prepares and packages send- out lab specimens Monitors and tracks pending lab reports Performs other job duties as required by manager/supervisor. QUALIFICATIONS: High school certificate or equivalent Proficient in using computers Strong attention to detail Prior clerical experience is a plus LANGUAGE: Must be able to read, write and speak English fluently The ability to speak a second language is a plus STATUS: This is an FLSA Non-exempt position.
