Overview / Responsibilities Wood Environment & Infrastructure Solutions’ Phoenix office has an immediate opening for a Facility Condition Assessor with detailed knowledge and experience in using BUILDER relative to mechanical, electrical, plumbing, fire protection, interior, exterior, and/or roofing systems for military facilities.
