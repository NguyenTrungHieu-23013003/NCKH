This position is responsible for a variety of activities in support of the Enterprise Information Technology (EIT) Recovery and Resolution Planning (RRP) team. The EIT RRP team is responsible for leading, directing, and ensuring completion of the firm-wide RRP technology plans and projects as required to support related regulatory guidance. Governance and Strategy – Assist in managing cycles of annual Management Information Systems (MIS) narrative submissions in support of Recovery & Resolution Planning. Manage strategic EIT RRP plans to stay current with EIT strategy, RRP architecture, capability validation, and future capability enablement. Partner closely with all Risk Management Lines of Defense, Recovery & Resolution Planning Office (RRPO), Operational Sustainment, and Capability Management team. Primary responsibilities include: Track and manage annual cycles for the written submission of RRP MIS content which includes tracking of response/feedback cycles in Wdesk toolset. Ensure continued progress toward deliverables and target dates from RRPO and coordinate with governance steering committee on internal approval cycle. Perform documentation of capabilities in place with an understanding of how the current state business environment satisfies regulatory expectations. Assist with creation or editing of RRP MIS narrative content and ensure alignment with messaging from partner capability areas, EIT organization, Risk Management Lines of Defense, RRPO groups. Provide analysis and support for work efforts as required, draft RRP materials to communicate with partner groups. Support reviews and challenges from external regulators, Risk Management Lines of Defense and RRPO groups.
