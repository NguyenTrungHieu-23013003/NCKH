Job Description As a team, we value correctness, efficiency, and safety. We measure and monitor everything, and have a culture of continuous reflection and improvement. We aim to eliminate friction in production and believe that no project should be delayed due to lack of reliable infrastructure. We believe that a well designed production environment can be beautiful. We're looking for a network engineer who shares our values, particularly those who have experience building, monitoring, and maintaining a highly-available backbone interconnecting multiple datacenters. You will: Help architect, design, and implement improvements to our global network Interface with the security team to design and deliver security-related enhancements Perform network based security risk assessments Configure and administer network monitoring tools Configure and administer routers and switches in a multi-vendor environment Provision and deploy network hardware in PoPs and data centers Troubleshoot network failures and performance issues Participate in an on-call rotation covering all of the above Mentor and train other network engineers on the team Occasionally be required to travel domestically and internationally
