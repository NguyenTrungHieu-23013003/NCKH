Job Description The MicroStrategy Developer will be a critical team member for the delivery of key customer commitments surrounding Alkami’s data warehouse project. This position requires you to be very logical and have strong MicroStrategy design and visualization skills. You will work individually and as a member of a development team to design, develop and maintain customized dashboards, visualizations and detailed reports. You will be involved in all aspects of the development and administration process, including: design, development, system testing and all other aspects of the software development process as well as configuration and management of the Microstrategy iServer and Web environment. In addition, you will: Develop reports that support the Alkami data warehouse initiative. Partner with the data modeler to ensure that the data structures and content support the reporting and analytic requirements of the organization. Partner with the Alkami product management to ensure that the MicroStrategy environment supports the data reporting requirements. Provide requirements that support the business semantic access layer using MicroStrategy. Responsibilites: Gather Analytic Reporting Requirements and Facilitate End User Testing MicroStrategy Development MicroStrategy Training for end users and internal support personnel MicroStrategy Administration and Configuration Operational support as required which is outside of the normal support team capabilities Incorporation of advanced and emerging MicroStrategy features
