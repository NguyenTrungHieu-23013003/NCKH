Facebook is seeking a Program Analyst to join our Global Data Center Connectivity (GDCC) Strategy and Planning team. Our data centers are the foundation upon which our rapidly growing infrastructure efficiently operates, and our innovative services are delivered. The Program Analyst will be a leader in orchestrating GDCC’s long range planning and capital management using today’s process while working within the team to improve and build robust process as the business scales. Providing key leadership to how we interface with corporate governance, pre-construction teams, project controls teams, and build a coherent long range capital plan. The Program Analyst will drive success with a knowledge of capital planning, stewardship, financial systems, and knowledge of how they fit with technical operations. The role is expected to provide key support of our initiatives to scale operations and to develop robust processes and tools enabling Facebook’s expansion. This position is full-time and based in our main office in Menlo Park, CA.
