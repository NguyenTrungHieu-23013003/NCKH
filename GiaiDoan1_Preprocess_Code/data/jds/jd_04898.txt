A Day in the Life of a Sr. BI & Analytics Developer: This is a senior role for experienced business intelligence and analytics developers, and/or data scientists. The role will be expert at eliciting requirements from business subject matter experts, leading discussions and facilitating sessions to get deep understanding of how the data and business intelligence can support their departments. This position is responsible for developing healthcare subject matter expertise as it relates to building the data structures supporting BI reporting and analytics. The role will design and build reports, dashboards, and perform advanced analytics as needed. The role will be expert at understanding data structures, the differences between Inmon and Kimball methodologies, as well as de-normalization techniques. The senior developer will mentor and assist junior staff in developing reports, dashboards, and analytics, and will guide their learning. This position requires a level of interest and passion for Big Data processing, warehousing, reporting, and analytics that is unparalleled!
