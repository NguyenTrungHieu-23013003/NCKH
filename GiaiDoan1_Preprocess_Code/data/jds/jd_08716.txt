Full Stack Software Developer responsibilities encompass the complete product lifecycle from innovative ideas to end-user deployment and back again. We believe in the value of end-to-end involvement of technology people in our sales and marketing process, and will aim to have all developers regularly meeting and interacting with end users and influencers to present material as a topic specialist and bring back market-focused insights for continuous improvement. Developers will also work closely with our support and QA team to triage customer tickets and squash bugs. Above all else, we are looking for good engineers. In addition to being proficient at translating problem statements into code, a successful candidate will possess the following traits: Devoted to seeing projects all the way through from conception, design, and implementation to release, maintenance, and support. Passionate about your craft and committed to doing things "the right way", always optimizing for maintainability and seeking process improvements. Thrives when working autonomously, setting reasonable goals for yourself and updating the rest of the team on progress or roadblocks you may face. Eager to learn about new technologies and dive into the latest open source tools. Understands that the code isn't done when the core logic has been implemented and always includes proper error handling, logging, etc. for when the software inevitably doesn't act quite how you thought it would. Mastery of one or more of the following will earn you serious bonus points: Python JavaScript Cloud management, deployment, and distributed systems architecture with Amazon Web Services Streaming media and real-time video processing Speech recognition and machine learning User interface design
