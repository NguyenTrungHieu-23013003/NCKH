The Data Architect is responsible for designing and implementing the data architecture for Lineage’s next generation supply chain operating platform. Specifically, this includes being responsible for developing normalized, dimensional, and hybrid data models to support reporting, analytics, and APIs used by other internal and external applications. The Data Architect will serve as the primary data modeler and provide oversight of the following areas: Data Ingestion- moving source system data to a unified data lake Data Modeling - determining field-level data usage Data Wrangling - identifying queries/algorithms to efficiently transform data from source system formats to a unified RDBMS schema Data Quality - ensuring complete and uninterrupted data mappings from source to canonical form At Lineage Logistics, IT’s aim is not to build the largest teams but to build a small, highly skilled, team that focuses specifically on having the capability, knowledge and skills focused in areas that define our core differentiators and allow us to leverage cloud, best-of-breed technologies and manage/leverage external teams of contractors for capacity. This means that in addition to being a great architect, you need to be able to review and collaborate with partners to ensure that we get the same quality from them too. As a company, Lineage Logistics leverages state-of-the-art technology to meet our customer’s needs, assist in our international growth, and create an infrastructure that enables Lineage to lead the industry. As the ideal candidate for this position, you will exhibit the technical expertise, business acumen and flexibility required to successfully work with all levels of Lineage’s organization.
