The Learning Specialist (LS) possesses a passion for technology and supports the growth of each of the store team members by teaching them about our product line-up, customer service skills, and resources that will prepare them to offer a world-class customer experience. The LS reports directly to the Store Manager and is the ultimate subject matter expert when it comes to role, product, demo, and sales training for all team members in the store. The LS also partners with the HQ Readiness Team to help drive company and retail store priorities to ensure strategic objectives are met within the store.
