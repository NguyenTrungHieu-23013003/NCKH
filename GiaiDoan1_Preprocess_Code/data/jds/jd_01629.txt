The BA works with the Production staff and Director/AD in executing the daily on-air graphic presentation of the show. BA’s will also work on high-end editing montages and b-rolls. Responsibilities Manages the workflow and direction for the graphics on each broadcast. The leader of the team consisting of the graphics operator and researcher that coordinate game statistics and accurate player and team graphics to support the broadcast.
