As a Regional Operations Assistant, you support the District Managers (DMs) in the region with the daily operations of their districts. You serve as a liaison between the DMs and the regional office, stores and home office, maintaining confidentiality. You also deliver world-class customer service to internal and external customers. Essential Functions • Provide administrative and operational support to the DMs, including booking travel and submitting expense reports. • Generate, maintain and collect standard and ad hoc reports for the DMs. • Serve as a resource for DMs and Store Managers on technical and policy issues related to travel, reporting, job posting, and other systems. • Analyze data and prepare presentations for the DMs to communicate essential information. • Make process recommendations and find efficiencies to ensure timely and successful execution. • Support the DMs in the completion of Home Office requests. • Serve as an administrative resource to stores on technical and policy issues related to business development. • Assist with special projects, as needed. • Provide the highest quality support to internal and external customers. • Resolve to the customer’s satisfaction any external and internal customer requests that are escalated. • Maintain accurate and current talent maps for their districts. • Create and manage requisitions and assist in screening resumes for open jobs. • Prepare materials and provide logistical support for district-wide meetings. Qualifications • Strong organizational and written/verbal communication skills. • Demonstrated ability to handle multiple projects and activities simultaneously. • Excellent computer skills (Excel, Word, Outlook, and PowerPoint). • Availability for early mornings, evenings, weekends and holidays to align with store needs. • Minimal travel to district meetings. • At least two years of experience providing administrative support.
